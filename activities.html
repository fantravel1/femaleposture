The Activity Principle

No single posture is perfect if held for hours. The healthiest approach involves:

  • Variety: Alternate between sitting, standing, and moving
  • Movement: Take breaks every 30-60 minutes
  • Awareness: Notice when you've drifted into poor positions
  • Optimization: Set up each environment to support good posture

The guides in this section help you optimize each activity while remembering that movement between activities is just as important.
