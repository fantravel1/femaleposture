# How Should I Stand With Good Posture?

**Quick Answer:** Stand with feet hip-width apart, weight evenly distributed, knees soft (not locked), pelvis neutral, spine tall, shoulders relaxed down and back, and head balanced with ears over shoulders.

## Ideal Standing Alignment

**From ground up:**
- Feet hip-width apart
- Weight even on both feet
- Knees soft, not locked
- Pelvis neutral (not tilted)
- Natural spine curves
- Shoulders down and back
- Head balanced, chin level

## The Wall Test

**Check yourself:**
1. Stand with heels 2 inches from wall
2. Touch wall with buttocks, shoulder blades, head
3. Small gap at neck and lower back
4. This is your alignment target

## Common Standing Mistakes

**Avoid:**
- Locked knees
- Hips pushed forward
- Weight on one leg only
- Shoulders up by ears
- Head forward

## Quick Posture Reset

When you notice slouching:
1. Squeeze shoulder blades
2. Lift crown of head
3. Tuck chin slightly
4. Engage core gently
5. Relax and maintain
