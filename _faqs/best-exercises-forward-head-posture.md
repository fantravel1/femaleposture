# What Are the Best Exercises for Forward Head Posture?

**Quick Answer:** The top exercises are chin tucks (to retrain position), upper back strengthening (to support head), and chest stretches (to allow shoulders back). Consistency with these three areas creates lasting change.

## Top Exercises

### 1. Chin Tucks (Most Important)
**How:** Pull chin straight back, making "double chin"
**Hold:** 5-10 seconds
**Reps:** 10-15 times
**Frequency:** Multiple times daily

### 2. Wall Angels
**How:** Back against wall, arms up like goalpost, slide up and down
**Reps:** 10-15
**Frequency:** Daily

### 3. Chest Doorway Stretch
**How:** Forearm on doorframe, lean through gently
**Hold:** 30-60 seconds each side
**Frequency:** 2-3 times daily

### 4. Prone Y-T-W Raises
**How:** Lie face down, lift arms in Y, T, and W positions
**Reps:** 10 each position
**Frequency:** Daily

### 5. Band Pull-Aparts
**How:** Hold band, pull apart to chest level
**Reps:** 15-20
**Frequency:** Daily

## Sample Routine

**Morning (2-3 min):**
- 10 chin tucks
- Chest stretches each side

**Throughout day:**
- Chin tucks at desk
- Posture checks hourly

**Evening (5-10 min):**
- Full exercise set
- Extended stretching
