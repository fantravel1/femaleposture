# Can Good Posture Reduce Anxiety?

**Quick Answer:** Yes, research shows upright, open postures can reduce feelings of anxiety and stress. Slouched, closed positions are associated with increased negative feelings and stress responses.

## The Body-Mind Connection

**How posture affects anxiety:**
- Body sends signals to brain
- Open postures = safety signals
- Closed postures = threat signals
- Breathing affected by position
- Hormonal responses influenced

## Anxiety-Related Posture Patterns

**When anxious, people often:**
- Hunch shoulders up
- Cross arms protectively
- Make themselves small
- Shallow chest breathing
- Look down

## Postures That Reduce Anxiety

**Try these:**
- Stand or sit tall
- Open your chest
- Drop shoulders away from ears
- Take up appropriate space
- Deep belly breathing

## Quick Reset for Anxious Moments

1. Stand up if possible
2. Roll shoulders back
3. Open your chest
4. Take three deep breaths
5. Relax and maintain

## Important Note

Posture can help manage anxiety feelings but isn't a replacement for professional help when anxiety is severe or persistent. It's one tool in a comprehensive approach.
