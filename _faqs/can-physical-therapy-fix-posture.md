# Can Physical Therapy Fix My Posture?

**Quick Answer:** Physical therapy is one of the most effective approaches for posture improvement. PTs assess your specific issues, provide targeted exercises, manual therapy, and education for lasting change.

## What Physical Therapists Do

**Their approach:**
- Comprehensive assessment
- Identify specific imbalances
- Targeted exercise prescription
- Manual therapy techniques
- Movement retraining
- Ergonomic education

## Why PT Is Effective

**Advantages:**
- Personalized to your issues
- Addresses root causes
- Teaches you to self-manage
- Progressive programming
- Evidence-based methods

## What to Expect

**Typical treatment:**
- Initial evaluation (45-60 min)
- Regular sessions (weekly or biweekly)
- Home exercise program
- Progress monitoring
- Discharge when goals met

## Your Participation

**For best results:**
- Do home exercises consistently
- Apply what you learn daily
- Communicate honestly
- Be patient with progress
- Maintain after discharge

## Finding the Right PT

**Look for:**
- Orthopedic or musculoskeletal specialist
- Interest in postural issues
- Exercise-focused approach
- Good communication
- Empowers you for independence

Physical therapy provides tools and knowledge for lifelong posture health.
