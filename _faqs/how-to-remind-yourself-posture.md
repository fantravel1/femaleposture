# How Can I Remember to Fix My Posture Throughout the Day?

**Quick Answer:** Use phone alarms, posture apps, environmental triggers (like every doorway), habit stacking (check posture every time you check email), and sticky notes until good posture becomes automatic.

## Reminder Strategies

**Technology:**
- Phone alarm every hour
- Posture reminder apps
- Smartwatch vibration alerts
- Calendar pop-ups
- Computer screen reminders

**Environmental cues:**
- Sticky notes on monitor
- Trigger: every doorway
- Red dot on phone
- Mirror placement
- Desktop widget

## Habit Stacking

**Link to existing habits:**
- Check posture when checking phone
- Reset with every email
- Posture check at red lights
- After bathroom breaks
- When drinking water

## Trigger-Based Reminders

**Every time you:**
- Get a text
- Open an app
- Walk through doorway
- Sit down
- Stand up

## Making It Automatic

**Progression:**
- Week 1-2: Many reminders
- Week 3-4: Reduce frequency
- Month 2+: Becoming habit
- Month 3+: More automatic
- Occasional refreshers after

## Best Practices

- Start with frequent reminders
- Reduce as awareness builds
- Don't rely on one method
- Make it easy and accessible
