# What Is Text Thumb (Texting Thumb)?

**Quick Answer:** Text thumb is repetitive strain injury of the thumb from excessive smartphone use. The repeated tapping, scrolling, and gripping motions strain the thumb tendons and joints, causing pain and stiffness.

## Symptoms

**What you may experience:**
- Thumb pain or aching
- Stiffness in thumb
- Clicking or catching
- Weakness in grip
- Pain radiating to wrist

## Causes

**Why it develops:**
- Repetitive texting motions
- Scrolling for long periods
- Gripping phone tightly
- Thumb-heavy phone use
- Poor phone ergonomics

## Prevention

**Reduce risk:**
- Use voice-to-text
- Take breaks from phone
- Alternate hands and fingers
- Use phone holder instead of holding
- Stretch thumbs regularly

## Treatment

**If you have symptoms:**
- Rest from texting
- Ice for inflammation
- Thumb stretches
- Splinting if severe
- Modify phone habits

## Stretches for Thumbs

- Gentle thumb circles
- Thumb extension stretches
- Massage thumb base
- Finger spreads
- Wrist circles

Reduce phone time and modify usage before symptoms become chronic.
