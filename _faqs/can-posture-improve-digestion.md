# Can Better Posture Improve Digestion?

**Quick Answer:** Yes, good posture can improve digestion. Sitting and standing upright gives your digestive organs space to function properly, potentially reducing acid reflux, bloating, and improving food movement through your system.

## How Posture Helps Digestion

**Mechanisms:**
- Less compression on stomach
- Proper organ positioning
- Better blood flow to digestive tract
- Diaphragm function supports gut
- Reduced abdominal pressure

## Digestion Tips Related to Posture

**Best practices:**
- Sit upright while eating
- Don't hunch over food
- Walk after meals
- Avoid lying down after eating
- Wear loose clothing at meals

## Posture Problems That Hurt Digestion

**Harmful positions:**
- Slumped on couch after eating
- Hunched over while eating
- Lying down too soon after meals
- Prolonged sitting compression

## Signs Posture Affects Your Digestion

**Indicators:**
- Bloating worse after sitting
- Reflux when slumped
- Feel better after walking
- Discomfort eases with movement
- Symptoms tied to position

## Simple Improvements

- Eat at a table, sitting upright
- Take a 10-15 minute walk after meals
- Wait 2-3 hours before lying down
- Practice upright sitting throughout day
- Stand periodically during work
