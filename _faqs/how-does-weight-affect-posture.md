# How Does Body Weight Affect Posture?

**Quick Answer:** Excess body weight affects posture, especially when carried in the midsection. It pulls the pelvis forward, increases spinal curves, and strains muscles and joints. Weight loss often improves posture.

## How Weight Affects Posture

**Mechanisms:**
- Belly weight pulls pelvis forward
- Increased lumbar curve
- Greater spinal compression
- Muscle fatigue from extra load
- Changed center of gravity

## Where Weight Is Carried Matters

**Location impact:**
- Belly: Most impact on spine
- Hips/thighs: Less spinal stress
- Upper body: Affects upper back
- Even distribution: Least problematic

## Weight-Related Posture Problems

**Common issues:**
- Increased lordosis
- Lower back pain
- Hip pain
- Knee stress
- Reduced mobility

## What Helps

**Improvement strategies:**
- Gradual weight loss if indicated
- Core strengthening
- Low-impact exercise
- Good posture habits
- Supportive footwear
