# Quick Posture Fixes FAQ

> **Need Help Now?** These quick fixes can provide immediate improvement. Use them throughout your day for better posture.

---

## Instant Fixes

### How do I quickly correct my posture?

**30-second reset:**
1. Stand or sit tall
2. Imagine string pulling crown of head up
3. Chin tuck gently
4. Roll shoulders back and down
5. Engage core lightly
6. Reset done

### What's the fastest way to look more confident?

- Stand at full height
- Shoulders back (not forced)
- Head up
- Weight evenly distributed
- Takes 5 seconds

### How do I stop slouching right now?

- Notice you're slouching
- Lift chest
- Drop shoulders
- Tuck chin slightly
- Return to activity with awareness

## At Your Desk

### Quick desk posture fix?

1. Slide hips to back of chair
2. Feet flat on floor
3. Lift screen if too low
4. Pull keyboard closer
5. Sit tall

## Standing

### How do I stand with better posture?

- Weight on both feet
- Knees soft, not locked
- Pelvis neutral
- Core engaged
- Shoulders relaxed back
- Head over shoulders

### Quick fix for standing fatigue?

- Shift weight
- Small steps in place
- Rise on toes briefly
- Engage then relax muscles

---

## Driving

### Posture fix for driving?

- Scoot seat forward enough
- Back against seat
- Head against headrest
- Shoulders relaxed
- Mirrors adjusted

---

## Sitting on Couch

### How do I not slouch on the couch?

- Sit back with support
- Pillow for lumbar
- Don't lie sideways
- Feet up with back supported

---

## The Truth

### Do quick fixes work long-term?

**They help, but:**
- Need to repeat frequently
- Don't replace exercises
- Build awareness
- Part of bigger picture
