# Can a Desk Job Ruin Your Posture?

**Quick Answer:** Desk jobs are one of the leading causes of poor posture due to prolonged sitting, screen positioning, and static positions. However, good ergonomics and movement habits can prevent postural damage.

## Why Desk Jobs Hurt Posture

**The setup:**
- Hours of sitting daily
- Screens often too low
- Reaching for keyboard/mouse
- Static positions for hours

**The effects:**
- Hip flexors shorten
- Back muscles weaken
- Shoulders round forward
- Head moves forward
- Core deconditions

## Common Desk Posture Problems

- Forward head posture
- Rounded shoulders
- Tight hip flexors
- Weak glutes
- Lower back pain
- Neck tension

## Preventing Damage

**Ergonomic setup:**
- Screen at eye level
- Chair supports lower back
- Feet flat on floor
- Arms at 90 degrees

**Movement:**
- Stand every 30 minutes
- Walk meetings when possible
- Desk exercises
- Lunchtime movement

**Exercises:**
- Daily posture routine
- Upper back strengthening
- Hip flexor stretching
- Core work
