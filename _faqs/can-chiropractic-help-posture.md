# Can Chiropractic Care Help My Posture?

**Quick Answer:** Chiropractic care can help improve posture through spinal adjustments and rehabilitation exercises, but lasting change requires your active participation with exercises and habit changes.

## What Chiropractors Can Do

**Their role:**
- Assess postural imbalances
- Spinal and joint adjustments
- Prescribe corrective exercises
- Address movement restrictions
- Monitor progress over time

## What They Can't Do Alone

**Limitations:**
- Make lasting change without your effort
- Fix posture in one visit
- Overcome poor daily habits
- Replace exercise and awareness

## What to Expect

**Typical approach:**
- Initial assessment
- Series of adjustments
- Exercise prescription
- Ergonomic recommendations
- Regular follow-up

## Your Critical Role

**For lasting results:**
- Do prescribed exercises
- Apply ergonomic changes
- Practice posture awareness
- Move regularly
- Stay consistent

Chiropractic care is most effective as part of a comprehensive posture improvement plan.
