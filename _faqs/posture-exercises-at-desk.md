# What Posture Exercises Can I Do at My Desk?

**Quick Answer:** Key desk exercises include chin tucks, shoulder blade squeezes, doorway chest stretches, seated cat-cow, and neck stretches. Do them throughout the day to counteract sitting.

## Top Desk Exercises

**Chin tucks:**
- Gently pull chin back
- Create double chin
- Hold 5 seconds
- Repeat 10 times

**Shoulder blade squeezes:**
- Squeeze shoulder blades together
- Hold 5 seconds
- Release
- Repeat 10 times

## Stretches at Your Desk

**Chest stretch:**
- Clasp hands behind back
- Pull shoulders back
- Open chest
- Hold 30 seconds

**Neck stretches:**
- Tilt ear to shoulder
- Hold 30 seconds each side
- Gentle, no forcing

## Movement Breaks

**Every hour:**
- Stand up
- Walk for 2-3 minutes
- Do a few exercises
- Reset posture
- Return refreshed

## Making It Habit

- Set timer reminders
- Link to existing habits
- Keep instructions visible
- Start with just one exercise
- Build gradually
