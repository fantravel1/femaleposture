# Can Yoga Fix Bad Posture?

**Quick Answer:** Yes, yoga is excellent for posture. It builds strength, flexibility, and body awareness simultaneously while teaching proper alignmentâ€”all essential for better posture.

## How Yoga Helps Posture

**Flexibility:**
- Stretches tight chest muscles
- Opens hip flexors
- Lengthens spine
- Releases shoulder tension

**Strength:**
- Builds core stability
- Strengthens back muscles
- Engages postural muscles
- Balances muscle groups

**Awareness:**
- Teaches body alignment
- Builds proprioception
- Creates mindfulness
- Transfers to daily life

## Best Yoga Poses for Posture

**For rounded shoulders:**
- Cobra/Sphinx
- Cow face arms
- Puppy pose

**For tight hips:**
- Low lunge
- Pigeon pose
- Warrior I

**For core strength:**
- Plank
- Boat pose
- Cat-cow

**For spine mobility:**
- Cat-cow
- Thread the needle
- Seated twists

## How Often

**For posture benefits:**
- 2-3 times per week minimum
- 20-60 minutes per session
- Consistency matters most
- Even 10 minutes helps

## Best Yoga Styles for Posture

- Hatha (alignment focus)
- Iyengar (precise alignment)
- Yoga for posture classes
- Gentle/restorative
