# Can Posture Affect Confidence?

**Quick Answer:** Yes, research shows upright posture increases feelings of confidence, power, and self-esteem. Expansive positions boost confidence, while closed, slouched positions increase feelings of powerlessness.

## The Research

**Studies show:**
- Upright posture increases positive mood
- Expansive poses affect hormone levels
- Slouching increases negative thoughts
- Posture affects memory recall
- Body position influences emotions

## How Posture Affects Confidence

**The mechanism:**
- Body-to-brain feedback
- Hormonal changes (some debate)
- Self-perception affected
- Creates confidence cycle
- Others perceive you differently

## Confident Posture Looks Like

**Characteristics:**
- Upright spine
- Open chest
- Shoulders back and down
- Head held high
- Eye contact easier
- Taking up appropriate space

## Low-Confidence Posture

**Signs:**
- Hunched shoulders
- Collapsed chest
- Making yourself small
- Looking down
- Closed body language

## Practical Application

**Use posture for confidence:**
- Before important meetings
- During interviews
- When feeling anxious
- For public speaking
- Daily practice builds habit
