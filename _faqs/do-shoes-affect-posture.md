# Do Your Shoes Affect Your Posture?

**Quick Answer:** Yes, shoes significantly affect posture. Your feet are your foundationâ€”footwear influences alignment through your ankles, knees, hips, and spine.

## How Shoes Affect Posture

**The chain reaction:**
- Foot position affects ankle angle
- Ankle affects knee alignment
- Knee affects hip position
- Hip affects spine alignment

## Problematic Shoe Types

**May cause issues:**
- High heels (shift weight forward)
- Completely flat shoes
- Worn-out shoes
- Flip-flops (alter gait)
- Narrow toe boxes

## Better Shoe Features

**Look for:**
- Proper arch support
- Low, stable heel
- Wide toe box
- Good cushioning
- Appropriate for activity

## When to Consider Orthotics

**May help if:**
- Flat feet or high arches
- Chronic foot/knee/hip pain
- Leg length difference
- Professional recommendation

Varying your footwear gives your feet different support, preventing overuse patterns.
