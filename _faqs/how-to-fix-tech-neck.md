# How Do I Fix Tech Neck?

**Quick Answer:** Fix tech neck by raising screens to eye level, doing chin tucks and chest stretches, taking regular breaks, and strengthening neck and upper back muscles. Most people see improvement within 2-4 weeks of consistent effort.

## What Is Tech Neck?

**Definition:**
- Forward head posture from device use
- Neck pain and stiffness
- Rounded upper back
- Tight chest and shoulders
- Common in all ages now

## Best Exercises

**Daily routine:**
- Chin tucks (10-15 reps, 3x daily)
- Chest doorway stretches
- Shoulder blade squeezes
- Upper back extensions
- Neck rotations

## Device Ergonomics

**Screen positioning:**
- Phone: Raise to eye level
- Laptop: External monitor or stand
- Desktop: Screen top at eye level
- Tablet: Use a stand, not lap
- Take breaks every 20-30 minutes

## Quick Relief

**When symptoms flare:**
- Gentle neck stretches
- Heat or ice
- Massage tight muscles
- Posture reset
- Walk and move

## Prevention

**Habits that help:**
- Set phone reminders for breaks
- Use voice commands more
- Print long documents
- Voice-to-text messaging
- Limit unnecessary scrolling

Consistency matters more than intensity. Small daily habits beat occasional intense corrections.
