# How Do I Walk With Good Posture?

**Quick Answer:** Walk with your head up and eyes forward, shoulders relaxed and back, core lightly engaged, arms swinging naturally, and feet landing heel-to-toe. Stand tall as if a string is pulling you up from the crown of your head.

## Key Elements of Good Walking Posture

**Head and neck:**
- Look forward, not down
- Chin parallel to ground
- Ears over shoulders

**Upper body:**
- Shoulders relaxed down
- Slight chest lift
- Arms swing naturally (opposite arm/leg)

**Core and hips:**
- Core lightly engaged
- Pelvis neutral
- Hips move smoothly

**Feet and legs:**
- Land heel first
- Roll through to toe
- Push off with back foot
- Natural stride length

## Common Walking Mistakes

**Avoid:**
- Looking at phone while walking
- Shuffling feet
- Arms tight at sides
- Leaning forward
- Overstriding

## Benefits of Good Walking Posture

- Less back and neck pain
- Better breathing
- More efficient movement
- Improved appearance
- Increased energy

## Practice Tips

- Start with short focused walks
- Use a mirror or window reflections
- Have someone video you
- Gradually build awareness
- Make corrections natural over time
