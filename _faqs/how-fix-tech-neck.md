# How Do You Fix Tech Neck?

**Quick Answer:** Fix tech neck by raising your devices to eye level, doing chin tucks and neck strengthening exercises daily, stretching tight muscles, and taking regular screen breaks. Consistency is key.

## Immediate Fixes

**Device Positioning:**
- Hold phone at eye level
- Raise computer screen
- Use laptop stand
- Move monitor up

**Take Breaks:**
- Every 20-30 minutes
- Look up and around
- Roll shoulders back
- Quick neck stretches

## Daily Exercises

**Must-Do:**
1. **Chin tucks** - 10 reps, 3x daily
2. **Neck stretches** - 30 seconds each direction
3. **Upper back strengthening** - rows, pull-aparts

**Additional Help:**
- Wall angels
- Chest doorway stretches
- Prone Y raises
- Face pulls

## Habit Changes

**Reduce screen time:**
- Use voice commands
- Make calls instead of texting
- Set screen time limits

**Better positions:**
- Eyes forward more often
- Workstation ergonomics
- Supportive pillow at night

## Timeline

- Week 1-2: Awareness builds
- Week 3-4: Improvement begins
- Month 2-3: Significant change
- Ongoing: Maintenance needed
