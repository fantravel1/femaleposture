# Hip Labral Tear

Hip labral tears affect the cartilage ring around the hip socket, potentially impacting hip stability and movement.

## Understanding Labral Tears

### What Happens

- Labrum becomes torn
- May cause clicking
- Hip stability affected
- Can trap in joint

### Posture Effects

- Movement limitations
- Compensation patterns
- May avoid certain positions
- Gait may change

## Symptoms

### Common Presentations

**Typical symptoms:**
- Groin or front hip pain
- Clicking or catching
- Stiffness
- Pain with activity

### Activity Related

**Often worse with:**
- Deep squatting
- Pivoting movements
- Prolonged sitting
- Athletic activities

## Causes

### Contributing Factors

**Common causes:**
- Hip impingement
- Repetitive movements
- Trauma
- Hip structure variations

### Women's Considerations

**Higher risk with:**
- Hip flexibility extremes
- Dance and gymnastics
- Yoga positions

## Management

### Conservative Approach

**Key elements:**
- Activity modification
- Hip strengthening
- Avoid aggravating positions
- Core stability

### Movement Guidelines

**Helpful strategies:**
- Strengthen around hip
- Avoid deep hip flexion
- Maintain hip strength
- Control range of motion

## Key Points

1. **Often from impingement** — Structural factors
2. **Strengthen hip** — Support the joint
3. **Avoid deep flexion** — May aggravate
4. **Conservative first** — Many improve
