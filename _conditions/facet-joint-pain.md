# Facet Joint Pain

Facet joint dysfunction causes localized back or neck pain, often related to posture and movement patterns.

## Understanding Facet Joints

### What Happens

- Small joints become irritated
- Often from compression
- Pain with extension
- Localized symptoms

### Posture Connection

- Extension increases load
- Poor posture stresses joints
- Sitting posture matters
- Sleep position affects

## Symptoms

### Typical Presentation

**Common findings:**
- One-sided pain
- Worse with extension
- Morning stiffness
- Pain with rotation

### Location Specific

**Depends on level:**
- Cervical: neck pain
- Thoracic: mid-back pain
- Lumbar: low back pain

## Management

### Posture Changes

**Key adjustments:**
- Reduce excessive curves
- Support during sitting
- Avoid prolonged positions
- Movement variety

### Exercise Approach

**Helpful strategies:**
- Flexion-based stretches
- Core strengthening
- Avoid excessive extension
- Maintain mobility

## Key Points

1. **Posture matters** — Affects joint loading
2. **Movement helps** — Avoid static positions
3. **Extension may aggravate** — Be careful with backbends
4. **Core supports** — Reduce joint stress
