# Hammertoes

Hammertoes cause abnormal bending of the smaller toes, affecting balance, push-off, and overall gait patterns.

## Understanding Hammertoes

### What Happens

- Toes bend at middle joint
- Can become rigid
- Corns develop on top
- Pain with shoes

### Posture Effects

- Balance affected
- Push-off weakened
- Weight distribution changes
- Gait adaptations

## Causes

### Contributing Factors

**Common causes:**
- Tight footwear
- High heels
- Muscle imbalance
- Nerve conditions

### Progression

**Stages:**
- Flexible initially
- Becomes semi-rigid
- May become fixed

## Management

### Conservative Care

**Options:**
- Roomier shoes
- Toe exercises
- Padding
- Stretching

### Exercises

**Helpful movements:**
- Toe curls with towel
- Toe spreads
- Marble pickups
- Barefoot time

## Key Points

1. **Footwear related** — Tight shoes contribute
2. **Early treatment best** — While still flexible
3. **Strengthen intrinsics** — Toe exercises help
4. **Affects balance** — Whole foot function matters
