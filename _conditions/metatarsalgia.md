# Metatarsalgia

Metatarsalgia causes pain in the ball of the foot, affecting push-off during walking and overall gait patterns.

## Understanding Metatarsalgia

### What Happens

- Metatarsal heads stressed
- Ball of foot painful
- Weight distribution changes
- Calluses may develop

### Posture Effects

- Gait changes
- Avoid forefoot pressure
- Compensation patterns
- May affect balance

## Causes

### Contributing Factors

**Common causes:**
- High heels
- Tight shoes
- High arch
- Excess weight

### Women's Risk

**Higher prevalence:**
- Footwear choices
- High heel wearing
- Narrower shoes

## Symptoms

### Common Presentations

**Typical symptoms:**
- Burning in ball of foot
- Stone bruise feeling
- Worse with standing
- Better barefoot often

### Associated Findings

**Often seen with:**
- Calluses
- Toe deformities
- Morton's neuroma

## Management

### Footwear Changes

**Key modifications:**
- Lower heels
- Wider toe box
- Metatarsal pads
- Cushioned soles

### Exercises

**Helpful movements:**
- Toe strengthening
- Calf stretching
- Foot mobility
- Arch strengthening

## Key Points

1. **Footwear key factor** — Modify shoe choices
2. **High heels contribute** — Shift weight forward
3. **Padding helps** — Metatarsal support
4. **Strengthen feet** — Improve function
