# Achilles Tendinopathy

Achilles tendinopathy causes pain in the heel cord, affecting push-off during walking and running patterns.

## Understanding Achilles Issues

### What Happens

- Tendon becomes painful
- May be swollen
- Stiffness in morning
- Pain with activity

### Posture Effects

- Shortened stride
- Altered push-off
- Compensation up chain
- Changed running form

## Types

### Insertional

**At heel bone:**
- Pain at attachment
- Bone spurs possible
- Worse with stairs

### Mid-Portion

**In tendon body:**
- 2-6 cm above heel
- Thickening possible
- Activity related

## Management

### Initial Treatment

**Options:**
- Load modification
- Heel raises temporarily
- Eccentric exercises
- Gradual loading

### Rehabilitation

**Key elements:**
- Eccentric strengthening
- Calf flexibility
- Gradual return to activity
- Address contributing factors

## Key Points

1. **Loading important** — Controlled loading heals
2. **Eccentric exercises** — Gold standard treatment
3. **Check biomechanics** — Foot and hip alignment
4. **Patience needed** — Recovery takes time
