# Arthritis and Posture

Arthritis affects millions of women, causing joint pain and stiffness. Good posture and proper alignment help protect joints from additional stress, while poor posture can accelerate wear and increase pain.

## Types of Arthritis

### Osteoarthritis (OA)

**Wear-and-tear arthritis:**
- Cartilage breaks down
- Most common type
- Affects hands, knees, hips, spine
- More common with age
- Influenced by mechanics

### Rheumatoid Arthritis (RA)

**Autoimmune condition:**
- Immune system attacks joints
- Inflammation primary issue
- Often affects smaller joints
- Systemic symptoms
- Requires medical management

## The Posture Connection

### How Posture Affects Arthritis

**Poor alignment:**
- Uneven joint loading
- Accelerated wear
- Increased pain
- Compensatory patterns
- More deformity risk

**Good alignment:**
- Even joint loading
- Slower progression
- Less pain
- Better function
- Joint protection

### Specific Joints

**Spine arthritis:**
- Neutral spine reduces stress
- Avoid prolonged flexion
- Movement variety important
- Support when sitting

**Knee arthritis:**
- Alignment affects wear pattern
- Hip strength helps alignment
- Avoid excessive loading
- Proper footwear

**Hip arthritis:**
- Core strength matters
- Pelvic position affects
- Avoid prolonged sitting
- Range of motion maintenance

## Exercise for Arthritic Joints

### Principles

**Guidelines:**
- Motion is lotion
- Low impact preferred
- Range of motion priority
- Strength protects joints
- Listen to pain (but move)

### Helpful Activities

**Generally beneficial:**
- Swimming and water exercise
- Walking
- Cycling
- Tai chi
- Gentle yoga

### Strengthening

**Important because:**
- Muscles support joints
- Reduces joint stress
- Improves function
- Slows progression

## Daily Management

### Joint Protection

**Principles:**
- Use largest joint possible
- Distribute loads evenly
- Avoid prolonged gripping
- Take movement breaks
- Proper lifting technique

### Ergonomics

**Workspace:**
- Supportive chair
- Proper desk height
- Easy-reach items
- Adaptive tools if needed

## Key Points

1. **Alignment matters** — Even loading protects joints
2. **Movement helps** — Despite seeming counterintuitive
3. **Strengthen muscles** — Supports and protects joints
4. **Low impact** — Protect while staying active
5. **Joint protection** — Smart daily habits
6. **Professional guidance** — Customize to your type
