# Hip Arthritis

Hip osteoarthritis causes progressive stiffness and pain that significantly impacts walking, posture, and daily function.

## Understanding Hip Arthritis

### What Happens

- Cartilage degenerates
- Joint space narrows
- Bone spurs form
- Range decreases

### Posture Effects

- Shortened stride
- Trunk lean to affected side
- Pelvic tilt changes
- Compensation patterns

## Symptoms

### Common Presentations

**Typical symptoms:**
- Groin pain
- Stiffness after sitting
- Pain with walking
- Reduced hip rotation

### Progression

**Over time:**
- Range of motion decreases
- Night pain possible
- Function affected
- Gait changes

## Management

### Exercise Focus

**Key elements:**
- Maintain mobility
- Hip strengthening
- Core stability
- Pool exercises helpful

### Daily Strategies

**Helpful approaches:**
- Warm up before activity
- Regular movement breaks
- Appropriate footwear
- Assistive devices if needed

## Key Points

1. **Movement is medicine** — Gentle exercise helps
2. **Strengthen around joint** — Muscles support hip
3. **Maintain flexibility** — Prevent further stiffness
4. **Watch compensations** — Address gait changes
