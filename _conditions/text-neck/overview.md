# Text Neck: The Modern Posture Problem

> **A Modern Epidemic:** Text neck describes the neck strain and postural changes from frequently looking down at smartphones and devices.

---

## What Is Text Neck?

### The Problem

- Looking down increases neck strain
- Head weighs 10-12 lbs
- At 60Â° forward, load increases to 60 lbs
- Hours of this daily = cumulative strain

### Symptoms

- Neck pain and stiffness
- Shoulder tension
- Headaches
- Upper back pain
- Forward head posture

---

## Why It Matters

### Short-Term

- Muscle fatigue
- Tension headaches
- Discomfort

### Long-Term Concerns

- Chronic postural changes
- Accelerated wear on spine
- Persistent pain patterns

---

## Prevention

### Change Phone Habits

- Raise phone to eye level
- Take frequent breaks
- Reduce screen time
- Use voice commands

### Exercises

- Chin tucks
- Neck stretches
- Upper back strengthening
- Chest stretches

---

## Quick Relief

1. Put the phone down
2. Chin tucks (10 reps)
3. Upper trap stretches
4. Shoulder rolls
5. Move and walk

---

## For Parents

Children and teens are especially vulnerable:
- Still developing
- High phone use
- Educate early
- Set limits and model good habits
