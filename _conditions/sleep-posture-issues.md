# Sleep Position Problems

How you sleep for 6-8 hours affects your spine and can contribute to pain patterns. Poor sleep positions can worsen existing issues or create new problems.

## Common Sleep Position Issues

### Stomach Sleeping

**Problems:**
- Neck rotated for hours
- Lower back overarched
- Shoulder compression
- Generally worst position

### Side Sleeping Without Support

**Issues:**
- Spine sags
- Hip compression
- Shoulder compression
- Needs pillow between knees

### Back Sleeping Problems

**Potential issues:**
- Too flat can strain back
- Need pillow under knees
- Right pillow height critical

## Effects

### Morning Symptoms

**May experience:**
- Morning stiffness
- Pain on waking
- Numbness
- Symptoms improve through day

### Chronic Issues

**Long-term problems:**
- Reinforces poor patterns
- Doesn't allow recovery
- Contributes to conditions

## Solutions

### Best Positions

**Generally recommended:**
- Side with pillow between knees
- Back with pillow under knees
- Avoid stomach sleeping

### Pillow Selection

**Important factors:**
- Height matches position
- Supports neck curve
- Comfortable material

### Mattress Matters

**Consider:**
- Medium-firm for most
- Replace when worn
- Individual preferences

## Key Points

1. **6-8 hours matters** — Significant time
2. **Position affects pain** — Can help or hurt
3. **Stomach worst** — Try to change
4. **Support important** — Pillows between knees
5. **Morning symptoms clue** — May be position
