# Poor Proprioception

Proprioception is your body's awareness of position and movement in space. Poor proprioception makes it difficult to maintain good posture without conscious effort.

## Understanding Proprioception

### What It Is

- Joint position sense
- Awareness of body in space
- Unconscious feedback
- Essential for posture

### Why It Matters

- Automatic posture maintenance
- Balance and coordination
- Movement control
- Injury prevention

## How It Relates to Posture

### When It's Poor

- Difficulty knowing when slouching
- Poor balance
- Clumsy movements
- Need mirrors to check posture

### Contributing Factors

- Aging
- Injury
- Sedentary lifestyle
- Joint problems

## Improving Proprioception

### Balance Training

- Single-leg stance
- Unstable surfaces
- Eyes closed progression

### Body Awareness

- Mindful movement
- Yoga/Pilates
- Movement variety

## Key Points

1. **Essential for posture** — Automatic maintenance
2. **Can be trained** — Improves with practice
3. **Balance work helps** — Challenges system
4. **Movement variety** — Keeps it sharp
