Why Life Stage Matters for Posture

Your body goes through profound changes throughout life. Hormones, pregnancy, breastfeeding, menopause, and aging all affect your musculoskeletal system in unique ways. Generic posture advice doesn't account for these differences.

  • Pregnancy: Relaxin hormone loosens joints, center of gravity shifts forward
  • Postpartum: Core weakness, hours of feeding and carrying baby
  • Menopause: Estrogen decline affects bone density and joint health
  • Senior years: Fall prevention becomes critical for independence
