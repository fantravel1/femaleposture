## Posture for Listening

### How Hearing Loss Affects Position

**Common adaptations:**
- Leaning toward speaker
- Turning good ear
- Head tilting
- Forward head posture
- Compensating positions

### Problems with Compensation

**Strain from:**
- Constant head turning

- Muscle tension
- Postural changes
