# Postpartum Body Image and Posture

> **Your Body Did Something Amazing:** It created life. The way you think about your changed body affects how you carry it.

---

## The Postpartum Reality

### Bodies Change

**After pregnancy:**
- Belly is different
- Breasts are different
- Hips may be wider
- Weight distributed differently
- Everything changed

### Society's Messages

**Pressure to:**
- "Bounce back"
- Look like before
- Lose weight quickly
- Hide signs of motherhood

### The Truth

**Reality:**
- Bodies don't "bounce"
- Recovery takes time
- Some changes permanent
- This is normal

---

## Body Image and Posture

### The Connection

**How we feel affects:**
- How we stand
- How we carry ourselves
- Confidence or hiding
- Physical presentation

### Negative Body Image

**May lead to:**
- Slouching to hide belly
- Rounding shoulders
- Trying to appear smaller
- Collapsed posture

### Positive Body Image

**Supports:**
- Standing tall
- Open posture
- Confidence
- Natural carriage

---

## Common Struggles

### The Belly

**Many women:**
- Feel self-conscious
- Try to hide it
- Struggle with looser skin
- Miss flat stomach

### The Reality

**Understanding:**
- Stretched skin takes time
- Diastasis affects appearance
- Some changes permanent
- Function over appearance

### Clothes That Don't Fit

**Frustrating:**
- Pre-pregnancy clothes don't fit
- Nothing feels right
- Shopping is hard
- Transition period difficult

### Finding What Works

**Practical:**
- Clothes that fit now
- Comfortable options
- Not waiting to fit old clothes
- Wardrobe adaptation

---

## Shifting Perspective

### What Your Body Did

**Remember:**
- Grew a human being
- Sustained life
- Went through birth
- Amazing accomplishment

### Function Over Form

**Focus on:**
- What your body can do
- Strength and capability
- Feeding/caring for baby
- Daily functioning

### Comparison Trap

**Avoid:**
- Social media comparisons
- Celebrity "bounce backs"
- Other new moms
- Unrealistic standards

---

## Building Acceptance

### Gradual Process

**Acceptance takes:**
- Time
- Intention
- Self-compassion
- Practice

### Daily Practices

**What helps:**
- Positive self-talk
- Gratitude for body
- Focusing on function
- Wearing comfortable clothes

### Posture as Practice

**Standing tall:**
- Even when uncomfortable
- Not hiding
- Taking up space
- Body language of confidence

---

## Physical Recovery

### Realistic Timeline

**Recovery takes:**
- Minimum 6 weeks basic healing
- 6-12 months for many changes
- Longer for some
- No rush

### Exercise and Body Image

**Healthy approach:**
- Exercise for health and mood
- Not punishment
- Gradual return
- See [Exercise Timeline](/life-stages/postpartum/exercise-timeline/)

### Strength Focus

**Reframe:**
- Getting stronger
- Rebuilding function
- For long-term health
- Not weight loss

---

## Feeding and Body

### Breastfeeding Bodies

**If nursing:**
- Breasts change significantly
- Body holds some fat
- Shape different
- Hormones affect body

### Weaning Changes

**After weaning:**
- Body changes again
- Breasts may deflate
- Ongoing adjustment
- Another transition
