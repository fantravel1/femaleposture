# Solo Parenting Physical Demands

> **Parenting alone means carrying all the physical demands without the relief of a co-parent. Protecting your body becomes even more critical when there's no one to take over when you're exhausted.**

---

## The Solo Parent Reality

Single parenting intensifies physical demands.

### What's Different

**Without partner:**
- No trading off baby
- All lifting is yours
- No night shift relief
- Every physical task
- Recovery harder

### Why Body Care Matters More

**Self-preservation:**
- You can't be injured
- No backup for you
- Your health is essential
- Prevention critical
- Must prioritize yourself

---

## Strategic Self-Care

### Non-Negotiables

**Protect these:**
- Basic sleep (when possible)
- Eating regularly
- Some movement
- Medical care when needed
- Cannot skip

### Efficiency

**Conserving energy:**
- Simplify everything possible
- Good enough is fine
- Systems that save effort
- Energy for essentials
- Let things go

---

## Lifting and Carrying

### Every Lift Matters

**With no breaks:**
- Proper technique always
- No rushed lifts
- Get close before lifting
- Lift with legs
- Cumulative strain adds up

### Reducing Lifts

**Minimizing:**
- Let baby climb when able
- Sit to interact
- Meet at their level
- Don't carry unnecessarily
- Find alternatives

### Baby Wearing

**Hands-free carrying:**
- Distributes weight better
- Frees your hands
- Sustainable holding
- Proper carrier essential
- Rotation of positions

---

## Night Waking Strategies

### No Relief Coming

**Managing alone:**
- Setup for efficiency
- Minimize movement needed
- Bedside organization
- Fastest return to sleep
- Everything within reach

### Protecting Sleep

**What you can control:**
- Sleep when baby sleeps (really)
- Ask for occasional overnight help
- Childcare for daytime rest
- Any rest counts
- Survival mode acceptable
