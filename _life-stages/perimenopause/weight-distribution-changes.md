# Body Weight Distribution Changes in Perimenopause

> **Many women notice their body shape changing during perimenopauseâ€”weight shifting to the midsection even without overall weight gain. Understanding these changes helps you adapt and maintain health.**

---

## What Changes

Hormones affect where fat is stored.

### The Shift

**What happens:**
- Fat redistribution
- More abdominal fat
- Less on hips and thighs
- Waist circumference increases
- Shape changes

### Why It Happens

**Hormonal effects:**
- Declining estrogen
- Relative androgen increase
- Metabolic changes
- Body composition shifts
- Natural process

### Visceral Fat
