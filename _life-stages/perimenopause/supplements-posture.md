# Supplements for Posture Support in Perimenopause

---

## Why Supplementation Changes

Perimenopause affects nutrient needs and absorption.

### What's Different Now

**Changing needs:**
- Estrogen decline affects bone
- Muscle maintenance harder
- Absorption may change
- Increased requirements
- Diet alone may not suffice

### Food First Approach

**Priority:**
- Nutrients from food best
- Supplements supplement, not replace
- Quality diet foundation
- Targeted additions
- Whole-food focus

---

## Calcium

### Why It Matters

**Bone health:**
- Bone loss accelerates
- Calcium is building block
- May need more now
- Essential for bones
- Prevention focus

### Recommendations

**Guidelines:**
- 1000-1200 mg daily (total)
- From food and supplements
- Divided doses better absorbed
- Not too much (risks exist)
- Food sources preferred

### Food Sources

**Best options:**
- Dairy products
- Leafy greens
- Fortified foods
- Sardines with bones
- Tofu (calcium-set)

### Supplementation

**If needed:**
- Make up difference from diet
- Calcium citrate often recommended
- Divided doses
- With food
- Not megadoses

## Vitamin D

### Essential Partner

**Why needed:**
- Helps calcium absorption
- Bone health essential
- Muscle function
- Immune support
- Many deficient

### Testing

**Know your levels:**
- Blood test available
- Many are low
- Know before supplementing high doses
- Target range
- Personalized approach

### Sources

**Getting vitamin D:**
- Sunlight exposure
- Fatty fish
- Fortified foods
- Supplements common
- Geographic considerations

### Supplementation

**If recommended:**
- D3 preferred form
- Dosing based on levels
- With fat for absorption
- Not excessive
- Retest periodically

## Magnesium

### Multiple Benefits

**What it does:**
- Bone health support
- Muscle function
- Sleep support
- Stress and anxiety
- Common deficiency

### Signs of Need

**Possible indicators:**
- Muscle cramps
- Sleep difficulties
- Anxiety
- Headaches
- Fatigue

### Forms

**Supplementation:**
- Glycinate for sleep and anxiety
- Citrate for bowel regularity
- Various forms available
- Start low
- Quality matters

### Food Sources

**Best foods:**
- Nuts and seeds
- Dark leafy greens
- Whole grains
- Dark chocolate
- Legumes

## Omega-3 Fatty Acids

### Inflammation and Joints

**Benefits:**
- Anti-inflammatory
- Joint comfort
- Heart health
- Brain health
- Multiple benefits

### Sources

**Best options:**
- Fatty fish (salmon, mackerel)
- Fish oil supplements
- Algae-based for vegetarians
- Quality matters
- EPA and DHA forms

### Supplementation

**If taking:**
- Quality purified fish oil
- EPA and DHA content
- Appropriate dosing
- May thin blood
- Discuss with provider
