# Hair and Skin Changes in Perimenopause

> **Perimenopause brings visible changes to hair and skin that can affect how you feel about your body. Understanding these hormonal effects helps you adapt your self-care and maintain confidence.**

---

## What's Changing

Hormones affect visible tissues.

### Hair Changes

**What may happen:**
- Thinning hair
- Texture changes
- Dryness
- Slower growth
- Distribution changes

### Skin Changes

**Hormonal effects:**
- Drier skin
- Less elasticity
- Fine lines increasing
- Collagen loss
- Texture changes

---

## Why It Happens

### Estrogen's Role

**In hair and skin:**
- Maintains collagen
- Supports thickness
- Provides hydration
- Keeps elasticity
- Protective effects

### Declining Estrogen

**Results:**
- Collagen production slows
- Skin thins
- Dryness increases
- Hair affected
- Visible changes
