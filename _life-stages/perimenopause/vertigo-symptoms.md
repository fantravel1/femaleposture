# Vertigo and Dizziness in Perimenopause

> **Unexpected dizziness and vertigo can emerge during perimenopause. Understanding the hormonal connection helps you manage symptoms and know when to seek medical evaluation.**

---

## The Hormone Connection

Hormones affect balance systems.

### How Estrogen Affects Balance

**Estrogen's role:**
- Affects inner ear function
- Impacts blood vessel function
- Influences blood pressure
- Affects brain function
- Multiple mechanisms

### During Perimenopause

**What happens:**
- Fluctuating estrogen
- Blood pressure changes
- Possible inner ear effects
- Various triggers
- New symptoms possible

---

## Types of Dizziness

### Different Experiences

**What you might feel:**
- Spinning (vertigo)
- Lightheadedness
- Unsteadiness
- Feeling faint
- Off-balance sensation

### Triggers

**Common causes:**
- Position changes
- Hot flashes
- Blood sugar drops
- Dehydration
- Stress

---

## When It's Perimenopause-Related

### Likely Hormonal

**Characteristics:**
- New during perimenopause
- Correlates with other symptoms
- No other explanation
- Fluctuates
- Temporary episodes

### Other Causes

**May need evaluation:**
- Inner ear disorders
- Blood pressure issues
- Medication effects
- Other conditions
- Worth checking

---

## Safety Considerations

### Fall Prevention

**When dizzy:**
- Move slowly
- Hold onto support
- Sit or lie down
- Wait for it to pass
- Don't push through

### Driving Caution

**Important:**
- Don't drive if dizzy
- Pull over if symptoms start
- Safety first
- Serious risk
- Be cautious

---

## Managing Symptoms

### Position Changes

**Moving safely:**
- Rise slowly
- Sit on bed edge first
- Stand holding support
- Wait before walking
- Gradual transitions

### During Episodes

**What helps:**
- Sit or lie down
- Focus on fixed point
- Deep breathing
- Wait it out
- Don't panic

### Prevention

**Reducing episodes:**
- Stay hydrated
- Regular meals
- Avoid triggers
- Manage stress
- Healthy habits

## Connection to Hot Flashes

### Related Symptoms

**Often together:**
- Hot flash triggers dizziness
- Blood vessel changes
- Similar timing
- Connected symptoms
- Common pattern

### Management

**Addressing both:**
- Hot flash management helps
- Cooling strategies
- Triggers avoidance
- Overall symptom management
- Connected approach

## Blood Sugar and Dizziness

### The Connection

**Low blood sugar:**
- Causes dizziness
- More common with hormone changes
- Related to eating patterns
- Manageable
- Important factor

### Prevention

**What helps:**
- Regular meals
- Protein with carbs
- No skipping breakfast
- Steady intake
- Blood sugar stability

## When to See a Doctor

### Evaluation Needed

**Seek help if:**
- Severe vertigo
- Frequent episodes
- Hearing changes
- Persistent symptoms
- Affecting function

### What to Report

**Tell provider:**
- Description of symptoms
- Timing and triggers
- Duration
- Associated symptoms
- Pattern

### Possible Tests

**Evaluation may include:**
- Blood pressure checks
- Blood tests
- Hearing tests
- Inner ear evaluation
- Other as needed
