- Brain fog

## Thyroid Basics

### What It Does

**Thyroid function:**
- Regulates metabolism
- Affects energy
- Influences weight
- Impacts mood
- Essential gland

### Types of Problems

**Common issues:**
- Hypothyroidism (underactive)
- Hyperthyroidism (overactive)
- Autoimmune thyroid
- More common with age
- Women especially

## Hypothyroidism Symptoms

### What to Notice

**Signs:**
- Fatigue
- Weight gain
- Cold intolerance
- Constipation
- Dry skin

### Physical Effects

**Body impact:**
- Muscle weakness
- Joint pain
- Sluggishness
- Slowed movement
- Physical symptoms

### Posture Connection

**How it affects:**
- Low energy affects posture
- May slump more
- Less motivation to exercise
- Muscle effects
- Physical presentation

---

## Hyperthyroidism Symptoms

### What to Notice

**Signs:**
- Anxiety
- Weight loss
- Heat intolerance
- Rapid heartbeat
- Tremors

### Physical Effects

**Body impact:**
- Muscle weakness (different kind)
- Shakiness
- Hyperactivity
- Sleep difficulty
- Physical symptoms
