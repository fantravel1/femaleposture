## Movement for Energy

### Paradox

**Understand:**
- Exercise when tired seems wrong
- But it helps
- Creates energy
- Improves sleep
- Worth the effort

### What Type

**Best for energy:**
- Walking
- Light activity
- Not exhausting exercise
- Energizing not depleting
- Gentle movement

### Timing

**When to exercise:**
- When you have some energy
- Earlier in day usually
- Not before bed
- Strategic timing
