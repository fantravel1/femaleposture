## The Hormone Connection

Estrogen affects joints more than many realize.

### What Estrogen Does

**Joint protection:**
- Reduces inflammation
- Supports cartilage
- Lubricates joints
- Maintains tissue health
- Protective effects

### When Estrogen Fluctuates

**Changes occur:**
- Inflammation increases
- Stiffness develops
- Aches and pains
- Morning stiffness
- Symptom onset

## Common Symptoms

### What You Might Experience

**Typical complaints:**
- Morning stiffness
- Aching joints
- Reduced flexibility
- Creaking or cracking
- Discomfort with movement

### Most Affected Areas

**Common locations:**
- Hands and fingers
- Knees
- Hips
- Shoulders
- Spine

### Pattern

**When it occurs:**
- Worse in morning
- After inactivity
- Better with movement
- May fluctuate with cycle
- Variable pattern
