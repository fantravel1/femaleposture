# Perimenopause, Desire, and Your Body

> **Changes Are Coming:** Perimenopause can affect desire, physical comfort, and body image. Understanding what's happening helps.

---

## What Changes

### Hormone Effects

**Fluctuating hormones may cause:**
- Variable desire
- Some physical changes beginning
- Mood effects
- Energy variations

### Physical Changes

**May include:**
- Beginning vaginal changes
- Breast changes
- Body composition shifts
- Various symptoms

---

## Desire and Energy

### Variable Libido

**Common experience:**
- Day to day variation
- Hormone-related changes
- Not predictable
- Individual experience

### Contributing Factors

**What affects desire:**
- Fatigue
- Stress
- Mood changes
- Physical symptoms
- Relationship factors

---

## Body Comfort

### Physical Self

**Feeling in your body:**
- May feel different
- Changing shape
- New sensations
- Adjustment period

### Self-Image

**May be affected:**
- By body changes
- By symptom experience
- By comparison to before
- Need compassion

---

## Staying Connected

### Communication

**With partner:**
- Talk about changes
- Share what helps
- Be patient together
- Adapt as needed

### Physical Touch

**Beyond intimacy:**
- Affection
- Connection
- Touch that feels good
- Many forms of closeness

---

## Physical Strategies

### Exercise

**Helps by:**
- Improving mood
- Energy levels
- Body confidence
- Overall wellbeing

### Movement and Body

**Feeling good physically:**
- Activity helps
- Strength building
- Flexibility
- Connection to body

### Self-Care

**Practices that help:**
- What makes you feel good
- Body care routines
- Relaxation
- Treating yourself well

---

## Managing Symptoms

### If Physical Symptoms Interfere

**Options:**
- Lubricants (early changes)
- Vaginal moisturizers
- Talk to provider
- Treatment available

### Timing and Energy

**Finding good times:**
- When energy is better
- When symptoms less
- What works now
- Adapting

---

## Emotional Aspects

### Grief for Changes

**Normal to feel:**
- Loss of how things were
- Adjustment to changes
- Processing transition
- Part of the journey

### Self-Compassion

**Important:**
- Bodies change
- This is natural
- You're still you
- Be kind to yourself

---

## When to Get Help

### Talk to Provider If

**Concerning:**
- Significant physical symptoms
- Distress about changes
- Want treatment options
- Relationship effects

### What's Available

**Treatment options:**
- Hormone therapy (for some)
- Local treatments
- Therapy if helpful
- Various approaches
