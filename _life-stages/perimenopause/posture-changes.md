## What Changes

Hormones affect posture in multiple ways.

### Tissue Changes

**Hormonal effects:**
- Ligament laxity changes
- Muscle mass decline
- Joint changes
- Bone density affects
- Tissue health shifts

### Symptom Impact

**How symptoms affect posture:**
- Fatigue leads to slumping
- Hot flashes cause restlessness
- Poor sleep affects muscle function
- Mood impacts body language
- Multiple factors

## Common Postural Shifts

### What You Might Notice

**Typical changes:**
- Forward head increasing
- Shoulders rounding more
- Upper back kyphosis
- Core weakness showing
- Gradual shifts

### Why They Happen

**Contributing factors:**
- Muscle loss
- Fatigue
- Hormonal tissue effects
- Life stage factors
- Cumulative changes
