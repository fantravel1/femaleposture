## Why Breathing Matters

Breathing affects many aspects of pregnancy wellness.

### Physical Benefits

**What breathing provides:**
- Oxygen to baby
- Relaxation of tight muscles
- Posture support
- Core and pelvic floor connection
- Physical comfort

### Mental Benefits

**Psychological effects:**
- Stress reduction
- Anxiety management
- Focus and calm
- Present moment awareness
- Emotional regulation

## Breathing Challenges in Pregnancy

### What Changes

**Pregnancy effects:**
- Diaphragm compressed by uterus
- Rib cage expansion limited
- Breathlessness common (especially late)
- Breathing patterns change
- Adaptation needed

### Working With Changes

**Adjustment:**
- Upright positions help
- Shorter breaths may be normal
- Practice helps
- Listen to body
- Gentle approach

---

## Diaphragmatic Breathing

### The Foundation

**Basic technique:**
- Breathe into belly, not chest
- Diaphragm descends
- Belly expands gently
- Ribs widen
- Core and floor coordinated

### How to Practice

**Steps:**
- Sit or recline comfortably
- Hand on belly
- Breathe in, belly rises
- Breathe out, belly falls gently
- Relaxed, not forced

### Benefits

**What it does:**
- Full oxygenation
- Relaxation response
- Core connection
- Pelvic floor coordination
- Foundation for labor

---

## Relaxation Breathing

### For Stress Reduction

**Calming technique:**
- Longer exhale than inhale
- 4 counts in, 6-8 counts out
- Slow and steady
- Activates relaxation response
- Reduces anxiety

### When to Use

**Situations:**
- Feeling anxious
- Before sleep
- During stressful moments
- Regular practice
- Anytime calm needed

---

## Breathing for Posture

### Posture Connection

**How breathing affects alignment:**
- Breath supports spine
- Core engagement with exhale
- Rib position affects posture
- Awareness enhances alignment
- Integrated function

### Practice with Posture

**Combined:**
- Sit or stand tall
- Breathe into belly and back ribs
- Exhale gently engage core
- Maintain alignment
- Repeat with awareness

---

## Rib Cage Breathing

### Technique

**For pregnancy:**
- Breath into side ribs
- Hands on sides of ribs
- Feel ribs expand sideways
- Accommodates growing belly
- Helpful late pregnancy

### Benefits

**Why practice:**
- Better oxygenation when compressed
- More comfortable breathing
- Posture support
- Preparation for labor
- Practical during late pregnancy

---

## Breathing for Common Discomforts

### For Tension

**Shoulder and neck:**
- Breathe in, shoulders drop on exhale
- Release tension with breath
- Conscious relaxation
- Regular practice
- Relief accumulates

### For Heartburn

**Positioning:**
- Upright breathing
- After meals
- Not lying flat
- Gentle breaths
- Some relief

### For Anxiety

**Calming:**
- Extended exhale
- Slow pace
- Regular practice
- Before triggering situations
- Skill building
