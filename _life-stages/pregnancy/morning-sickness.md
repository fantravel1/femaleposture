# Morning Sickness and Posture

> **When You Feel Awful:** Nausea can strike any time during early pregnancy. How you position yourself can help or hinder.

---

## Understanding Morning Sickness

### When It Happens

**Timing:**
- First trimester typically
- Can be all day (not just morning)
- Usually improves by week 12-14
- For some, lasts longer

### Why It Occurs

**Causes:**
- Hormone changes (hCG)
- Sensitivity to smells
- Stomach changes
- Various factors

---

## Positions That Help

### Lying Down

**When nauseous:**
- Left side often helps
- Propped slightly up
- Knees bent
- Relaxed position

### Sitting

**If upright:**
- Good air flow
- Not constricted
- Cool environment
- Near bathroom

### Avoiding Triggers

**Position for:**
- Away from smells
- Cool air access
- Ability to rest
- Quick bathroom access

---

## Positions That May Worsen

### To Avoid

**Can increase nausea:**
- Lying flat on back
- Bending over
- Sudden position changes
- Hot, stuffy environments

### Movement

**Be careful with:**
- Quick movements
- Bending down
- Getting up fast
- Jarring motions

---

## Getting Up

### From Lying Down

**When nauseous:**
- Move very slowly
- Roll to side first
- Sit on edge briefly
- Rise gradually

### Morning Strategy

**On waking:**
- Stay in bed a moment
- Eat something small (if tolerable)
- Rise slowly
- Don't rush

---

## At Work

### If Nausea Strikes

**What to do:**
- Find cool air
- Sit if needed
- Loosen clothing
- Deep breaths

### Desk Position

**For comfort:**
- Near window if possible
- Away from smells
- Quick bathroom access
- Able to rest if needed

### Taking Breaks

**When nauseous:**
- Fresh air
- Walk slowly
- Cool down
- Rest as needed

---

## Eating and Nausea

### Eating Position

**When eating:**
- Sit upright
- Not lying down
- Small portions
- Slow eating

### After Eating

**Position:**
- Stay upright
- Don't lie flat immediately
- Gentle movement helps digestion
- Wait before activity

---

## Physical Activity

### During Nausea Phases

**Exercise:**
- May help or worsen
- Gentle activity often good
- Don't push if sick
- Listen to your body

### What Works

**If exercising:**
- Walking often okay
- Avoid jarring
- Stay cool
- Stop if worsens

---

## Breathing

### For Nausea Relief

**Deep breathing:**
- Slow breaths
- Belly breathing
- May help
- Calms system

### Fresh Air

**If possible:**
- Open window
- Step outside
- Cool breeze
- Away from smells

---

## Body Tension

### Nausea Creates Tension

**What happens:**
- Muscles tense with nausea
- Shoulders rise
- Body clenches
- Adds discomfort

### Releasing Tension

**Try:**
- Shoulder drops
- Jaw relaxation
- Gentle stretching
- Conscious release

---

## Sleep Positions

### For Nausea

**At night:**
- Side-lying
- Slightly elevated
- Comfortable
- Snacks nearby for morning

### Morning Nausea Prep

**Before bed:**
- Crackers by bed
- Water nearby
- Easy access to bathroom
- Prepare for morning

---

## When It's Severe

### Hyperemesis Gravidarum

**Severe nausea/vomiting:**
- Beyond typical morning sickness
- Can't keep food/water down
- Losing weight
- Requires medical care

### When to Call Doctor

**Seek help if:**
- Can't keep fluids down
- Losing weight
- Very weak
- Severe symptoms
