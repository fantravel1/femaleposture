# Sports Tryout Preparation

> **Sports tryouts test both physical readiness and mental toughness. Proper preparation—conditioning, injury prevention, and confidence building—gives you the best chance to show what you can do.**

---

## Physical Preparation

Getting your body ready matters.

### Conditioning Ahead

**Start early:**
- Don't wait until last week
- Build fitness gradually
- Sport-specific training
- General conditioning
- Consistent work

### What to Work On

**Key areas:**
- Cardiovascular fitness
- Strength
- Flexibility
- Sport-specific skills
- All components

---

## Sport-Specific Training

### Know the Demands

**Understand your sport:**
- What's tested at tryouts
- Key skills needed
- Physical requirements
- Common drills
- Prepare specifically

### Practice Skills

**Regular work:**
- Fundamental skills
- Weak areas especially
- Game situations
- Under pressure
- Consistent practice

---

## Injury Prevention

### Before Tryouts

**Protect yourself:**
- Address existing issues
- Don't overtrain
- Proper rest
- Gradual increases
- Listen to your body

### Common Risks

**Avoid:**
- Overuse injuries
- Trying to do too much
- Ignoring pain
- Skipping warm-up
- Pushing when hurt

### If Something Hurts

**What to do:**
- Rest it
- Ice if acute
- Tell parents/coaches
- Get evaluated if needed
- Don't make it worse

---

## Warm-Up Skills

### Before Tryouts

**Essential:**
- Arrive early enough
- Full warm-up
- Dynamic stretching
- Sport-specific movements
- Body ready to perform

### Warm-Up Routine

**Include:**
- Light jogging
- Dynamic movements
- Sport-specific drills
- Gradual intensity increase
- Fully prepared

---

## Posture and Presence

### Looking Athletic

**Body language:**
- Stand tall
- Confident posture
- Engaged and ready
- Eyes up
- Energy shows

### During Tryouts

**Present well:**
- Hustle always
- Positive attitude
- Good positioning
- Effort visible
- Coachable look

---

## Mental Preparation

### Managing Nerves

**It's normal:**
- Everyone's nervous
- Channel the energy
- Breathing techniques
- Focus on preparation
- You've got this

### Confidence Building

**Mental work:**
- Visualize success
- Remember your skills
- Positive self-talk
- Focus on process
- Not outcome

### Staying Focused

**During tryouts:**
- One play at a time
- Don't dwell on mistakes
- Reset quickly
- Present moment
- Control what you can

---

## Night Before

### Physical Rest

**Preparation:**
- Good sleep
- Don't overtrain
- Light activity okay
- Relaxation
- Body ready

### Practical Prep

**Have ready:**
- Equipment prepared
- Uniform/clothes
- Water and snacks
- Know where and when
- Nothing forgotten

---

## Tryout Day

### Morning Routine

**Set yourself up:**
- Good breakfast
- Allow extra time
- Calm preparation
- Positive mindset
- Ready to go

### Arrival

**When you get there:**
- Arrive early
- Warm up properly
- Check in
- Positive attitude
- Ready to show skills

---

## During Tryouts

### Show Your Best

**What coaches look for:**
- Skill level
- Coachability
- Attitude
- Effort
- Potential

### If You Make a Mistake

**Response:**
- Let it go
- Next play
- Stay positive
- Keep trying
- Recovery matters

### Throughout

**Maintain:**
- High energy
- Positive body language
- Hustle
- Encouragement to others
- Team player attitude

---

## After Tryouts

### Recovery

**Physical:**
- Cool down
- Stretch
- Hydrate
- Rest
- Take care of body

### Mental

**Emotional processing:**
- Did your best
- Outcome out of your control
- Learn from experience
- Whatever happens
- Growth regardless

---

## If You Don't Make It

### Healthy Response

**It happens:**
- Disappointment okay
- Not end of world
- Growth opportunity
- Other options exist
- Keep perspective

### Next Steps

**Moving forward:**
- Ask for feedback
- Keep developing
- Other sports/activities
- Try again next time
- Resilience building
