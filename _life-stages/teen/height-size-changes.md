# Dealing with Height and Size Changes as a Teen

> **Your Body Is Transforming:** Growing taller or changing shape affects how you move through the world. Adjusting takes time.

---

## What's Happening

### Growth Spurts

**During adolescence:**
- Rapid height changes
- Limbs grow at different rates
- Proportions shift
- Can happen quickly

### Body Composition

**Also changing:**
- Weight distribution
- Muscle development
- Shape changes
- Different for everyone

---

## How Growth Affects Posture

### Coordination Challenges

**During spurts:**
- May feel clumsy
- Coordination off
- Body awareness delayed
- Brain catching up to body

### Postural Changes

**Height affects:**
- How you fit in spaces
- Desk and chair fit
- How you see yourself
- How you carry yourself

### Growing Pains

**Physical discomfort:**
- Muscle and bone growth
- Temporary aches
- Usually not serious
- Part of growing

---

## If You're Tall

### Adjusting to Height

**Challenges:**
- May slouch to fit in
- Feel conspicuous
- Furniture doesn't fit
- Standing out literally

### Embracing Height

**Better approach:**
- Stand tall
- Height is an asset
- Good posture looks confident
- Own your height

### Practical Issues

**Managing:**
- Request appropriate desk/chair
- Adjust car seat properly
- Find clothes that fit
- Advocate for your needs

---

## If You're Short

### Adjusting to Size

**Challenges:**
- May stretch to reach
- Furniture too big
- Different perspective
- Comparison to peers

### Good Posture for Any Height

**Still important:**
- Stand tall at your height
- Good alignment
- Confident presence
- Posture matters at every height

### Practical Issues

**Managing:**
- Footrest for chairs
- Appropriate furniture
- Step stools when needed
- Comfortable adaptations

---

## Uneven Growth

### Limbs Before Torso

**Common pattern:**
- Arms and legs grow first
- Torso catches up later
- Temporary disproportion
- Feels awkward

### Adjusting

**Be patient:**
- It evens out
- Temporary phase
- Everyone goes through it
- Won't always feel this way

---

## Self-Consciousness

### Feeling Awkward

**Normal to:**
- Feel uncomfortable in body
- Notice others' bodies
- Compare yourself
- Want to blend in

### Working Through It

**Strategies:**
- Focus on what body can do
- Remember it's temporary
- Good posture helps confidence
- Everyone is adjusting

---

## Body Image

### Different Sizes Are Normal

**Truth:**
- Huge variation in normal
- Genes play biggest role
- Comparison isn't helpful
- Healthy comes in all sizes

### Media vs. Reality

**Remember:**
- Images are manipulated
- Real bodies vary widely
- Your body is unique
- That's okay

---

## Physical Activity

### During Growth

**Exercise benefits:**
- Supports bone health
- Builds coordination
- Helps body image
- Feels good

### Adjusting Sports

**May need:**
- Equipment changes
- Position changes
- Patience with coordination
- Continued practice

---

## Nutrition for Growing

### Supporting Growth

**Your body needs:**
- Adequate calories
- Protein
- Calcium
- Good nutrition overall

### Not Restricting

**Important:**
- Growing bodies need fuel
- Don't diet during growth
- Nourish your body
- Support development

---

## This Phase Passes

### It Gets Easier

**In time:**
- Growth slows
- Body stabilizes
- Coordination returns
- More comfortable

### Building Now

**Good habits:**
- Posture awareness now
- Physical activity
- Body acceptance
- Healthy relationship with body
