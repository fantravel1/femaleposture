# Exam Stress and Your Body

> **Your Body Feels Your Stress:** Exam pressure doesn't just affect your mindâ€”it shows up in your muscles, posture, and physical wellbeing.

---

## How Stress Affects Your Body

### Physical Symptoms

**During stressful times:**
- Tense shoulders
- Tight neck
- Headaches
- Jaw clenching
- Stomach upset
- Difficulty sleeping

### Posture Effects

**Stress posture:**
- Shoulders rise toward ears
- Hunching over
- Forward head
- Shallow breathing
- Clenched muscles

---

## The Study Slump

### Extended Study Sessions

**What happens:**
- Hours hunched over books
- Poor desk setup
- Forget to move
- Tension builds

### Physical Results

**Leading to:**
- Back pain
- Neck ache
- Fatigue
- Eye strain
- More stress

---

## Breaking the Cycle

### Movement Breaks

**Every 30 minutes:**
- Stand up
- Stretch
- Walk around
- Shake out tension

### Why It Helps

**Benefits:**
- Releases tension
- Clears mind
- Refreshes body
- Actually helps studying

---

## Quick Tension Releases

### Shoulder Shrugs

**Simple relief:**
- Raise shoulders to ears
- Hold 5 seconds
- Drop and relax
- Repeat 5 times

### Neck Stretches

**Gentle:**
- Ear toward shoulder
- Hold 30 seconds
- Both sides
- Don't force

### Hand and Wrist Stretch

**After writing/typing:**
- Stretch fingers
- Circle wrists
- Shake hands out
- Reduce cramping

### Jaw Release

**If clenching:**
- Open mouth wide
- Yawn
- Massage jaw
- Let jaw hang loose

---

## Breathing for Stress

### Deep Breathing

**Technique:**
- Breathe into belly
- Count of 4 in
- Count of 6 out
- Several breaths

### Why It Works

**Effects:**
- Activates calm system
- Releases physical tension
- Clears head
- Reduces anxiety

### When to Use

**Try:**
- Before studying
- During breaks
- Before tests
- When anxious

**Proper setup:**

## Sleep During Exam Time

### Often Sacrificed

**Common pattern:**
- Late night studying
- Poor sleep
- More stress
- Worse performance

### Why Sleep Matters

**Sleep helps:**
- Memory consolidation
- Stress recovery
- Clearer thinking
- Better performance

### Prioritizing Rest

**Even during exams:**
- Set bedtime
- Stop studying earlier
- Sleep > extra hour of study
- Quality over quantity
