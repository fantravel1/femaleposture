## Common Posture Issues

### Hypermobility

**Dancer challenge:**
- Many dancers hypermobile
- Flexibility without strength
- Joint instability risk
- Needs strength balance
- Control important

### Overarching

**Common problem:**
- Excessive lower back arch
- Rib flare
- Compensation patterns
- Core weakness often
- Address early

### Turnout Compensation

**Hip rotation:**
- Forcing turnout
- Feet turned more than hips
- Knee and ankle stress
- Work within ability
- Safe development

## Injury Prevention

### Common Injuries

**Watch for:**
- Hip pain
- Ankle and foot issues
- Back problems
- Stress fractures
- Overuse injuries

### Prevention Strategies

**What helps:**
- Proper warm-up
- Cool-down and stretching
- Adequate rest
- Good nutrition
- Listen to body

### When to Stop

**Don't push through:**
- Sharp pain
- Persistent discomfort
- Changing technique to avoid pain
- Swelling
- Seek help
