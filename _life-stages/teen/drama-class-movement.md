# Drama Class Movement and Posture

Drama and theater training naturally develops body awareness and postural control. Stage presence, character work, and movement exercises all build skills that transfer to everyday posture. Whether you're in drama class, theater club, or just interested in performance, these skills support lifelong physical awareness.

Acting requires conscious, purposeful movement.

### Observation Skills
