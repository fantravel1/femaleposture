# Bra Fitting and Posture for Teens

> **Support Matters:** Wearing the wrong bra size can affect your posture and comfort. Finding proper fit makes a difference.

---

## Why Bra Fit Matters

### Posture Connection

**Wrong fit affects:**
- Shoulder position
- Back strain
- How you stand
- Comfort throughout day

### Growing Bodies

**During development:**
- Size changes frequently
- Getting fitted matters
- Needs change over time
- Regular reassessment needed

---

## Signs of Poor Fit

### Too Small

**Problems:**
- Straps dig in
- Band rides up
- Tissue spilling out
- Uncomfortable

**Effect on posture:**
- Shoulder tension
- Restricted breathing
- General discomfort

### Too Large

**Problems:**
- No support
- Band rides up
- Straps fall
- Gapping

**Effect on posture:**
- No help for posture
- Adjusting constantly
- May slouch more

---

## Basics of Fit

### Band

**Should:**
- Be level around body
- Not ride up in back
- Be snug (but not tight)
- Provide most support

### Cups

**Should:**
- Contain all tissue
- No gapping
- No spillage
- Smooth fit

### Straps

**Should:**
- Stay in place
- Not dig in
- Not do all the work
- Be adjustable

### Center Gore

**Should:**
- Lie flat against chest
- Not float away
- Indicate proper fit

---

## Getting Fitted

### Professional Fitting

**Helpful because:**
- Expert assessment
- Try different styles
- Learn about fit
- Comfortable environment

**Where:**
- Department stores
- Specialty shops
- Some chain stores
- Ask about fitting services

### Measuring at Home

**Basic method:**
- Measure under bust (band size)
- Measure fullest part (cup calculation)
- Online guides available
- Starting point only

## First Bras

### When to Start

**Signs you might need one:**
- Development beginning
- Breast buds appearing
- Activity discomfort
- Personal preference

### Starter Options

**Good choices:**
- Sports bras
- Training bras
- Soft cup bras
- Comfortable, simple styles

---

## Different Activities

### Sports Bras

**For activity:**
- Extra support needed
- Reduces movement
- Affects posture during exercise
- Get properly fitted

**Features:**
- Compression or encapsulation
- No-bounce support
- Comfortable for movement
- Breathable fabric

### Everyday Bras

**For daily wear:**
- Comfortable for sitting
- Appropriate support
- Doesn't show through clothes
- Works for your activities

---

## Common Issues

### Strap Problems

**Digging in:**
- Usually means band is too big
- Band should support, not straps
- Try smaller band, larger cup

**Falling down:**
- Straps too long
- Band too big
- Wrong style for body

### Back Pain

**May indicate:**
- Wrong size
- Inadequate support
- Need professional fitting
- Time to reassess

### Slouching

**Connection:**
- Some hide development
- Posture suffers
- Proper fit helps confidence
- Support enables good posture

---

## Breast Development and Posture

### Growing Bodies

**What happens:**
- Weight distribution changes
- Center of gravity shifts
- May affect posture
- Muscles adapt

### Common Responses

**Some teens:**
- Slouch to hide
- Round shoulders forward
- Feel self-conscious
- Posture affects how they feel

### Building Confidence

**Helpful:**
- Proper support
- Good posture practice
- Self-acceptance
- Strength building

---

## Care and Replacement

### Bra Care

**Extend life:**
- Wash regularly
- Air dry when possible
- Rotate between bras
- Store properly

### When to Replace

**Signs:**
- Band stretched out
- Underwires poking
- Size changed
- Worn or damaged

### Growing Bodies

**Reassess regularly:**
- Size changes
- Every 6 months or so
- With growth spurts
- When fit changes

---

## Talking About It

### If Uncomfortable

**It's okay to:**
- Ask for help
- Talk to parent/guardian
- Ask store staff
- Seek information

### Body Positive Approach

**Remember:**
- Bodies are different
- All sizes normal
- Fit matters, not size
- Your body, your comfort
