## Upper Body Exercises

### Rows

**For upper back:**
- Band anchored at waist height
- Pull toward body
- Squeeze shoulder blades
- Major posture benefit
- Upper back strength

### Chest Press

**Front body:**
- Band behind back
- Press arms forward
- Chest engagement
- Balance with rows
- Controlled movement

### Shoulder Press

**Overhead strength:**
- Stand on band
- Press handles up
- Shoulder stability
- Core engaged
- Not too heavy

### Lat Pulldown

**Upper back:**
- Band overhead
- Pull down to sides
- Wide back muscles
- Posture support
- Common weakness

### Bicep Curls

**Arm strength:**
- Stand on band
- Curl handles up
- Elbow stable
- Controlled movement
- Functional strength

---

## Core Exercises

### Pallof Press

**Anti-rotation:**
- Band at chest height
- Press and hold away
- Resist rotation
- Core stability
- Excellent exercise

### Band-Assisted Dead Bug

**Core stability:**
- Band around feet
- Dead bug movement
- Added resistance
- Core engagement
- Challenging but accessible

### Woodchops

**Rotational:**
- Band anchored high or low
- Diagonal pulling
- Core rotation
- Functional movement
- Complete core

---

## Lower Body Exercises

### Squats

**Leg strength:**
- Stand on band
- Hold handles at shoulders
- Squat with resistance
- Bone loading
- Functional strength

### Glute Bridges

**Hip strength:**
- Loop band above knees
- Bridge up
- Press out against band
- Glute activation
- Important muscles

### Side Steps

**Hip abductors:**
- Loop band around ankles
- Step sideways
- Maintain tension
- Hip stability
- Balance support

### Leg Press Lying

**Quad work:**
- Band around foot
- Press leg away
- Controlled return
- Thigh strengthening
- Joint-friendly

---

## Posture-Specific Exercises

### Face Pulls

**Upper back:**
- Band at face height
- Pull apart toward face
- External rotation
- Posture muscles
- Excellent exercise

### Band Pull-Aparts

**Rhomboids:**
- Band at chest height
- Pull apart horizontally
- Shoulder blades squeeze
- Directly targets posture
- Essential inclusion

### Reverse Fly

**Upper back:**
- Hinge forward
- Pull band apart
- Upper back activation
- Counters forward posture
- Daily modern life antidote

## Sample Workout

### Full Body Routine

**Complete session:**
1. Warm-up (marching, arm circles)
2. Rows - 10-15 reps
3. Chest press - 10-15 reps
4. Squats - 10-15 reps
5. Glute bridges - 10-15 reps
6. Shoulder press - 10-15 reps
7. Face pulls - 10-15 reps
8. Core work - 30 seconds each
9. Cool-down stretching

### Frequency

**How often:**
- 2-3 times per week
- Rest days between
- Consistent schedule
- Sustainable practice
- Regular commitment
