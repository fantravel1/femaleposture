# Hormone Therapy and Posture

> **Hormone replacement therapy (HRT) can affect posture-related tissues including bones, muscles, and joints. Understanding these effects helps inform treatment decisions.**

---

## How Hormones Affect Posture

Estrogen influences many posture-related systems.

### Bone Health

**Estrogen's role:**
- Maintains bone density
- Prevents rapid bone loss
- Protects against osteoporosis
- Vertebral health
- Foundation for posture

### Muscle Tissue

**Effects:**
- Muscle mass maintenance
- Muscle strength
- Function preservation
- Sarcopenia prevention
- Postural muscle health

### Connective Tissue

**Collagen and more:**
- Ligament health
- Joint function
- Tissue elasticity
- Overall support
- Structural integrity

## HRT and Bone Health

### What Research Shows

**Evidence:**
- HRT preserves bone density
- Reduces fracture risk

- Protective effects
- Well-documented benefit

### Posture Implications

**How it helps:**
- Prevents vertebral fractures
- Maintains spinal structure
- Protects against kyphosis
- Bone health sustained
- Posture supported

### Systemic Therapy

**Full-body effects:**

- Posture-related benefits

### Local Therapy

**Targeted:**
- Vaginal estrogen
- Local effects only
- Different purpose
- Not for bone/muscle
- Specific use
