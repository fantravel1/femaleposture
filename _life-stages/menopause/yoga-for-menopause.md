# Yoga for Menopause

> **Your Practice Can Evolve:** Yoga offers particular benefits during menopause. Adapting your practice serves your changing body.

---

## Why Yoga for Menopause

### Benefits

**Yoga can help with:**
- Stress reduction
- Sleep improvement
- Flexibility maintenance
- Bone health (weight-bearing)
- Mood support
- Hot flash reduction
- Overall wellbeing

### Adaptability

**Yoga offers:**
- Modifications for any level
- Gentler as needed
- Various styles
- Mind-body connection

---

## Starting or Modifying Practice

### If New to Yoga

**Begin with:**
- Beginner-friendly classes
- Menopause-specific classes if available
- Gentle styles
- Listen to your body

### If Experienced

**Consider:**
- Modifying practice
- Different styles
- Less intensity when needed
- Evolving needs

---

## Poses for Common Symptoms

### For Hot Flashes

**Cooling poses:**
- Forward folds
- Supported bridge
- Legs up wall
- Restorative poses

**Avoid when overheating:**
- Hot yoga
- Intense flows
- Inversions (for some)

### For Sleep

**Calming practice:**
- Evening routine
- Gentle stretches
- Legs up wall
- Savasana
- Breathing exercises

### For Mood

**Balancing:**
- Heart openers (gentle)
- Standing poses
- Breathing practices
- Consistent practice

### For Joint Stiffness

**Helpful:**
- Gentle movements
- Cat-cow
- Hip openers
- Shoulder circles
- Neck stretches

---

## Recommended Poses

### Standing Poses

**For bone health:**
- Warrior variations (modified as needed)
- Tree pose
- Triangle
- Weight-bearing benefits

**Modifications:**
- Use wall for balance
- Shorter holds if needed
- Blocks for support

### Hip Openers

**For stiffness:**
- Pigeon (supported)
- Figure four
- Bound angle
- Wide-legged forward fold

**Approach:**
- Gentle entry
- Props for support
- Don't force
- Respect limits

### Restorative Poses

**For restoration:**
- Supported child's pose
- Supported reclining bound angle
- Legs up wall
- Supported savasana

**Benefits:**
- Deep relaxation
- Stress reduction
- Cooling
- Recovery

### Gentle Backbends

**Heart openers:**
- Supported bridge
- Gentle cobra
- Fish with props
- Avoid deep backbends if not ready

---

## Breathing Practices

### Cooling Breath

**Sitali:**
- Roll tongue (if possible)
- Inhale through tongue
- Exhale through nose
- Cooling effect

### Calming Breath

**Extended exhale:**
- Inhale count of 4
- Exhale count of 6-8
- Activates relaxation
- Before bed helpful

### General Awareness

**Breath focus:**
- Diaphragmatic breathing
- Slow, steady pace
- Calming effect
- Any time practice

## What to Modify

### Hot Yoga

**Consider:**
- May worsen hot flashes
- Dehydrating
- Not ideal for many
- If you love it, monitor response

### Intense Vinyasa

**Be aware:**
- May need modification
- Listen to energy
- Can be great or too much
- Adapt day by day

### Inversions

**Approach:**
- May affect hot flashes (varies)
- Modified versions fine
- Legs up wall good alternative
- Listen to your body

## Building Your Practice

### Consistency Matters

**Regular practice:**
- Even 10-15 minutes helps
- Daily better than occasional long
- Build routine
- Sustainable approach

### Home Practice

**Options:**
- Online classes
- Apps
- Videos
- Personal routine

### Classes

**Finding support:**
- Menopause-specific if available
- Senior-friendly
- Gentle levels
- Supportive teachers
