# Menopause, Intimacy, and Your Body

> **Changes Don't Mean End:** Menopause brings physical changes that can affect intimacy. Understanding and adapting helps maintain connection.

---

## Physical Changes

### What Happens

**During menopause:**
- Vaginal dryness
- Tissue changes
- Decreased lubrication
- Possible discomfort

### Why It Occurs

**Hormonal effects:**
- Estrogen decline
- Affects vaginal tissue
- Natural process
- Very common

---

## Body Comfort

### Physical Adaptations

**What may help:**
- Lubricants
- Moisturizers
- Time and patience
- Communication

### Medical Options

**If needed:**
- Vaginal estrogen (local)
- Other treatments
- Discuss with provider
- Options exist
