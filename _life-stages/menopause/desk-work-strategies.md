## Hot Flash Management

### Preparation

**Be ready:**
- Dress in layers
- Portable fan
- Cold water accessible
- Cooling towels
- Emergency supplies

### During Flash

**What helps:**
- Remove layers
- Use fan
- Cold water
- Step away if possible
- Breathe through it

### Workspace Setup

**Optimal environment:**
- Near ventilation
- Personal fan
- Thermostat access if possible
- Cooling options nearby
- Environment control
