# Menopause Hair and Skin Changes

> **Visible Changes:** Menopause affects hair and skin in ways you can see. Understanding why and what helps supports confidence.

---

## Why Changes Happen

### Hormone Effects

**Estrogen decline affects:**
- Collagen production
- Oil production
- Hair growth cycle
- Skin elasticity

### What to Expect

**Common changes:**
- Skin drier
- Less elastic
- Hair thinning
- Texture changes

---

## Skin Changes

### Dryness

**What happens:**
- Less oil produced
- Moisture decreases
- Can be uncomfortable
- More visible aging

### Elasticity

**Collagen decline:**
- Skin less firm
- More wrinkles
- Loss of plumpness
- Natural process

### Sensitivity

**May notice:**
- More reactive
- Irritation easier
- Different products needed
- Gentler care

---

## Hair Changes

### Thinning

**Common experience:**
- Overall thinning
- Less volume
- Visible scalp sometimes
- Can be distressing

### Texture Changes

**Hair may become:**
- Drier
- More brittle
- Different texture
- Harder to manage

### Other Hair Changes

**May notice:**
- Facial hair increase
- Body hair changes
- Color changes
- Various effects

---

## Self-Care Strategies

### For Skin

**Helpful:**
- Gentle, hydrating products

- Moisturize regularly
- Gentle cleansers

### For Hair

**May help:**
- Gentle handling
- Volumizing products
- Good nutrition
- Stress management
