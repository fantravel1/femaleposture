## Why Pilates for Menopause

Pilates addresses key menopause concerns.

### Core Strength Focus

**Foundation:**
- Core is foundation of posture
- Supports spine
- Reduces back pain
- Functional strength
- Priority at this stage

### Posture Improvement

**Alignment benefits:**
- Awareness of alignment
- Strengthens postural muscles
- Counters slumping tendency
- Confidence and presence
- Long-term health

### Low Impact

**Joint friendly:**
- Controlled movements
- No jarring
- Safe for joints
- Appropriate for stiffness
- Accessible with modifications

### Beginner Friendly

**Starting out:**
- Beginner or all-levels class

## Menopause Modifications

### Hot Flashes

**In class:**
- Dress in layers
- Cooling towel helpful
- Fan yourself as needed
- Stay hydrated
- Class usually cooler than cardio

### Joint Stiffness

**Adapting:**
- Modified positions available
- Props for support
- Don't force range
- Instructor can help
- Honor your body

### Energy Fluctuations

**Working with:**
- Modify intensity as needed
- Rest when required
- Some movement better than none
- Gentle options available
- Listen to yourself
