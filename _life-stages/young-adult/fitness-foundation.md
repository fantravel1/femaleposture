# Building Your Fitness Foundation in Your 20s

> **Now Is the Time:** The fitness habits you build now set the stage for decades of health. Your body is forgiving nowâ€”it won't always be.

---

## Why Your 20s Matter

### The Opportunity

**This decade:**
- Bodies recover quickly
- Habits form easily
- Time often available
- Foundation can be built

### The Reality Check

**If you don't start now:**
- Harder later
- Fewer patterns to build on
- Playing catch-up
- Missing the window

---

## What to Include

### Strength Training

**Essential because:**
- Builds bone density
- Preserves muscle
- Supports posture
- Boosts metabolism

**Getting started:**
- 2-3 times per week
- Learn proper form
- Start light
- Progress gradually

### Cardiovascular Exercise

**Benefits:**
- Heart health
- Stress management
- Energy
- Weight management

**Options:**
- Walking
- Running
- Cycling
- Swimming
- Dancing
- What you enjoy

### Flexibility and Mobility

**Often neglected:**
- Maintains range of motion
- Prevents injury
- Supports good posture
- Do regularly

### Core Work

**Foundation:**
- Supports spine
- Enables other activities
- Posture essential
- See [Core Training](/tools/core-training-progression/)

---

## Starting from Zero

### If You've Never Exercised

**Begin with:**
- Walking
- Basic bodyweight exercises
- Gradual progression
- Don't overdo it initially

### Building Up

**Progression:**
- Add slowly
- Learn form before intensity
- Build consistency first
- Then add challenge

### Finding What Works

**Experiment:**
- Try different activities
- Notice what you enjoy
- What fits your schedule
- What you'll actually do

---

## Making It Stick

### Consistency Over Intensity

**The key:**
- Regular moderate exercise beats occasional intense
- Build the habit
- Then increase
- Sustainable approach

### Scheduling

**Make it non-negotiable:**
- Put in calendar
- Same times if possible
- Treat as appointment
- Prioritize

### Removing Barriers

**Set yourself up:**
- Gym close by/at home equipment
- Clothes ready
- Workout planned
- Reduce friction

---

## Posture-Specific Fitness

### What Matters for Posture

**Focus on:**
- Core strength
- Back muscles
- Shoulder stability
- Hip strength
- Balance

### Common Imbalances

**Address:**
- Weak posterior chain (back side)
- Tight hip flexors
- Weak core
- Rounded shoulders

### Exercises That Help

**Include:**
- Rows and pulls
- Hip bridges
- Planks
- Squats
- Shoulder exercises

---

## Avoiding Common Mistakes

### Going Too Hard

**Problems:**
- Injury
- Burnout
- Inconsistency
- Giving up

**Instead:**
- Start moderate
- Build gradually
- Sustainable approach

### Ignoring Form

**Why it matters:**
- Prevents injury
- Better results
- Builds right patterns
- Worth learning

### All Cardio, No Strength

**Balance needed:**
- Strength often neglected
- But crucial for posture
- For bone health
- For metabolism

### Skipping Recovery

**Rest matters:**
- Muscles grow during rest
- Prevents overtraining
- Sustainable practice
- Part of the plan
