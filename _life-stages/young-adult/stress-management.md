# Stress Management and Posture in Your 20s

> **Your Body Carries Your Stress:** The habits you build now for managing stress will affect your physical health for decades. Your body is listening.

---

## Stress in Young Adulthood

### Common Stressors

**This life stage:**
- Career building
- Financial pressure
- Relationship navigation
- Life decisions
- Identity formation
- Comparison culture

### Why Now Matters

**Critical period:**
- Habits are forming
- Patterns establishing
- Body still adaptable
- Building foundation

---

## How Stress Affects Your Body

### Physical Manifestations

**What happens:**
- Muscle tension (shoulders, jaw)
- Shallow breathing
- Clenched posture
- Pain and discomfort
- Sleep disruption

### Postural Effects

**Stress posture:**
- Shoulders rise toward ears
- Forward head
- Rounded upper back
- Jaw clenching
- Breath holding

---

## The Stress-Posture Cycle

### How It Works

**Cycle:**
- Stress → tense muscles
- Tense muscles → poor posture
- Poor posture → pain
- Pain → more stress

### Breaking the Cycle

**Interventions:**
- Address stress
- Release tension
- Improve posture
- Multiple entry points

---

## Physical Stress Relief

### Movement

**Why essential:**
- Releases tension
- Burns stress hormones
- Improves mood
- Resets body

**What helps:**
- Any exercise you'll do
- Walking
- Running
- Dance
- Team sports
- Yoga

### Stretching

**For tension:**
- Neck and shoulder rolls
- Chest openers
- Hip stretches
- Full body releases

### Breathing

**Quick reset:**
- Deep breathing
- Box breathing
- Physiological sigh
- Activates relaxation response

## Stress and Sleep

### The Connection

**Bidirectional:**
- Stress → poor sleep

### Better Sleep

**For stress management:**

- Sleep hygiene
