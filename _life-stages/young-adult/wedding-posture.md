# Wedding Day Posture

> **Your wedding day means hours of photos and attention on you. Good posture enhances how you look in your dress, in photos, and helps you manage the physical demands of a long, memorable day.**

---

## Why Posture Matters

Your wedding is highly documented.

### The Photography Factor

**Consider:**
- Hundreds of photos
- Video footage
- Professional and guest photos
- Memories forever
- Posture shows in all

### All-Day Event

**Physical demands:**
- Long day
- On your feet
- Social interactions
- Multiple events
- Stamina required

---

## Dress Considerations

### How Dress Affects Posture

**Structure matters:**
- Bodice design
- Weight of dress
- Train management
- Movement limitations
- Fit affects comfort

### Different Styles

**Posture needs:**
- Ball gown: upright for structure
- Mermaid: core engagement
- A-line: balanced stance
- Backless: extra awareness
- Style-specific

### Training to Train

**Practice wearing:**
- Wear dress before wedding
- Move in it
- Sit in it
- Practice posture
- No surprises

---

## Photo Posture Basics

### Standing Poses

**How to stand:**
- Weight on back foot
- Front foot forward
- Shoulders back and down
- Chin slightly lifted
- Elongated neck

### Avoiding Common Mistakes

**What not to do:**
- Slouching
- Rounded shoulders
- Double chin from head position
- Tense shoulders
- Uncomfortable positioning

### Photographer Guidance

**Work together:**
- Good photographers direct
- Follow their cues
- Ask for feedback
- Practice poses beforehand
- Trust the professional

## Specific Photo Situations

### Couple Portraits

**With partner:**
- Angled toward each other
- Natural positioning
- Relaxed but aligned
- Connection visible
- Genuine moments

### Group Photos

**Family and friends:**
- Maintain your posture
- Don't compress for others
- Shoulders back
- Stand tall
- Good position in frame

### Candids

**Natural moments:**
- General awareness
- Natural good posture
- Not forced
- Genuine expressions
- Habitual alignment helps

### Ceremony
