## Quick Setup Routine

### Arriving Efficiently

**Steps:**
- Adjust chair first
- Set up laptop stand
- Position equipment
- Quick ergonomic check
- Ready to work

### Chair Adjustment

**Every time:**
- Height for feet flat
- Back support positioned
- Armrests if available
- Quick adjustments
- Don't skip

### Laptop Positioning

**With stand:**
- Screen at eye level
- Keyboard separate
- Mouse beside
- Proper angles
- Worth the setup
