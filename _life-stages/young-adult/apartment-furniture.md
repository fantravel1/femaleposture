## The Apartment Challenge

Small spaces create posture problems.

### Common Issues

**Young adult reality:**
- Budget constraints
- Small spaces
- Multi-purpose rooms
- Inherited furniture
- Limited options

### Why It Matters

**Furniture affects:**
- How you sit daily
- Work-from-home posture
- Sleep quality
- Overall comfort
- Long-term health

---

## Priority Purchases

### Where to Invest

**Most important:**
- Mattress (sleep matters)
- Desk chair (if working from home)
- Desk or work surface
- These affect hours of your day
- Worth spending more

### Where to Save

**Less critical:**
- Decorative items
- Accent furniture
- Can upgrade later
- Secondhand acceptable
- Budget friendly

---

## Desk Setup

### Small Space Solutions

**Options:**
- Wall-mounted desk
- Narrow desk
- Convertible furniture
- Dining table double-use
- Creative solutions

### Chair Selection

**Even in small spaces:**
- Adjustable height essential
- Back support needed
- Armrests helpful
- Wheels optional
- Worth investing

### Budget Chair Options

**Affordable:**
- IKEA basics with cushion
- Secondhand office chairs
- Task chairs
- Add lumbar support
- Improve what you have

---

## Living Room Seating

### Couch Considerations

**Choosing:**
- Seat depth matters
- Back support
- Height appropriate
- Not too soft
- Comfort and support

### Making Bad Couches Work

**If you have:**
- Add lumbar pillow
- Cushions for support
- Don't live on it
- Limit sitting time
- Compensate

### Floor Seating

**If using:**
- Cushions for support
- Back against wall
- Change positions
- Not for long periods
- Occasional use

---

## Bedroom Setup

### Mattress Priority

**Essential investment:**
- Affects sleep quality
- Affects posture recovery
- Worth spending
- Research options
- Many online options now

### Budget Mattress

**If tight on budget:**
- Mattress toppers help
- Even foam works
- Firm enough support
- Not too old
- Something decent

### Pillow Importance

**Don't overlook:**
- Neck alignment
- Sleep quality
- Replace regularly
- Affordable impact
- Simple upgrade

---

## Multi-Purpose Spaces

### Living/Working

**When same room:**
- Designated work area
- Proper work setup
- Switch contexts
- Don't work from couch always
- Boundaries help

### Bedroom as Office

**If necessary:**
- Separate the functions
- Real desk if possible
- Not bed as desk
- Boundaries
- Mental and physical
