# Moving Day Posture: Safe Lifting and Carrying

> **Don't Start in a New Place with a Bad Back:** Moving is physically demanding. Smart technique prevents injuries that could last long after you've unpacked.

---

## Moving Day Risks

### What Makes It Dangerous

**Moving involves:**
- Lots of lifting
- Awkward loads
- Stairs often
- Fatigue as day goes on
- Rushing
- Heavy furniture

### Common Injuries

**What happens:**
- Back strains
- Pulled muscles
- Dropped items on feet
- Exhaustion injuries

---

## Packing Smart

### Box Weight

**Critical:**
- Heavy items in small boxes
- Light items in big boxes
- No overly heavy boxes
- Test before carrying

### Distribution

**Inside boxes:**
- Heavy items on bottom
- Pad fragile items
- Don't overfill
- Balanced loads

### Labeling

**Helps posture:**
- Know what's heavy
- Plan carrying strategy
- Prioritize placement
- Less surprise heavy boxes

---

## Lifting Boxes

### Every Single Time

**Proper technique:**
- Squat down
- Keep back straight
- Grip firmly
- Lift with legs
- Keep close to body

### Common Mistakes

**Avoid:**
- Bending at waist
- Twisting while lifting
- Reaching too far
- Jerky movements

### Testing Weight

**Before full lift:**
- Tilt box to test
- Check weight
- Get help if heavy
- Don't be a hero

---

## Carrying

### Posture While Carrying

**Maintain:**
- Box close to body
- Core engaged
- Upright posture
- Balanced load

### Vision

**See where going:**
- Don't stack too high
- Clear sightlines
- Know the path
- Watch for obstacles

### Stairs

**Extra caution:**
- See steps
- Hold rail when possible
- Lighter loads
- Take your time

---

## Furniture

### Heavy Items

**Always get help:**
- Couches
- Mattresses
- Dressers
- Refrigerators
- Anything heavy

### Furniture Dollies

**Use equipment:**
- Dolly for heavy items
- Sliders for furniture
- Moving straps
- Worth the rental

### Awkward Shapes

**Technique:**
- Communicate with helper
- Plan the move
- Clear the path
- Go slowly

---

## Throughout the Day

### Pace Yourself

**Energy management:**
- Take breaks
- Stay hydrated
- Eat regularly
- Don't push through exhaustion

### Stretching

**Between loads:**
- Back stretches
- Hamstring stretches
- Shoulder rolls
- Keep loose

### As Fatigue Sets In

**Later in day:**
- Form deteriorates
- More risk
- Lighten loads
- Take more breaks
- Ask for help

---

## Hiring Movers

### When to Consider

**Worth it if:**
- Heavy furniture
- Many stairs
- Long distance
- Can afford it
- Protecting your body

### Even with Movers

**You still:**
- Pack boxes (use good technique)
- Direct traffic
- Handle fragile items
- Move smaller things

---

## Unpacking

### Setting Up

**Don't forget:**
- Still use good form
- Lifting into spaces
- Bending to unpack
- Fatigue from moving day

### Furniture Placement

**When arranging:**
- Use sliders
- Push don't pull
- Get help for heavy pieces
- Don't rush

---

## After the Move

### Recovery

**Give your body:**
- Rest
- Stretching
- Light movement
- Time to recover

### If Pain Develops

**Take seriously:**
- Don't ignore back pain
- Ice if acute
- Rest
- Seek help if severe

---

## Moving Tips Summary

### Do

**Smart moving:**
- Pack smart
- Lift with legs
- Use equipment
- Take breaks
- Get help

### Don't

**Avoid:**
- Heavy single trips
- Bending at waist
- Twisting with loads
- Rushing
- Being a hero
