# Starting Your Career: Posture Habits for Your 20s

> **Build Now, Benefit Forever:** Your 20s are when career habits form. The posture patterns you develop now will affect you for decades. Start smart.

---

## Why Your 20s Matter

### Habits Form Now

**This is when:**
- Work patterns establish
- Bodies still adapt well
- Prevention is powerful
- Foundation is set

### The Long Game

**Think ahead:**
- Career is 40+ years
- Body needs to last
- Problems compound
- Prevention beats treatment

---

## The New Worker Challenge

### First Desk Job

**Common experience:**
- First full-time sitting
- 8+ hours at desk
- Suboptimal setup often
- Don't know what you don't know

### What Happens

**Without attention:**
- Posture degrades quickly
- Pain starts within months
- Habits form fast
- Become "normal"

---

## Setting Up Right

### Your Workstation

**Essential setup:**
- Monitor at eye level
- Keyboard at elbow height
- Chair properly adjusted
- Feet flat or on footrest
- See [Desk Work Posture](/activities/desk-work/)

### Advocating for Yourself

**Don't be shy:**
- Request ergonomic equipment
- Ask about setup
- Most employers accommodate
- It's reasonable

---

## Building Good Habits Early

### Movement Breaks

**Establish now:**
- Stand every 30 minutes
- Walk to colleague instead of email
- Take real lunch breaks
- Move throughout day

### Posture Awareness

**Build awareness:**
- Notice when you slouch
- Adjust regularly
- Set reminders initially
- Becomes automatic

### Exercise Routine

**Non-negotiable:**
- Regular exercise
- Counteracts sitting
- Stress management
- Long-term health
