Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer personal consultations?

No, FemalePosture.com is an educational resource only. We don't provide personal medical advice or consultations. For individual guidance, please consult a qualified healthcare provider such as a physical therapist.

Can I use your content?

Our content is copyrighted. Please contact us for licensing inquiries. Brief quotes with attribution and a link back are generally acceptable for educational purposes.

How can I stay updated?

Bookmark our site and check back regularly for new content. We're continuously adding new guides, exercises, and resources.

I found an exercise that hurt me. What should I do?

Stop the exercise immediately and consult a healthcare provider. Our exercises are designed for general audiences, but everyone's body is different. Pain is a signal to stop and seek professional guidance.
