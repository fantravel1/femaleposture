# Weekly Posture Improvement Plan

> **Consistency Is Key:** Improving posture takes regular practice. This weekly plan provides structure to build lasting habits.

---

## How to Use This Plan

### The Structure

- Daily exercises and stretches
- Specific focus areas each day
- Built-in rest and variety
- Progressive over time

### Time Commitment

- Morning routine: 5-10 minutes
- Throughout day: Brief breaks
- Evening: 5-10 minutes
- Total: 20-30 minutes daily (spread out)

### Customization

- Start with what you can do
- Add as habits form
- Focus on your problem areas
- Adjust timing to your schedule

---

## Monday: Upper Back Focus

### Morning (5-10 min)

1. **Wall angels** — 10 reps
2. **Chin tucks** — 15 reps
3. **Chest opener stretch** — 30 sec

### During Day

- 3 posture check-ins (set reminders)
- Brief desk stretches every hour
- Walk for at least 10 minutes

### Evening (5-10 min)

1. **Thoracic extension** (foam roller or chair) — 2 min
2. **Thread the needle** — 30 sec each side
3. **Upper trap stretch** — 30 sec each side
4. **Child's pose** — 1 min

---

## Tuesday: Core Focus

### Morning (5-10 min)

1. **Dead bug** — 10 reps each side
2. **Bird-dog** — 10 reps each side
3. **Pelvic tilts** — 15 reps

### During Day

- Practice abdominal bracing while sitting
- 3 posture check-ins
- Walk breaks

### Evening (5-10 min)

1. **Plank** (modified okay) — 3 x 20-30 sec
2. **Glute bridges** — 15 reps
3. **Knee-to-chest stretch** — 30 sec each
4. **Cat-cow** — 10 reps

---

## Wednesday: Lower Body Focus

### Morning (5-10 min)

1. **Hip flexor stretch** — 30 sec each side
2. **Glute bridges** — 15 reps
3. **Clamshells** — 15 each side

### During Day

- Stand up every 30 minutes
- 3 posture check-ins
- Take stairs if possible

### Evening (5-10 min)

1. **Pigeon pose or figure-four stretch** — 45 sec each
2. **Hamstring stretch** — 30 sec each
3. **Quadriceps stretch** — 30 sec each
4. **Calf stretch** — 30 sec each

---

## Thursday: Upper Back + Neck

### Morning (5-10 min)

1. **Chin tucks** — 15 reps
2. **Shoulder blade squeezes** — 15 reps
3. **Doorway stretch** — 30 sec each arm position

### During Day

- Neck stretches every 2 hours
- 3 posture check-ins
- Walk for 10+ minutes

### Evening (5-10 min)

1. **Rows** (with band or weights) — 15 reps
2. **Wall angels** — 10 reps
3. **Levator scapulae stretch** — 30 sec each
4. **Foam roller chest opening** — 2 min

---

## Friday: Full Body Integration

### Morning (5-10 min)

1. **Cat-cow** — 10 reps
2. **Bird-dog** — 8 each side
3. **Chest opener** — 30 sec
4. **Hip circles** — 10 each direction

### During Day

- Practice good posture in all activities
- 3 posture check-ins
- End-of-week reflection: What went well?

### Evening (5-10 min)

1. **Plank** — 2 x 30 sec
2. **Squats** (bodyweight) — 15 reps
3. **Standing back extension** — 10 reps
4. **Full body stretch sequence** — 5 min

---

## Saturday: Active Recovery

### Morning

- Longer walk (20-30 min) or
- Gentle yoga/stretching class
- Swimming or other enjoyable activity

### Focus

- Enjoy movement
- No structured exercises required
- Stay active but gentle

### If You Want to Practice

- Gentle stretching only
- Focus on tight areas from week
- Nothing strenuous

---

## Sunday: Rest and Reset

### Light Activity Only

- Gentle walking
- Easy stretching if desired
- Rest your body

### Preparation

- Plan next week's sessions
- Identify any problem areas to focus on
- Set up reminders for posture check-ins

### Reflection

- How did you feel this week?
- What exercises helped most?
- What was challenging?
- Adjustments for next week?

---

## Posture Check-In Guide

### What to Check (3x Daily)

1. **Head position:** Ears over shoulders?
2. **Shoulders:** Rolled back, not rounded?
3. **Sitting:** Supported with good alignment?
4. **Standing:** Weight even, not slumped?

### Quick Correction

1. Stand tall (string pulling you up)
2. Chin tuck gently
3. Roll shoulders back
4. Engage core lightly
5. Even weight on feet

---

## Progression Over Time

### Weeks 1-2: Foundation

- Focus on learning exercises
- Build consistency
- Start with easier versions

### Weeks 3-4: Building

- Add repetitions or duration
- Improve form
- Notice changes

### Month 2+: Advancing

- Increase difficulty
- Add resistance if appropriate
- Exercises become more automatic

### Ongoing

- Maintain gains
- Adjust for new challenges
- Listen to your body

---

## Tips for Success

### Making It Stick

- Same time each day
- Link to existing habits
- Start small and build
- Track your practice

### Common Obstacles

**"No time":**
- Even 5 minutes helps
- Split throughout day
- Do something rather than nothing

**"I forget":**
- Phone reminders
- Leave yoga mat out
- Link to morning routine

**"I don't see results":**
- Posture change takes time
- Take photos monthly
- Notice how you feel

## Customizing for Your Needs

### For Forward Head Posture Focus

Add extra:
- Chin tucks
- Deep neck flexor exercises
- Upper trap stretches

### For Lower Back Pain

Add extra:
- Core exercises
- Hip flexor stretches
- Glute strengthening

### For Rounded Shoulders

Add extra:
- Rows and pull-aparts
- Chest stretches
- Wall angels
