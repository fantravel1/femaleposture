# Posture Reminders: Building Awareness

> **Remember to Check In:** The biggest challenge with posture is remembering to pay attention. Here's how to build reminders into your day.

---

## Why Reminders Matter

### The Problem

- We forget about posture when focused
- Poor posture becomes default
- No natural feedback loop
- Hours pass in bad positions

### The Solution

- External reminders
- Linked habits
- Environmental cues
- Building automatic awareness

---

## Reminder Strategies

### Phone Timers

- Set hourly reminders
- Use posture reminder apps
- Gentle notification sounds
- Effective but can be ignored

### Environmental Cues

- Sticky notes at workstation
- Mirror check points
- Colored dots on monitor
- Physical reminders in space

### Habit Stacking

Link posture checks to:
- Every email send
- Bathroom breaks
- Getting water
- Phone calls
- Meetings ending

### Wearable Devices

- Posture sensors (Upright, Lumo)
- Vibration reminders
- Immediate feedback
- Can be effective

---

## Quick Posture Check

**When reminder goes off:**
1. Ears over shoulders?
2. Shoulders back and relaxed?
3. Core engaged lightly?
4. If sitting: back supported?
5. Correct and continue

---

## Making It Stick

### Start Simple

- One reminder type
- Few times per day
- Build gradually

### Don't Overdo It

- Too many = ignored
- Find sustainable frequency
- Quality over quantity

### Notice the Benefit

- Pay attention to how you feel
- Positive reinforcement
- Connect correction to relief

---

## Sample Reminder Schedule

**Morning:** 9am, 10am, 11am
**Afternoon:** 1pm, 2pm, 3pm, 4pm
**Evening:** Once before bed
