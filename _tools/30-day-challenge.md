## Before You Start

### Take a Starting Photo

- Side-view photo in natural standing posture
- Have someone else take it
- Don't "pose"â€”stand as you normally would
- Save for comparison at day 30

### Do a Self-Assessment

Answer honestly:
1. Do I have forward head posture?
2. Are my shoulders rounded?
3. Does my lower back arch excessively or flatten?
4. Where do I feel tension or pain?

Record your answers.

## After the Challenge

### Maintenance Routine

Continue with:

- Strength circuit 3x/week (10 min)
- Stretches as needed
- Posture awareness throughout day

### If You Miss Days

No guilt. Just restart. One missed day doesn't erase progress.

- Add more challenging exercises
- Work on specific issues
- Consider yoga or Pilates
