# Stretching Routine Builder

> **Make It Yours:** A routine that fits your life gets done. Use this guide to create a stretching practice tailored to your time, needs, and goals.

---

## How to Use This Guide

1. Determine your available time
2. Identify your problem areas
3. Select stretches from each category
4. Build your routine
5. Practice consistently

---

## Time Templates

### 5-Minute Quick Routine
- 3-4 stretches
- 30-45 seconds each
- Hit major areas only

### 10-Minute Daily Routine
- 6-8 stretches
- 30-60 seconds each
- Cover most problem areas

### 15-Minute Thorough Routine
- 8-12 stretches
- 30-60 seconds each
- Full body coverage

### 20+ Minute Deep Session
- Complete routine
- Longer holds (60-90 seconds)
- Multiple stretches per area

## Stretches by Area

### NECK (Choose 1-2)

**Neck Tilt**
- Ear toward shoulder
- 30 seconds each side
- *Best for: Side neck tightness*

**Neck Rotation**
- Turn head to look over shoulder
- 20-30 seconds each side
- *Best for: Rotation limitation*

**Chin Tuck**
- Pull chin back (double chin)
- Hold 5 seconds, repeat 10x
- *Best for: Forward head posture*

**Levator Scapulae Stretch**
- Look into armpit
- Gentle pressure with hand
- 30 seconds each side
- *Best for: Upper trap/neck junction*

---

### SHOULDERS/CHEST (Choose 1-3)

**Doorway Stretch**
- Forearm on doorframe
- Step through
- 30-45 seconds each side
- *Best for: Chest tightness*

**Cross-Body Shoulder Stretch**
- Arm across body
- Pull with other hand
- 30 seconds each side
- *Best for: Posterior shoulder*

**Behind Back Clasp**
- Hands behind back
- Squeeze shoulder blades
- 30 seconds
- *Best for: Chest and front shoulder*

**Wall Chest Stretch**
- Hand on wall at shoulder height
- Turn away from wall
- 30 seconds each side
- *Best for: Deep pec stretch*

**Thread the Needle**
- On hands and knees
- Reach arm under body
- 30 seconds each side
- *Best for: Upper back and shoulder*

---

### UPPER BACK (Choose 1-2)

**Cat-Cow**
- On hands and knees
- Alternate arching and rounding
- 10 cycles
- *Best for: Spinal mobility*

**Seated Rotation**
- Sit tall, cross arms
- Rotate torso each direction
- 30 seconds each side
- *Best for: Thoracic rotation*

**Child's Pose**
- Kneel, reach arms forward
- Sit back on heels
- 30-60 seconds
- *Best for: Overall back release*

**Prone Extension**
- Lie face down
- Press up on hands, keeping hips down
- 30 seconds
- *Best for: Extension mobility*

---

### LOWER BACK (Choose 1-2)

**Knee to Chest**
- Lie on back
- Pull knee to chest
- 30 seconds each side
- *Best for: Lower back release*

**Double Knee to Chest**
- Both knees to chest
- Rock gently side to side
- 30-60 seconds
- *Best for: General low back*

**Supine Twist**
- Lie on back
- Drop knees to one side
- 30-45 seconds each side
- *Best for: Rotational release*

**Cat-Cow**
- (Also good for upper back)
- Focus on lower back movement
- 10 cycles

---

### HIPS (Choose 2-3)

**Hip Flexor Stretch (Kneeling)**
- Kneeling lunge position
- Push hips forward
- 45-60 seconds each side
- *Best for: Front of hip*

**Pigeon Pose (or Figure-4)**
- Hip external rotation stretch
- 45-60 seconds each side
- *Best for: Outer hip/glute*

**Butterfly Stretch**
- Soles of feet together
- Let knees drop
- 30-60 seconds
- *Best for: Inner thigh/groin*

**90-90 Stretch**
- Both legs at 90 degrees
- Lean forward over front leg
- 30-45 seconds each side
- *Best for: Hip rotation*

**Frog Stretch**
- On hands and knees
- Knees wide apart
- Rock back gently
- 30-60 seconds
- *Best for: Deep inner hip*

---

### HAMSTRINGS/LEGS (Choose 1-2)

**Standing Hamstring Stretch**
- Foot on low surface
- Lean forward with flat back
- 30-45 seconds each side
- *Best for: Back of thigh*

**Lying Hamstring Stretch**
- On back, leg toward ceiling
- Use strap or hands
- 30-45 seconds each side
- *Best for: Controlled hamstring*

**Forward Fold (Seated)**
- Legs straight, reach forward
- 30-60 seconds
- *Best for: Hamstrings and back*

**Quad Stretch (Standing)**
- Pull heel toward butt
- 30 seconds each side
- *Best for: Front of thigh*

**Calf Stretch (Wall)**
- Hands on wall, one leg back
- Press heel down
- 30 seconds each side
- *Best for: Lower leg*

## Tips for Success

### Consistency Over Intensity
- Daily short sessions beat weekly long ones
- Show up, even if briefly
- Build the habit first

### Proper Technique
- Breathe normally
- Hold steady, don't bounce
- Stop at stretch, not pain
- Relax into position

### Progression
- Gradually increase hold times
- Add stretches as time allows
- Reassess every 4-6 weeks
