---

## URL Structure Guidelines

```
Pattern: https://femaleposture.com/{hub}/{category}/{page-slug}/

Examples:
https://femaleposture.com/conditions/forward-head-posture/complete-guide/
https://femaleposture.com/life-stages/pregnancy/first-trimester/
https://femaleposture.com/exercises/chin-tucks/
https://femaleposture.com/es/condiciones/cabeza-adelantada/
```
