How to Use This Library

1. Start with Your Assessment

Take our posture self-assessment or wall test to identify your specific patterns.

2. Pick 3-5 Key Exercises

Don't try to do everything. Focus on exercises that target your specific issues. Consistency with a few exercises beats occasional work on many.

3. Practice Daily

Most posture exercises can be done daily. 10-15 minutes of focused practice creates more change than occasional longer sessions.

4. Reassess Monthly

Check your progress using the same assessments. Adjust your program as you improve.
