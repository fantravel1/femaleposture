# Dining Out Posture

Dining out involves extended sitting. Proper posture supports comfortable, confident meals.

## Restaurant Seating

### Choosing Seats

**Seating options:**
- Booth vs chairs
- Table height matters
- Comfortable choice
- Good positioning
- Request preferences

### Booth Sliding

**Getting in:**
- Slide in carefully
- Position comfortably
- Upright posture
- Accessible to table
- Comfortable distance

## Seated Position

### At Table

**Dining posture:**
- Sit upright
- Shoulders relaxed
- Close to table
- Arms comfortable
- Engaged position

### Table Height

**Adjusting:**
- Table at comfortable level
- Forearms can rest
- Not reaching up
- Comfortable eating
- Natural position

## During Meal

### Eating Form

**Dining posture:**
- Sit up straight
- Lean slightly forward
- Bring food to mouth
- Don't hunch over plate
- Confident dining

### Long Meals

**Extended dining:**
- Shift positions slightly
- Maintain core engagement
- Enjoy conversation
- Take breaks
- Comfortable duration

## Social Aspect

### Conversation Position

**Engaging others:**
- Face companions
- Open posture
- Eye contact
- Relaxed shoulders
- Warm presence

## Finishing Up

### End of Meal

**Wrapping up:**
- Push back slightly
- Relaxed finish
- Natural conversation
- Comfortable wait
- Unhurried exit

Good dining out posture supports confident, comfortable restaurant experiences.
