# Kickboxing Posture

Kickboxing combines punching and kicking. Proper posture enables powerful, safe technique.

## Fighting Stance

### Basic Position

**Guard stance:**
- Staggered feet
- Knees bent
- Hands up
- Chin down
- Core engaged

### Weight Distribution

**Ready to move:**
- Balanced stance
- Light on feet
- Ready to strike
- Ready to move
- Stable base

## Punches

### Jab

**Lead hand:**
- Straight punch
- Shoulder rotation
- Hip follows
- Quick return
- Guard maintained

### Cross

**Rear hand:**
- Powerful straight
- Full hip rotation
- Shoulder drive
- Pivot back foot
- Core engaged

### Hooks and Uppercuts

**Power punches:**
- Body rotation
- Elbow angle
- Hip power
- Core stability
- Quick recovery

## Kicks

### Front Kick

**Pushing kick:**
- Chamber knee
- Thrust with hip
- Foot position
- Rechamer
- Balance recovery

### Roundhouse Kick

**Rotational kick:**
- Pivot support foot
- Hip rotation
- Shin connection
- Follow through
- Quick recovery

### Side Kick

**Lateral power:**
- Chamber position
- Hip extension
- Heel connection
- Core stability
- Controlled recovery

## Fitness Context

### Class Setting

**Workout focus:**
- Form priority
- Cardio benefit
- Stress release
- Full body workout
- Empowering exercise

Good kickboxing posture creates powerful, safe striking movements.
