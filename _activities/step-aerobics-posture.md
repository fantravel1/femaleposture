# Step Aerobics Posture

Step aerobics combines cardio with platform work. Proper posture ensures safety and effectiveness.

## Basic Stance

### Floor Position

**Ready stance:**
- Feet hip-width
- Core engaged
- Shoulders back
- Eyes forward
- Ready for step

### On Platform

**Step position:**
- Full foot on step
- Weight centered
- Core engaged
- Upright posture
- Stable base

## Step Technique

### Basic Step

**Up and down:**
- Full foot contact
- Drive through heel
- Core stable
- Controlled descent
- Light landing

### Leading Leg

**Alternating:**
- Switch leads regularly
- Balance both sides
- Natural pattern
- Prevent fatigue
- Even development

## Movement Patterns

### Basic Moves

**Foundation:**
- Basic step
- V-step
- Turn step
- Over the top
- Controlled motion

### Arms

**Upper body:**
- Add arm movements
- Controlled range
- Coordinate with legs
- Full body workout
- Energy increase

## Safety

### Platform Awareness

**Avoiding falls:**
- Watch step edge
- Full foot contact
- Controlled speed
- Don't look down
- Know your space

### Intensity

**Managing effort:**
- Start low platform
- Build gradually
- Modify as needed
- Honor fatigue
- Safe progression

Good step aerobics posture creates safe, effective cardiovascular training.
