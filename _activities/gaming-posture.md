# Gaming Posture

Gaming sessions can last hours, making proper posture crucial. Whether using controller or keyboard, good positioning prevents strain and improves performance.

## Screen Setup

### Monitor Position

**Visual ergonomics:**
- Eye level with top of screen
- Arm's length distance
- Minimal glare
- Appropriate brightness
- Multiple monitor consideration

### TV Gaming

**Console setup:**
- Comfortable viewing distance
- Screen at eye level
- Room lighting
- Couch vs chair
- Position options

## Controller Gaming

### Hand Position

**Holding controller:**
- Relaxed grip
- Thumbs comfortable
- Wrists neutral
- Arms supported
- Shoulders relaxed

### Body Position

**While playing:**
- Back supported
- Feet grounded
- Core engaged when sitting forward
- Position variety
- Regular breaks

## Keyboard and Mouse

### PC Gaming Setup

**Ergonomic desk:**
- Keyboard at proper height
- Mouse within easy reach
- Wrists supported
- Chair adjusted
- Monitor positioned

### Hand Position

**Input device use:**
- Neutral wrists
- Fingers curved
- Relaxed grip
- Movement from arm
- Regular stretches

## Break Strategies

### Taking Breaks

**Movement intervals:**
- Set reminders
- Stand between matches
- Walk around
- Eye rest
- Full body stretches

Good gaming posture lets you play longer and better while protecting your body from the strain of extended sessions.
