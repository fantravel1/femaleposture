# Contemporary Dance Posture

Contemporary dance blends techniques with expression. Proper posture enables fluid, powerful movement.

## Standing Posture

### Neutral Base

**Starting position:**
- Feet parallel
- Core engaged
- Spine long
- Shoulders released
- Head floating

### Breath Integration

**Moving with air:**
- Inhale prepares
- Exhale initiates
- Breath guides movement
- Natural rhythm
- Organic motion

## Floor Work

### Getting Down

**Controlled descent:**
- Sequential folding
- Core controlled
- Weight managed
- Smooth transition
- Safe landing

### On the Floor

**Ground connection:**
- Weight distributed
- Momentum used
- Core engaged
- Fluid motion
- Push and yield

### Rising

**Coming up:**
- Sequential rise
- Core initiated
- Momentum assisted
- Controlled ascent
- Fluid transition

## Release Technique

### Letting Go

**Release quality:**
- Tension release
- Weight surrender
- Breath driven
- Controlled freedom
- Organic quality

### Recovery

**After release:**
- Core engagement
- Controlled rebound
- Energy recycled
- Flow continued
- Seamless movement

## Expressiveness

### Embodiment

**Full expression:**
- Whole body story
- Emotion in motion
- Connected movement
- Personal voice
- Authentic expression

Good contemporary dance posture enables expressive, fluid movement.
