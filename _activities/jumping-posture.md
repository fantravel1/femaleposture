# Jumping Posture

Jumping exercises build power. Proper posture ensures safe, effective plyometric training.

## Takeoff Position

### Jump Initiation

**Starting jump:**
- Athletic stance
- Knees bent
- Arms swing
- Core engaged
- Power generation

### Explosive Movement

**Leaving ground:**
- Drive through feet
- Extend fully
- Arms assist
- Powerful motion
- Coordinated movement

## Landing Mechanics

### Safe Landing

**Ground contact:**
- Soft landing
- Knees bend
- Absorb impact
- Quiet feet
- Controlled deceleration

### Knee Position

**Alignment:**
- Knees track over toes
- No collapse inward
- Proper mechanics
- Injury prevention
- Safe practice

## Common Jumps

### Squat Jumps

**Basic plyometric:**
- Squat position
- Jump up
- Land softly
- Return to squat
- Controlled repetitions

### Box Jumps

**Elevated target:**
- Appropriate height
- Full commitment
- Soft landing
- Step down
- Safe practice

## Progressive Training

### Building Skill

**Jump progression:**
- Start low impact
- Build gradually
- Master landing
- Increase challenge
- Safe progression

## Surface Matters

### Impact Absorption

**Jumping surface:**
- Appropriate floor
- Cushioned if possible
- Even surface
- Safe landing zone
- Equipment quality

Good jumping posture ensures safe, effective plyometric training.
