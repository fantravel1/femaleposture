# Makeup Application Posture

Makeup application requires focus and precision. Proper posture enables comfortable, effective application.

## Mirror Setup

### Seated Preferred

**At vanity:**
- Seated position
- Good lighting
- Mirror at face level
- Comfortable reach
- Everything accessible

### Mirror Height

**Face level:**
- No neck strain
- Comfortable viewing
- Good lighting angle
- Close enough to see
- Proper position

## Arm Position

### Supporting Arms

**During application:**
- Elbow support helpful
- Rest arm when possible
- Don't hunch forward
- Relax shoulders
- Comfortable reach

### Fine Detail Work

**Precision moments:**
- Steady hand
- Elbows braced
- Close to mirror
- Brief duration
- Return to comfortable

## Lighting

### Seeing Clearly

**Good illumination:**
- Natural light ideal
- Bright enough
- Even lighting
- No shadows
- Accurate colors

## Common Issues

### Neck Forward

**Avoiding strain:**
- Bring mirror closer
- Don't lean in
- Use magnifying mirror
- Maintain alignment
- Short sessions

### Shoulder Tension

**Preventing strain:**
- Relax shoulders down
- Take breaks
- Stretch between
- Efficient routine
- Don't rush

## Efficient Routine

### Streamlined Application

**Reducing time:**
- Organized products
- Efficient order
- Multi-use products
- Practice helps
- Comfortable routine

Good makeup application posture reduces strain during beauty routines.
