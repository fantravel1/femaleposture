# Gaming Posture: Console and PC Gaming

> **Game Without Pain:** Gaming sessions can be long and intense. Proper posture helps you play longer and prevents chronic issues.

---

## The Gaming Challenge

Gaming involves:
- Prolonged sitting
- Intense focus (forget body position)
- Repetitive hand movements
- Static positions for hours

---

## PC Gaming Setup

### Monitor Position

- Top of screen at eye level
- Arms length away
- Direct line of sight

### Desk and Chair

- Ergonomic chair with support
- Elbows at 90 degrees
- Feet flat on floor
- Keyboard and mouse accessible

### Take Breaks

- Every hour minimum
- Stretch and move
- Eye breaks (20-20-20 rule)

---

## Console Gaming

### Sitting Position

- Back support (not couch slouch)
- Screen at eye level
- Arms supported if possible

### Avoid

- Lying down with head propped
- Leaning far forward
- Marathon sessions without breaks

---

## Essential Stretches for Gamers

1. Wrist circles and stretches
2. Shoulder rolls
3. Neck stretches
4. Hip flexor stretches
5. Upper back stretches
