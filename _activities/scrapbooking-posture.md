# Scrapbooking Posture

Scrapbooking involves cutting and arranging. Proper posture supports comfortable creative sessions.

## Workspace Setup

### Table Space

**Craft table:**
- Large surface
- Organized materials
- Good lighting
- Comfortable height
- Creative space

### Seating Position

**At table:**
- Supportive chair
- Proper height
- Feet on floor
- Back supported
- Comfortable work

## Cutting Work

### Paper Cutting

**Using scissors:**
- Relaxed grip
- Wrist neutral
- Sharp scissors
- Controlled cuts
- Comfortable motion

### Craft Knife

**Precision cutting:**
- Cutting mat
- Ruler guide
- Controlled pressure
- Safety first
- Accurate cuts

## Arranging Pages

### Layout Work

**Designing pages:**
- Close to work
- Easy reach
- Creative viewing
- Move elements
- Enjoyable arranging

### Adhesive Application

**Gluing:**
- Controlled application
- Precise placement
- Steady hands
- Clean work
- Final positions

## Extended Sessions

### Long Crafting

**Creative flow:**
- Take breaks
- Stretch periodically
- Rest eyes
- Move around
- Sustainable sessions

### Posture Check

**During work:**
- Check slouching
- Shoulders down
- Back straight
- Comfortable position
- Quality work

Good scrapbooking posture enables enjoyable, comfortable memory keeping.
