# Slack Lining Posture

Slack lining develops exceptional balance. Proper posture enables successful line walking.

## Standing Balance

### On the Line

**Basic position:**
- One foot at a time
- Weight centered
- Arms out for balance
- Core very engaged
- Eyes forward

### Finding Balance

**Initial attempts:**
- Start with support
- Find center point
- Micro-adjustments
- Patient practice
- Progressive challenge

## Walking

### First Steps

**Moving forward:**
- One step at a time
- Pause to balance
- Small steps
- Arms active
- Eyes ahead (not down)

### Foot Placement

**On the line:**
- Lengthwise along line
- Whole foot
- Soft placement
- Feel the line
- Precise positioning

## Core Engagement

### Stability Center

**Balance core:**
- Extremely engaged
- Constant adjustment
- Hip stability
- Responsive motion
- Essential control

### Arms

**Counter-balance:**
- Active balancing
- Wide if needed
- Constant adjustment
- Natural response
- Essential tools

## Falling

### Safe Falls

**When you fall:**
- Expect to fall
- Step off cleanly
- Be ready
- Part of practice
- Learn from falls

### Progressive Practice

**Building skill:**
- Low line first
- Short distances
- Gradual length
- Height last
- Patient progression

## Benefits

### Balance Training

**Skill development:**
- Proprioception
- Core strength
- Focus
- Body awareness
- Transferable skill

Good slack lining posture enables balance development on the line.
