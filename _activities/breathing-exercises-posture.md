# Breathing Exercises Posture

Breathing exercises benefit from optimal alignment. Proper posture enables full respiratory function.

## Seated Position

### Upright Sitting

**For breathwork:**
- Tall spine
- Shoulders back
- Chest open
- Relaxed muscles
- Core lightly engaged

### Floor or Chair

**Either works:**
- Floor for tradition
- Chair for accessibility
- Same principles
- Comfort matters
- Personal preference

## Lying Position

### Supine Breathing

**On back:**
- Flat surface
- Knees bent optional
- Arms at sides
- Fully relaxed
- Easy for beginners

### Relaxation Response

**Deep relaxation:**
- Comfortable position
- Support where needed
- Complete release
- Body scan
- Peaceful position

## Chest and Belly

### Diaphragmatic Focus

**Belly breathing:**
- Belly expands on inhale
- Ribs expand
- Chest moves minimally
- Natural motion
- Core awareness

### Hand Placement

**Awareness tool:**
- Hand on belly
- Hand on chest
- Feel the movement
- Learn pattern
- Develop awareness

## Advanced Breathwork

### Pranayama

**Yogic breathing:**
- Seated traditionally
- Various techniques
- Specific positions
- Progressive learning
- Guidance helpful

### Intensity

**Strong breathwork:**
- Seated preferred
- Stable position
- Support if needed
- Practice safely
- Know your limits

## Integration

### Regular Practice

**Building habit:**
- Consistent position
- Same time daily
- Comfortable space
- Routine development
- Lifestyle integration

Good breathing posture enables optimal respiratory exercise benefits.
