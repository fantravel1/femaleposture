# Exam Taking Posture

Exam taking requires sustained focus. Proper posture supports concentration and comfortable writing.

## Test Position

### At Desk

**Exam setup:**
- Comfortable seat
- Materials organized
- Good posture
- Ready position
- Focused start

### Writing Position

**Pen or pencil:**
- Relaxed grip
- Wrist supported
- Smooth writing
- Arm comfortable
- Extended writing ready

## Concentration

### Alert Posture

**Mental focus:**
- Upright sitting
- Deep breathing
- Core engaged lightly
- Clear thinking
- Focused state

### Stress Management

**Test anxiety:**
- Breathe deeply
- Relax shoulders
- Unclench jaw
- Calm body
- Clear mind

## Extended Exams

### Long Tests

**Multi-hour exams:**
- Shift positions
- Take allowed breaks
- Stretch when possible
- Maintain stamina
- Pace yourself

### Fatigue Management

**Staying alert:**
- Good posture helps
- Micro-movements
- Deep breaths
- Brief eye rests
- Sustained focus

## Computer Testing

### Screen Exams

**Digital tests:**
- Screen position
- Comfortable typing
- Mouse access
- Good posture
- Technology comfort

## After Exam

### Completion

**Finishing:**
- Review if time
- Relaxed finish
- Stretch after
- Release tension
- Transition out

Good exam taking posture supports sustained concentration and test performance.
