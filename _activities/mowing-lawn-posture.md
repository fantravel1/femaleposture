# Mowing Lawn Posture

Lawn mowing involves pushing and walking. Proper posture prevents strain during yard work.

## Push Mower Technique

### Handle Position

**Gripping mower:**
- Both hands
- Relaxed grip
- Arms comfortable
- Shoulders relaxed
- Upright stance

### Pushing Form

**Forward motion:**
- Core engaged
- Push from legs
- Upright posture
- Steady pace
- Controlled movement

## Walking Form

### Yard Navigation

**Walking behind mower:**
- Natural gait
- Upright posture
- Comfortable pace
- Watch terrain
- Safe footing

### Slope Handling

**Hills and inclines:**
- Mow across slopes
- Not up and down
- Extra caution
- Stable footing
- Safety first

## Turning

### End of Row

**Turning mower:**
- Slow for turns
- Controlled motion
- Push or pull as needed
- Efficient pattern
- Safe handling

## Self-Propelled

### Easier Option

**Powered assist:**
- Less pushing effort
- Guide and steer
- Controlled pace
- Reduced strain
- Worth investment

## Duration Management

### Large Yards

**Extended mowing:**
- Take breaks
- Hydrate
- Rest in shade
- Split sessions
- Pace yourself

### Heat Consideration

**Summer mowing:**
- Early morning best
- Avoid peak heat
- Stay hydrated
- Rest frequently
- Safe timing

Good lawn mowing posture enables efficient, comfortable yard maintenance.
