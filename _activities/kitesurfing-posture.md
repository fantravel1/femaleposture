# Kitesurfing Posture

Kitesurfing combines kite flying with board riding. Proper posture enables control and power harnessing.

## Body Position

### Basic Stance

**On water:**
- Harness engaged
- Hips forward
- Knees bent
- Arms relaxed
- Core engaged

### Edge Control

**Board angle:**
- Heels pressed
- Weight on back foot
- Upwind edge
- Controlled lean
- Power management

## Kite Control

### Bar Position

**Flying the kite:**
- Arms relaxed
- Bar centered
- Subtle movements
- Sheet in/out
- Responsive control

### Power Zone

**Kite position:**
- Edge of window for travel
- Power zone for boost
- Neutral zone for rest
- Constant awareness
- Smooth transitions

## Water Start

### Getting Up

**From water:**
- Board across wind
- Kite at 11 or 1
- Dive for power
- Stand gradually
- Balance immediately

### Stance on Board

**Riding position:**
- Back foot in back strap
- Front foot forward
- Weight centered
- Edging as needed
- Looking direction of travel

## Upwind Riding

### Technique

**Going upwind:**
- Strong edge
- Weight back
- Kite higher
- Constant pressure
- Efficient angle

## Safety

### Kite Management

**Safe practices:**
- Always leash attached
- Know quick release
- Assess conditions
- Respect power
- Progressive learning

Good kitesurfing posture creates control and power management for safe riding.
