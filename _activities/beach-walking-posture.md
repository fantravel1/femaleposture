# Beach Walking Posture

Beach walking offers unique terrain. Proper posture adapts to sand while maximizing benefits.

## Sand Surface

### Soft Sand

**Deep sand walking:**
- More challenging
- Greater workout
- Shorter steps
- Core engaged
- Ankle stability needed

### Wet Sand

**Firm surface:**
- Easier walking
- Better support
- Longer strides
- Near water
- Efficient walking

## Barefoot Benefits

### Natural Walking

**Unshod movement:**
- Feel the sand
- Foot strength
- Natural gait
- Sensory experience
- Ground connection

### Foot Care

**Barefoot caution:**
- Watch for shells
- Avoid hot sand
- Check for debris
- Protect soles
- Safe barefoot

## Walking Form

### Upright Posture

**Beach form:**
- Head up
- Shoulders back
- Core engaged
- Natural arm swing
- Confident stride

### Pace Adjustment

**Sand adaptation:**
- Slower in soft sand
- More energy required
- Comfortable pace
- Enjoy surroundings
- Sustainable walking

## Environmental Factors

### Sun and Heat

**Weather awareness:**
- Sun protection
- Hydration
- Timing walks
- Comfortable conditions
- Safe practice

### Tide Awareness

**Water levels:**
- Know tide patterns
- Safe route
- Wet sand easier
- Plan appropriately
- Enjoyable walking

Good beach walking posture maximizes coastal walking benefits.
