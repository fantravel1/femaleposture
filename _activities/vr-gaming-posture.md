# VR Gaming Posture

VR gaming involves wearing headsets and moving. Proper posture enhances experience and prevents injury.

## Headset Fitting

### Proper Adjustment

**Wearing headset:**
- Secure but not tight
- Balanced weight
- Clear vision
- Comfortable straps
- Proper fit

### Neck Consideration

**Headset weight:**
- Head balanced
- Don't lean forward
- Neck neutral
- Take breaks
- Manage fatigue

## Play Area Safety

### Space Clearing

**Before play:**
- Clear obstacles
- Know boundaries
- Safe flooring
- Room to move
- Hazard-free zone

### Guardian System

**Boundary awareness:**
- Set boundaries
- Trust system
- Stay in zone
- Safe movement
- Aware of limits

## Standing VR

### Upright Position

**Standing games:**
- Balanced stance
- Core engaged
- Natural posture
- Safe footing
- Movement room

### Movement Games

**Active VR:**
- Warm up first
- Move safely
- Know surroundings
- Controlled motion
- Athletic approach

## Seated VR

### Chair Gaming

**Sitting games:**
- Good posture
- Stable chair
- Feet grounded
- Swivel chair works
- Comfortable setup

## Session Duration

### Break Importance

**VR fatigue:**
- Regular breaks
- Eye rest
- Motion sickness awareness
- Hydrate
- Know limits

Good VR gaming posture enables safe, immersive virtual reality experiences.
