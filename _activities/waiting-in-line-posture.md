# Waiting in Line Posture

Waiting in lines involves extended standing. Proper posture prevents fatigue during queues.

## Standing Position

### Balanced Stance

**In line:**
- Weight distributed
- Feet comfortable width
- Core engaged lightly
- Shoulders relaxed
- Patient standing

### Shifting Weight

**Long waits:**
- Shift periodically
- Alternate legs
- Small movements
- Prevent fatigue
- Comfortable waiting

## Posture Maintenance

### Upright Position

**During wait:**
- Stand tall
- Don't slump
- Relaxed but upright
- Good posture
- Patient bearing

### Phone Use

**While waiting:**
- Raise phone up
- Avoid head drop
- Brief use
- Posture aware
- Neck protection

## Long Queues

### Extended Waits

**Managing duration:**
- Move when line moves
- Maintain awareness
- Patient attitude
- Comfortable stance
- Sustainable waiting

### Fatigue Management

**Long standing:**
- Subtle movement
- Weight shifts
- Core engagement
- Breathing awareness
- Patience practice

## Movement Forward

### Line Progression

**Moving up:**
- Walk naturally
- Stay attentive
- Appropriate spacing
- Ready when called
- Smooth transition

Good waiting in line posture prevents fatigue during extended queue standing.
