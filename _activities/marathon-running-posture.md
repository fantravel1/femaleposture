# Marathon Running Posture

Marathon running demands sustainable posture. Proper form conserves energy over 26.2 miles.

## Efficient Form

### Economy Focus

**Saving energy:**
- Relaxed movement
- Minimal vertical bounce
- Efficient stride
- Controlled arm swing
- Sustainable effort

### Posture Basics

**Foundation:**
- Tall spine
- Slight forward lean
- Relaxed shoulders
- Core engaged
- Eyes ahead

## Stride Mechanics

### Foot Strike

**Ground contact:**
- Natural landing
- Under hips
- Quick turnover
- Minimal braking
- Efficient pattern

### Cadence

**Step rate:**
- Higher cadence preferred
- Shorter stride
- Quick feet
- Less impact
- Energy efficient

## Arm Mechanics

### Efficient Swing

**Upper body:**
- 90 degree bend
- Relaxed hands
- Controlled swing
- No crossing midline
- Energy conservation

## Fatigue Management

### Late Miles

**When tired:**
- Check posture
- Reset form
- Mental cues
- Stay efficient
- Finish strong

### Form Cues

**When struggling:**
- Stand tall
- Relax shoulders
- Short quick steps
- Breathe fully
- Keep moving

## Race Strategy

### Even Effort

**Pacing:**
- Conservative start
- Consistent effort
- Save for end
- Trust training
- Finish strong

Good marathon running posture maintains efficiency across the full distance.
