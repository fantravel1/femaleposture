# Trash Taking Out Posture

Taking out trash involves lifting bags. Proper posture prevents strain during waste disposal.

## Bag Lifting

### Picking Up

**Lifting bag:**
- Squat down
- Secure grip
- Back straight
- Lift with legs
- Controlled motion

### Weight Check

**Before lifting:**
- Assess weight
- Double bag if needed
- Not too heavy
- Manageable load
- Safe lifting

## Carrying

### Transport Position

**Carrying bag:**
- Away from body slightly
- Arm relaxed
- Core engaged
- Quick trip
- Efficient transport

### Walking

**To bin:**
- Direct route
- Careful footing
- Quick journey
- Safe path
- Complete task

## Bin Handling

### Lifting Into

**Into container:**
- Controlled placement
- Don't fling
- Secure deposit
- Close lid
- Complete task

### Wheeled Bins

**To curb:**
- Push don't pull
- Upright position
- Controlled movement
- Easy transport
- Efficient task

## Regular Practice

### Frequent Task

**Weekly chore:**
- Routine task
- Consistent technique
- Safe practice
- Quick job
- Complete regularly

Good trash taking out posture prevents strain during waste disposal.
