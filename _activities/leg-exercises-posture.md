# Leg Exercises Posture

Leg exercises build lower body strength. Proper posture ensures effective, safe training.

## Squats

### Basic Squat Form

**Squat technique:**
- Feet shoulder-width
- Knees track over toes
- Hips back
- Chest up
- Controlled depth

### Common Corrections

**Form fixes:**
- Knees not caving
- Weight in heels
- Upright torso
- Core engaged
- Full range

## Lunges

### Forward Lunge

**Stepping forward:**
- Controlled step
- Front knee over ankle
- Back knee toward floor
- Upright torso
- Balanced motion

### Reverse Lunge

**Stepping back:**
- Controlled step back
- Front knee stable
- Lowering down
- Core engaged
- Safer option often

## Deadlifts

### Hip Hinge

**Deadlift form:**
- Hips back
- Back straight
- Weight close to legs
- Engage glutes and hamstrings
- Controlled movement

## Step-Ups

### Elevated Step

**Step-up form:**
- Full foot on surface
- Drive through heel
- Upright torso
- Controlled stepping
- Alternate legs

## Safety

### Weight Selection

**Appropriate load:**
- Form first
- Progress gradually
- Listen to body
- Appropriate challenge
- Safe training

Good leg exercise posture ensures effective, safe lower body strengthening.
