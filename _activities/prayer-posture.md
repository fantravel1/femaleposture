# Prayer Posture

Prayer often involves sustained positions that can strain the body. Proper posture supports your spiritual practice while protecting your physical health.

## Kneeling Position

### Proper Form

**Knee comfort:**
- Cushion or mat
- Weight distributed
- Spine upright
- Core engaged
- Hands positioned

### Duration Management

**Extended kneeling:**
- Shift weight periodically
- Use knee support
- Know your limits
- Rise carefully
- Alternative positions

## Standing Position

### Proper Alignment

**Upright prayer:**
- Feet grounded
- Weight balanced
- Spine tall
- Shoulders relaxed
- Head balanced

### Duration Support

**Long standing:**
- Shift weight
- Soft knees
- Comfortable footwear
- Position changes
- Sit when needed

## Seated Position

### Chair or Bench

**Seated prayer:**
- Back supported or upright
- Feet grounded
- Hands comfortable
- Alert posture
- Breathing easy

### Floor Sitting

**Cross-legged:**
- Cushion for height
- Sustainable position
- Spine upright
- Comfort priority
- Position variety

## Prostration

### Full Bow

**Safe technique:**
- Controlled descent
- Knees first
- Core engaged
- Safe rise
- Physical capacity awareness

Good prayer posture supports your spiritual practice while caring for the body as part of your whole being.
