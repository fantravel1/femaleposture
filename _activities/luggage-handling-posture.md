# Luggage Handling Posture

Travel involves luggage handling. Proper technique prevents injury during travel.

## Packing

### On Surface

**Preparing luggage:**
- Pack on raised surface
- Not on floor
- Comfortable height
- Organize efficiently
- Weight distribution

### Weight Awareness

**Keeping it light:**
- Minimize packing
- Light luggage
- Consider shipping
- Wear heavy items
- Strategic choices

## Lifting Suitcase

### From Floor

**Picking up:**
- Squat down
- Close to body
- Lift with legs
- Handle secure
- Controlled motion

### Into Overhead

**Airplane bin:**
- Two hands
- Lift progressively
- Ask for help
- Slide in carefully
- Don't overreach

### Into Car

**Trunk loading:**
- Close to trunk
- Lift properly
- Slide in
- Don't twist
- Manage weight

## Wheeling

### Rolling Technique

**Walking with bag:**
- Pull behind
- Don't twist
- Straight arm
- Switch hands
- Smooth surfaces easier

### Two-Wheel vs Four

**Bag type:**
- Four wheels easier
- Push beside you
- Less arm strain
- Smooth rolling
- Preferred when possible

## Carrying

### Shoulder Bags

**When no wheels:**
- Alternate shoulders
- Short distances
- Crossbody better
- Limit weight
- Know limits

### Backpack

**Best distribution:**
- Both shoulders
- Hip belt if available
- Close to back
- Adjusted properly
- Healthiest option

Good luggage handling posture protects during travel.
