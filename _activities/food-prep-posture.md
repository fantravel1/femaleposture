# Food Prep Posture

Food preparation involves extended standing. Proper posture prevents kitchen fatigue.

## Counter Height

### Work Surface

**Kitchen counter:**
- Ideally at hip level
- Comfortable chopping height
- No hunching
- Adjust as needed
- Ergonomic setup

### Height Adjustment

**If counter wrong height:**
- Cutting board raise
- Stool for sitting
- Anti-fatigue mat
- Adaptive solutions
- Comfortable working

## Chopping Position

### Knife Work

**Cutting form:**
- Stand close to counter
- Shoulders relaxed
- Wrist neutral
- Safe technique
- Efficient cutting

### Board Position

**Cutting board:**
- Stable placement
- Comfortable height
- Proper angle
- Safe cutting
- Organized prep

## Standing Duration

### Extended Prep

**Long cooking:**
- Anti-fatigue mat
- Supportive shoes
- Shift positions
- Brief sitting breaks
- Sustainable prep

### Fatigue Management

**Preventing strain:**
- Prep in stages
- Sit when possible
- Alternate tasks
- Take breaks
- Paced cooking

## Stovetop Work

### At Range

**Cooking position:**
- Close enough
- Safe distance from heat
- Comfortable reaching
- Proper tool length
- Safe cooking

## Cleanup

### Dish Washing

**At sink:**
- Counter height
- Upright stance
- Not hunching over
- Comfortable reaching
- Efficient cleanup

Good food prep posture prevents fatigue during meal preparation.
