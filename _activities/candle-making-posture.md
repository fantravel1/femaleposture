# Candle Making Posture

Candle making involves standing and pouring. Proper posture supports safe, comfortable crafting.

## Work Area

### Counter Height

**Standing work:**
- Comfortable height
- Safe workspace
- Organized supplies
- Heat-safe surface
- Room to move

### Standing Position

**At workspace:**
- Upright stance
- Anti-fatigue mat
- Comfortable shoes
- Core engaged
- Relaxed shoulders

## Wax Handling

### Melting Wax

**Heat source:**
- Safe position
- Watch temperature
- Controlled heating
- Proper tools
- Safety focus

### Pouring Technique

**Into molds:**
- Stable grip
- Controlled pour
- Close to mold
- Two hands if heavy
- Careful motion

## Hot Materials

### Safety Position

**Working with heat:**
- Face away from heat
- Proper tools
- Controlled movements
- Protective gear
- Safe handling

### Burn Prevention

**Safety measures:**
- Long sleeves optional
- Clear workspace
- No distractions
- Focused attention
- Safe practices

## Finishing Work

### Wick Trimming

**After cooling:**
- Seated work fine
- Steady hands
- Sharp scissors
- Final touches
- Satisfying finish

Good candle making posture supports safe, enjoyable candle crafting.
