# Resistance Band Posture

Resistance bands offer versatile training anywhere. Proper posture maintains effective tension throughout exercises.

## Band Setup

### Anchor Points

**Secure attachment:**
- Sturdy attachment
- Check before starting
- Door attachments proper
- No slipping risk
- Height appropriate

### Tension Management

**Band selection:**
- Appropriate resistance
- Tension throughout range
- Not too loose
- Not too heavy
- Progressive options

## Body Position

### Standing Exercises

**Proper stance:**
- Stable base
- Band under feet or attached
- Core engaged
- Shoulders set
- Balanced position

### Floor Exercises

**Ground work:**
- Band secured appropriately
- Core engaged
- Spine aligned
- Controlled movement
- Full range

## Technique Principles

### Tension Maintenance

**Effective work:**
- Band taut throughout
- Control both directions
- No snapping
- Smooth motion
- Full range

### Body Control

**During exercise:**
- Core stability
- No compensation
- Isolated movement when intended
- Balanced effort
- Proper breathing

## Common Exercises

### Upper Body

**Arm and back:**
- Rows
- Presses
- Curls
- Extensions
- Shoulder work

### Lower Body

**Legs and glutes:**
- Squats with band
- Lateral walks
- Glute bridges
- Leg extensions
- Hip abduction

Good resistance band posture maintains proper tension and alignment for effective training anywhere.
