# Krav Maga Posture

Krav Maga is practical self-defense. Proper posture enables effective protection in real situations.

## Fighting Stance

### Basic Position

**Ready stance:**
- Feet staggered
- Knees bent
- Hands up
- Chin protected
- Core engaged

### Passive Stance

**Pre-conflict:**
- Hands visible
- Non-threatening
- Ready to defend
- Verbal de-escalation
- Prepared position

## Defenses

### Against Strikes

**Blocking:**
- 360 defense
- Inside defenses
- Outside defenses
- Simultaneous counter
- Immediate response

### Against Grabs

**Breaking holds:**
- Leverage use
- Weak points
- Counter attacks
- Escape methods
- Create distance

### Against Weapons

**Threat response:**
- Assess situation
- Compliance vs resistance
- Redirect weapon
- Control and counter
- Escape priority
