# Podcast Recording Posture

Podcast recording requires voice clarity. Proper posture supports optimal vocal production.

## Microphone Position

### Mic Distance

**Optimal placement:**
- Few inches from mouth
- Consistent distance
- Pop filter between
- Proper angle
- Clear audio

### Mic Height

**Level positioning:**
- Mouth level
- Not reaching up
- Not looking down
- Natural position
- Comfortable speaking

## Seated Recording

### Chair Position

**Recording setup:**
- Upright posture
- Back supported
- Feet grounded
- Comfortable position
- Sustainable posture

### Upper Body

**Voice support:**
- Shoulders relaxed
- Chest open
- Core engaged lightly
- Natural breathing
- Clear voice

## Breathing

### Vocal Support

**For speaking:**
- Deep breathing
- Diaphragm engaged
- Relaxed throat
- Clear articulation
- Sustained speaking

## Extended Sessions

### Long Recording

**Hour+ sessions:**
- Take breaks
- Stretch periodically
- Hydrate
- Maintain posture
- Sustainable recording

### Voice Care

**Protecting voice:**
- Warm up
- Stay hydrated
- Natural volume
- Good posture
- Voice health

Good podcast recording posture supports clear, sustainable vocal performance.
