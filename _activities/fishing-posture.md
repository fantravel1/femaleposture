# Fishing Posture

Fishing involves various positions. Proper posture enables comfortable, successful fishing.

## Standing Fishing

### Bank Fishing

**Standing position:**
- Comfortable stance
- Weight balanced
- Ready for cast
- Stable base
- Relaxed waiting

### Wade Fishing

**In water:**
- Secure footing
- Current awareness
- Stable stance
- Core engaged
- Balance priority

## Seated Fishing

### On Shore

**Sitting and waiting:**
- Comfortable seat
- Rod supported
- Relaxed position
- Ready for bite
- Patient waiting

### Boat Fishing

**On vessel:**
- Stable position
- Sea legs
- Seated when rough
- Standing when calm
- Safety priority

## Casting

### Overhead Cast

**Standard cast:**
- Feet stable
- Core rotation
- Arm motion
- Follow through
- Relaxed release

### Side Cast

**Tight spaces:**
- Modified motion
- Horizontal swing
- Core involved
- Controlled motion
- Accurate placement

## Waiting

### Patient Position

**Between bites:**
- Comfortable stance or seat
- Rod supported
- Relaxed attention
- Ready to respond
- Sustainable wait

## Reeling In

### Fighting Fish

**Landing catch:**
- Stable stance
- Rod tip up
- Pump and reel
- Core engaged
- Sustained effort

Good fishing posture enables comfortable, successful angling.
