# Shoulder Exercises Posture

Shoulder exercises require careful form. Proper posture protects joints while building strength.

## Pressing Movements

### Shoulder Press

**Overhead press:**
- Core braced
- Natural path
- Full extension
- Controlled return
- Safe technique

### Form Points

**Press technique:**
- Not arching back
- Controlled weight
- Proper range
- Balanced movement
- Safe training

## Lateral Raises

### Side Raise

**Lateral raise form:**
- Slight elbow bend
- Raise to shoulder height
- Controlled movement
- No swinging
- Quality reps

### Front Raise

**Anterior work:**
- Controlled raising
- Shoulder height max
- Alternating or together
- Proper weight
- Safe technique

## Rotator Cuff

### Internal/External Rotation

**Rotator work:**
- Light weight
- Controlled motion
- Full range
- Protective exercise
- Injury prevention

## Posture During

### Core Engagement

**Stable base:**
- Core engaged
- No leaning
- Controlled posture
- Proper alignment
- Safe foundation

## Joint Protection

### Shoulder Safety

**Safe training:**
- Warm up essential
- Appropriate weight
- Listen to body
- Proper form
- Injury prevention

Good shoulder exercise posture protects joints while building deltoid strength.
