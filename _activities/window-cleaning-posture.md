# Window Cleaning Posture

Window cleaning involves reaching and wiping. Proper posture prevents strain during cleaning.

## Reaching Height

### Standard Windows

**Eye level and above:**
- Step stool for high
- Extension tools help
- Don't overreach
- Safe access
- Comfortable reach

### Low Windows

**Below eye level:**
- Kneel or squat
- Comfortable position
- Pad knees
- Access easily
- Protected joints

## Wiping Motion

### Arm Movement

**Cleaning action:**
- Full arm motion
- Shoulder engaged
- Wrist neutral
- Smooth strokes
- Efficient cleaning

### Pressure Applied

**Against glass:**
- Moderate pressure
- Not straining
- Let solution work
- Circular or straight
- Effective technique

## Inside vs Outside

### Interior Windows

**Easier access:**
- Safer position
- Stable footing
- Comfortable reach
- Routine cleaning
- Standard approach

### Exterior Windows

**More challenging:**
- Safety first
- Proper equipment
- Consider professional
- Know limits
- Safe practice

## Extended Cleaning

### Many Windows

**Multiple windows:**
- Take breaks
- Alternate arms
- Pace work
- Split over time
- Sustainable cleaning

Good window cleaning posture enables safe, effective glass cleaning.
