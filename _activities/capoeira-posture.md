# Capoeira Posture

Capoeira blends martial arts with dance and acrobatics. Proper posture enables fluid, powerful movement.

## Ginga

### Basic Sway

**Foundation movement:**
- Weight transfer
- Continuous motion
- Side to side
- Arms guard
- Never still

### Body Position

**Ready stance:**
- Knees bent
- Core engaged
- Weight mobile
- Guard up
- Eyes aware

## Defensive Movements

### Esquivas

**Evasive moves:**
- Squat away
- Lean escape
- Roll out
- Quick recovery
- Continuous flow

### Negativa

**Low position:**
- One leg extended
- Support on hand
- Low to ground
- Defensive position
- Transition ready

## Kicks

### Meia Lua

**Half moon kick:**
- Circular path
- Core rotation
- Controlled power
- Balance recovery
- Flow maintained

### Armada

**Spinning kick:**
- Full rotation
- Power generation
- Spotting
- Controlled landing
- Continued movement

## Acrobatic Elements

### Au

**Cartwheel:**
- Controlled entry
- Core engaged
- Through movement
- Controlled exit
- Flow integration

### Floor Work

**Ground game:**
- Low movements
- Acrobatic escapes
- Ground attacks
- Transition skills
- Fluid motion

## The Roda

### Circle Play

**Game context:**
- Partner interaction
- Musical response
- Call and answer
- Playful but real
- Mutual respect

Good capoeira posture enables the fluid martial art movement and play.
