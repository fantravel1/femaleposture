# Indoor Cycling Posture

Indoor cycling offers controlled training. Proper posture maximizes workout benefit.

## Bike Setup

### Stationary Bikes

**Adjustment:**
- Seat height first
- Handlebar height
- Reach comfortable
- Proper fit essential
- Take time to adjust

### Trainer Setup

**With road bike:**
- Same fit as outdoor
- May need adjustment
- Stability important
- Level surface
- Proper positioning

## Riding Position

### Seated Effort

**In saddle:**
- Core engaged
- Shoulders relaxed
- Light grip
- Eyes forward
- Sustainable position

### Standing Climbs

**Out of saddle:**
- Weight on pedals
- Core very engaged
- Controlled motion
- Bike stable
- Proper technique

## Extended Sessions

### Long Workouts

**Indoor training:**
- Position changes
- Stand periodically
- Stretch on bike
- Stay hydrated
- Sustainable effort

### Entertainment

**Staying engaged:**
- Screen at eye level
- Music or video
- Virtual rides
- Training programs
- Mental engagement

## Differences from Outdoor

### Adjustments

**Indoor specifics:**
- No coasting
- More standing helpful
- Fans for cooling
- Entertainment needed
- Different feel

### Benefits

**Indoor advantages:**
- Controlled environment
- Consistent conditions
- Structured workouts
- Time efficient
- Weather independent

Good indoor cycling posture enables effective, comfortable training.
