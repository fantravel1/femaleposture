# Bed Making Posture

Bed making involves reaching and bending. Proper posture prevents strain during daily task.

## Sheet Tucking

### Hospital Corners

**Tucking technique:**
- Kneel or half-kneel
- Access corners
- Proper technique
- Comfortable position
- Quality result

### Fitted Sheet

**Stretching sheet:**
- Walk around bed
- Don't overreach across
- Corner by corner
- Safe positioning
- Secure fit

## Blanket Arrangement

### Spreading Covers

**Laying blankets:**
- Stand at side
- Drape evenly
- Adjust from sides
- Smooth motion
- Efficient arrangement

### Tucking Edges

**Secure covers:**
- Comfortable reach
- Kneel for low access
- Neat finish
- Easy access
- Quality result

## Pillow Fluffing

### Pillow Care

**Fluffing technique:**
- Hold pillow
- Punch or shake
- Arm motion
- Arrangement care
- Finishing touch

## Making Strategy

### Efficient Approach

**Best method:**
- One side at a time
- Work around bed
- Don't climb on bed
- Efficient pattern
- Quick completion
