# Dynamic Stretching Posture

Dynamic stretching prepares the body for activity. Proper posture enables effective warm-up.

## Principles

### Movement Through Range

**Active stretching:**
- Moving through range
- Not holding
- Controlled motion
- Progressive range
- Muscle activation

### Activity Specific

**Match the workout:**
- Related movements
- Prepare specific muscles
- Sport relevant
- Progressive intensity
- Warm-up complete

## Lower Body

### Leg Swings

**Hip mobility:**
- Standing tall
- Forward and back
- Side to side
- Controlled swing
- Increasing range

### Walking Lunges

**Hip and leg prep:**
- Upright torso
- Controlled step
- Deep lunge
- Alternating legs
- Moving forward

### High Knees

**Hip flexor activation:**
- Running motion
- Knee drive
- Quick tempo
- Arms coordinated
- Light and quick

## Upper Body

### Arm Circles

**Shoulder prep:**
- Standing tall
- Full circles
- Progressive size
- Both directions
- Warm shoulders

### Trunk Rotations

**Spine warm-up:**
- Feet planted
- Rotate torso
- Arms following
- Controlled motion
- Full range

## Progressive Intensity

### Building Up

**Warm-up structure:**
- Start gentle
- Increase range
- Increase speed
- Activity specific
- Ready to perform

## Timing

### Pre-Workout

**When to perform:**
- Before activity
- 5-10 minutes
- Full body coverage
- Progressive intensity
- Ready for workout

Good dynamic stretching posture prepares the body for effective activity.
