# Golf Posture

Golf requires precise body positioning for a consistent, powerful swing. Understanding proper golf posture, especially as it relates to the female body, improves your game while preventing injury.

## Address Position

### Setup Basics

**Foundation elements:**
- Feet shoulder-width
- Slight knee bend
- Hip hinge forward
- Spine angle maintained
- Arms hanging naturally

### Women-Specific Setup

**Female considerations:**
- Slightly wider stance option
- Hip flexibility use
- Shoulder positioning
- Club length fit
- Comfortable balance

## Swing Mechanics

### Backswing

**Proper motion:**
- Shoulder turn
- Hip resistance
- Weight shift
- Arm position
- Club path

### Downswing

**Power generation:**
- Hip initiation
- Weight transfer
- Arm drop
- Impact position
- Extension through

### Follow Through

**Completion:**
- Full rotation
- Balanced finish
- Spine angle release
- Weight forward
- Comfortable end position

## Common Errors

### Posture Problems

**Typical issues:**
- Standing too upright
- Hunched shoulders
- Excessive knee bend
- Swaying
- Loss of spine angle

### Corrections

**Better mechanics:**
- Proper hip hinge
- Relaxed shoulders
- Athletic knee flex
- Stable base
- Maintained posture

## Injury Prevention

### Common Golf Injuries

**Risk areas:**
- Lower back
- Shoulders
- Elbows
- Wrists
- Knees

### Protective Practices

**Staying healthy:**
- Proper warm-up
- Strength training
- Flexibility work
- Technique lessons
- Gradual practice increase

Proper golf posture creates the foundation for a consistent swing while protecting your body for years of enjoyable play.
