# Walking Fitness Posture

Fitness walking is exercise through walking. Proper posture maximizes health benefits.

## Walking Form

### Upright Posture

**Basic position:**
- Tall spine
- Core engaged
- Shoulders back
- Eyes forward
- Chin parallel

### Arm Swing

**Upper body:**
- Bent arms 90 degrees
- Pump with walk
- Natural swing
- Adds intensity
- Full body workout

## Stride

### Step Length

**Efficient stride:**
- Natural length
- Don't overstride
- Quick steps
- Heel to toe roll
- Efficient motion

### Pace

**Intensity:**
- Brisk pace
- Conversation possible
- Heart rate elevated
- Sustainable effort
- Fitness benefit

## Foot Action

### Landing

**Ground contact:**
- Heel strikes first
- Roll through foot
- Push off toes
- Natural motion
- Efficient walking

## Power Walking

### Higher Intensity

**More effort:**
- Faster arm pump
- Quicker steps
- More core engagement
- Elevated heart rate
- Near jogging effort

### Hip Movement

**Additional motion:**
- More hip rotation
- Extends stride
- Speed addition
- Natural increase
- Racewalking influence

## Terrain

### Hills

**Incline walking:**
- Shorter stride up
- Lean into hill
- Arms drive harder
- Sustainable effort
- Good training

### Various Surfaces

**Different ground:**
- Sidewalks common
- Trails for variety
- Track for consistency
- Mall for weather
- Options available

Good fitness walking posture maximizes the health benefits of walking.
