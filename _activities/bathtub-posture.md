# Bathtub Posture

Bathtub soaking offers relaxation. Proper positioning maximizes comfort and safety.

## Getting In

### Safe Entry

**Into tub:**
- Hold support
- Step carefully
- Lower gradually
- Controlled descent
- Safe entry

### Water Level

**Before entry:**
- Appropriate depth
- Comfortable temperature
- Ready to soak
- Safe level
- Prepared bath

## Soaking Position

### Reclined Position

**Relaxed soak:**
- Back supported
- Neck comfortable
- Arms relaxed
- Legs extended
- Full immersion

### Support Options

**Comfort additions:**
- Bath pillow
- Rolled towel
- Neck support
- Comfortable head
- Enhanced relaxation

## During Bath

### Relaxation

**Enjoying soak:**
- Deep breathing
- Muscle relaxation
- Stress release
- Self-care time
- Mindful moment

### Reading or Screens

**Entertainment:**
- Keep electronics safe
- Raise book/tablet
- Comfortable viewing
- Protected devices
- Relaxed activity

## Getting Out

### Safe Exit

**Leaving tub:**
- Careful standing
- Hold support
- Drain water first optional
- Steady yourself
- Towel ready

### Wet Surfaces

**Exit safety:**
- Non-slip mat outside
- Careful stepping
- Secure footing
- Take your time
- Safe exit

## Extended Baths

### Long Soaks

**Duration:**
- Monitor temperature
- Add hot water carefully
- Don't overdo
- Stay hydrated
- Listen to body

Good bathtub posture enables safe, relaxing bath experiences.
