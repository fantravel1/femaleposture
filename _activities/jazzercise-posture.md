# Jazzercise Posture

Jazzercise combines jazz dance with fitness. Proper posture creates effective, injury-free workouts.

## Jazz Foundation

### Dance Stance

**Basic position:**
- Feet shoulder-width
- Knees soft
- Core engaged
- Arms ready
- Head lifted

### Movement Quality

**Jazz style:**
- Sharp and smooth
- Controlled power
- Expressive movement
- Full body engagement
- Musicality

## Basic Movements

### Jazz Walk

**Moving with style:**
- Toe to heel
- Hip emphasis
- Shoulders stable
- Arms styling
- Character expression

### Isolations

**Body part focus:**
- Head movements
- Shoulder hits
- Hip accents
- Controlled isolation
- Jazz expression

## Cardio Portions

### High Energy

**Workout phase:**
- Maintain form
- Full range motion
- Modify if needed
- Keep moving
- Challenge yourself

### Transitions

**Between sections:**
- Stay ready
- Controlled movement
- Breath management
- Form maintained
- Ready for change

## Strength Integration

### Muscle Work

**Toning sections:**
- Proper form priority
- Controlled reps
- Core engaged
- Full range
- Mind-muscle connection

## Cool Down

### Recovery

**End of class:**
- Slower movement
- Stretch integration
- Breath focus
- Form maintained
- Full recovery

Good Jazzercise posture enables energetic, safe dance fitness.
