# Shaving Posture

Shaving involves reaching various body areas. Proper posture enables safe, effective hair removal.

## Shower/Bath Setup

### Stable Footing

**Essential safety:**
- Non-slip mat
- Secure stance
- Don't rush
- Safe reaching
- Stability first

### Foot Rest

**Leg shaving:**
- Ledge or stool
- Raises leg
- Easier access
- Balanced position
- Better angles

## Leg Shaving

### Standing Technique

**In shower:**
- One foot raised
- Knee bent
- Comfortable reach
- Alternate legs
- Stable base

### Seated Option

**In bath:**
- Edge of tub
- Legs accessible
- Relaxed position
- More comfortable
- Take your time

## Underarm Shaving

### Arm Position

**Overhead reach:**
- Arm raised
- Good access
- Quick motion
- Alternate arms
- Brief position

## Other Areas

### Various Reaches

**Body shaving:**
- Mirrors help
- Careful positioning
- Don't strain
- Take time
- Safe technique

## Post-Shaving

### Moisturizing

**After care:**
- Standing or seated
- Comfortable application
- Reach all areas
- Thorough coverage
- Self-care complete

## Safety

### Avoiding Cuts

**Careful technique:**
- Sharp razor
- Appropriate pressure
- Don't rush
- Good visibility
- Focus on task

Good shaving posture enables safe, effective body hair removal.
