# Bocce Ball Posture

Bocce ball is social lawn bowling. Proper posture enables accurate throws and enjoyable play.

## Throwing Position

### Stance

**Ready to throw:**
- Feet comfortable
- Balanced position
- Ball in hand
- Target focused
- Relaxed ready

### Approach

**Stepping:**
- Optional step
- Forward motion
- Controlled approach
- Balanced delivery
- Natural movement

## Throwing Technique

### Underhand Delivery

**Standard throw:**
- Underhand motion
- Smooth release
- Follow through
- Controlled speed
- Accurate aim

### Loft vs Roll

**Shot selection:**
- Roll for distance
- Loft over obstacles
- Terrain reading
- Strategic choice
- Skill development

## Target Assessment

### Reading the Court

**Before throwing:**
- Note pallino position
- See other balls
- Choose strategy
- Plan the shot
- Thoughtful play

### Distance Control

**Speed management:**
- Practice needed
- Court surface matters
- Consistent delivery
- Feel development
- Accuracy over power

## Playing Comfort

### Relaxed Game

**Social aspect:**
- Enjoyable pace
- Conversation friendly
- Light exercise
- Outdoor enjoyment
- All ages welcome

### Bending

**Picking up balls:**
- Squat or bend properly
- Frequent movement
- Good stretching
- Part of activity
- Keep comfortable

Good bocce ball posture enables accurate, enjoyable lawn bowling.
