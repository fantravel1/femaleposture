# Skincare Routine Posture

Skincare routines involve mirror work. Proper posture enables comfortable daily care.

## Mirror Position

### Bathroom Setup

**At sink:**
- Face mirror
- Comfortable distance
- Good lighting
- Relaxed position
- Efficient access

### Seated Option

**If available:**
- Vanity seat
- More comfortable
- Less fatigue
- Extended routines
- Better for detail

## Application Techniques

### Standing Application

**Quick routine:**
- Face mirror
- Arms relaxed
- Quick motions
- Efficient products
- Brief duration

### Massage Techniques

**Facial massage:**
- Both hands
- Upward motions
- Relaxed shoulders
- Brief arms up
- Self-care focus

## Multi-Step Routines

### Extended Care

**Many products:**
- Efficient order
- Products accessible
- Don't rush
- Enjoy the routine
- Self-care time

### Wait Times

**Between products:**
- Step back
- Lower arms
- Relax position
- Return when ready
- Use time wisely

## Morning vs Evening

### Quick Morning

**Efficiency focus:**
- Streamlined routine
- Quick application
- Minimal standing
- Get ready efficiently
- Time management

### Extended Evening

**Self-care focus:**
- Take your time
- Seated if possible
- Relaxing ritual
- Pamper yourself
- Enjoy the process

Good skincare routine posture enables comfortable daily self-care.
