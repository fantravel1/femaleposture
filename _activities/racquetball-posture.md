# Racquetball Posture

Racquetball offers fast-paced action in an enclosed court. Proper posture enables quick reactions and powerful shots in this dynamic sport.

## Ready Position

### Center Court Stance

**Prepared position:**
- Feet shoulder-width
- Knees bent
- Weight forward
- Racquet ready
- 360-degree awareness

### Athletic Posture

**Movement-ready:**
- Low center of gravity
- Core engaged
- Eyes tracking ball
- Quick reactions
- Balanced stance

## Stroke Mechanics

### Forehand

**Power drive:**
- Rotation setup
- Hip initiation
- Full swing
- Follow through
- Balance recovery

### Backhand

**Opposite side:**
- Shoulder turn
- Compact swing
- Wrist snap
- Quick execution
- Ready position

### Ceiling Shot

**Defensive play:**
- Under ball
- Upward stroke
- Soft touch
- High trajectory
- Court recovery

## Court Awareness

### Wall Play

**Four-wall dynamics:**
- Ball trajectory reading
- Positioning decisions
- Anticipation
- Quick adjustment
- Strategic placement

### Movement Patterns

**Court coverage:**
- Efficient paths
- Quick pivots
- Center recovery
- Explosive movement
- Balanced transitions

## Women-Specific Considerations

### Physical Factors

**Female players:**
- High intensity workout
- Speed development
- Power building
- Eye protection essential
- Great cardio

Good racquetball posture enables fast reactions and powerful shots in the enclosed court environment.
