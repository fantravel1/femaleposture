# Tango Posture

Tango dancing demands intimate connection and precise movement. Proper posture enables authentic tango.

## The Embrace

### Close Embrace

**Connection:**
- Chest connection
- Relaxed arms
- Core engaged
- Head slightly left
- Unified movement

### Open Embrace

**Space for movement:**
- Frame maintained
- Core connected
- Arms defining space
- Visual connection
- Movement freedom

## Walking

### Tango Walk

**Foundation:**
- Reaching legs
- Ground connection
- Sequential transfer
- Elegant carriage
- Musical timing

### Forward Steps

**Women's walk:**
- Back steps often
- Extended reach
- Delayed transfer
- Core stable
- Elegant lines

### Back Walk

**Controlled retreat:**
- Core lifts
- Leg extends
- Controlled arrival
- Balance maintained
- Connection preserved

## Ochos

### Forward Ochos

**Pivoting pattern:**
- Hip rotation
- Core initiated
- Pivot on standing leg
- Arms follow
- Figure eight path

### Back Ochos

**Reverse pattern:**
- Core rotation
- Controlled pivot
- Weight transfer
- Elegant motion
- Complete rotation

## Expression

### Musicality

**Dancing the music:**
- Listen deeply
- Pause and accent
- Phrase expression
- Emotional connection
- Musical conversation

### Embellishments

**Personal style:**
- Leg adornments
- Subtle accents
- Within the lead
- Character expression
- Personal voice

Good tango posture creates intimate, musical partner connection.
