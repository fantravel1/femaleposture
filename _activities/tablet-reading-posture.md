# Tablet Reading Posture

Tablet reading is common activity. Proper posture supports comfortable, sustained digital reading.

## Holding Tablet

### Weight Distribution

**Tablet in hands:**
- Both hands
- Supported position
- Relaxed grip
- Comfortable hold
- Sustainable holding

### Propped Position

**Using stand:**
- Tablet stand preferred
- Case with stand
- Eye level viewing
- Hands free
- Comfortable reading

## Seated Reading

### Chair Position

**At desk or chair:**
- Upright posture
- Screen at good angle
- Arms supported
- Comfortable viewing
- Sustainable position

### Reclined Reading

**Relaxed position:**
- Back supported
- Tablet propped
- Comfortable angle
- Neck supported
- Extended reading comfort

## Bed Reading

### Lying Position

**In bed:**
- Prop tablet up
- Neck supported
- Not flat on back holding up
- Side lying okay
- Comfortable viewing

## Duration Management

### Break Taking

**Extended reading:**
- Regular breaks
- Look away
- Rest arms
- Stretch periodically
- Eye rest

### Blue Light

**Screen exposure:**
- Night mode
- Reduce eye strain
- Evening adjustments
- Sleep consideration
- Screen hygiene

Good tablet reading posture supports comfortable digital reading sessions.
