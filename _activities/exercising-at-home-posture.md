# Home Exercise Posture

Working out at home lacks the mirrors and instructor feedback of gyms. Proper body awareness maintains form during home workouts.

## Floor Exercise

### Mat Work

**Ground exercises:**
- Proper mat for cushion
- Aligned positioning
- Core engagement
- Breathing focus
- Form awareness

### Plank Positions

**Holding form:**
- Straight line head to heels
- Core tight
- No sagging
- No piking
- Shoulder stability

### Core Work

**Ab exercises:**
- Neck protected
- No pulling on head
- Breathing pattern
- Lower back supported
- Quality over quantity

## Following Videos

### Form First

**When mirroring:**
- Slow down if needed
- Form over speed
- Know modifications
- Listen to your body
- Pause to reset

### Screen Position

**Watching workout:**
- Screen at good height
- Can see while moving
- Safe distance
- Clear movement space
- Good lighting

## Standing Exercises

### Balance Work

**Without mirror:**
- Wall nearby
- Focal point
- Body awareness
- Progress gradually
- Safety first

### Strength Moves

**Proper form:**
- Body awareness crucial
- Core engagement
- Joint alignment
- Controlled movement
- Self-check periodically

## Environment

### Space Setup

**Safe workout area:**
- Clear floor space
- Stable flooring
- Equipment organized
- Good ventilation
- Appropriate temperature

Good home exercise posture requires heightened body awareness to maintain form without external feedback.
