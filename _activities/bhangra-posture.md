# Bhangra Posture

Bhangra is high-energy Punjabi folk dance. Proper posture enables powerful, joyful movement.

## Basic Stance

### Foundation

**Ready position:**
- Wide stance
- Knees bent
- Weight low
- Arms high
- Energy up

### Energy Level

**High intensity:**
- Full power
- Maximum expression
- Sustained energy
- Joyful movement
- Community celebration

## Signature Movements

### Shoulders

**Shoulder bounces:**
- Alternating or together
- Rhythmic motion
- Up and down
- Energy expression
- Core stability

### Arms

**Power arms:**
- Raised high
- Strong gestures
- Fist positions
- Wide movements
- Energy amplification

## Footwork

### Basic Steps

**Foundation:**
- Weight shifts
- Kicks and stamps
- Jumps and hops
- Rhythmic patterns
- Grounded power

### Jumps

**Elevation:**
- Explosive jumps
- Knees up
- Arms coordinate
- Landing controlled
- Energy maintained

## Upper Body

### Core Engagement

**Center of power:**
- Very engaged
- Enables jumps
- Supports arms
- Maintains energy
- Movement foundation

### Expression

- Big smile

- Community spirit
- Celebration
- Pure joy
