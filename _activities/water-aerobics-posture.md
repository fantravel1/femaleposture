# Water Aerobics Posture

Water aerobics offers low-impact exercise with water resistance. Proper posture maintains alignment in the buoyant environment.

## Pool Position

### Water Depth

**Appropriate level:**
- Chest or waist deep typical
- Feet touching bottom
- Balance possible
- Instructor visible
- Comfortable temperature

### Pool Floor

**Foot position:**
- Stable base
- Grounded when needed
- Awareness of slipping
- Aqua shoes helpful
- Balance practice

## Body Alignment

### In Water

**Upright posture:**
- Core engaged
- Shoulders back
- Head above water
- Natural alignment
- Buoyancy adjustment

### Maintaining Position

**Staying put:**
- Core stability
- Arm movements help
- Feet grounded
- Water resistance
- Balance work

## Exercise Form

### Upper Body

**Arm exercises:**
- Water provides resistance
- Full range of motion
- Controlled speed
- Breath above water
- Both directions work

### Lower Body

**Leg exercises:**
- Kicks and lifts
- Water resistance
- Balance challenge
- Core engagement
- Controlled movement

### Cardio Moves

**Aerobic exercises:**
- Jogging in place
- Jumping jacks
- Cross-country skiing
- Core engaged
- Proper form

## Women-Specific Benefits

### Joint Protection

**Low impact:**
- Buoyancy supports
- Joint-friendly
- Arthritis appropriate
- Injury recovery
- Pregnancy option

Good water aerobics posture maintains proper alignment despite water's buoyancy and resistance.
