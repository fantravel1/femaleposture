# Suitcase Packing Posture

Suitcase packing involves bending and organizing. Proper posture prevents strain during travel prep.

## Suitcase Position

### Optimal Placement

**For packing:**
- On bed or surface
- Comfortable height
- Open fully
- Easy access
- Good visibility

### Floor Packing

**If necessary:**
- Kneel or sit
- Comfortable position
- Avoid hunching
- Rise periodically
- Protect back

## Packing Technique

### Organized Approach

**Loading luggage:**
- Heavy items bottom
- Organized sections
- Rolling or folding
- Efficient use of space
- Systematic packing

### Reaching Into

**Deep suitcase:**
- Face suitcase
- Comfortable reach
- Don't overextend
- Position items
- Easy access

## Closing Suitcase

### Compression

**Closing up:**
- Press down evenly
- Use compression features
- Zip carefully
- Not overstuffed
- Secure closure

## Lifting Packed Suitcase

### Safe Lifting

**When packed:**
- Squat down
- Test weight
- Lift with legs
- Close to body
- Wheels help

## Multiple Bags

### Travel Preparation

**Several pieces:**
- Pack each efficiently
- Manage weights
- Rolling bags
- Organized packing
- Ready for travel

Good suitcase packing posture prevents strain during travel preparation.
