# Sitting Waiting Posture

Waiting while seated is common. Proper posture supports comfortable, patient waiting.

## Waiting Room

### Reception Area

**Seated waiting:**
- Upright posture
- Back against chair
- Feet on floor
- Relaxed but alert
- Patient waiting

### Variable Seating

**Different chairs:**
- Adapt to available
- Make best of it
- Maintain posture
- Comfortable as possible
- Patient sitting

## Duration

### Short Waits

**Brief waiting:**
- Standard seated posture
- Phone use okay
- Patient waiting
- Ready when called
- Attentive position

### Long Waits

**Extended waiting:**
- Shift positions
- Stand if possible
- Walk if appropriate
- Manage discomfort
- Patient endurance

## Mental Approach

### Patient Waiting

**Mindset:**
- Accept wait
- Use time well
- Relaxed attitude
- Comfortable position
- Positive approach

## Phone Use

### While Waiting

**Device use:**
- Raise phone up
- Reduce neck strain
- Pass time
- Posture aware
- Productive waiting

Good sitting waiting posture supports comfortable, patient waiting periods.
