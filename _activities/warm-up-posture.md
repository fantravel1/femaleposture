# Warm Up Posture

Warm up prepares the body for activity. Proper posture ensures effective preparation.

## Purpose

### Preparation

**Getting ready:**
- Increase blood flow
- Raise temperature
- Lubricate joints
- Activate muscles
- Mental readiness

### Injury Prevention

**Safety benefit:**
- Reduce injury risk
- Improve performance
- Better range of motion
- Enhanced coordination
- Optimal readiness

## Light Cardio

### General Warm-up

**Initial phase:**
- Light walking
- Gentle jogging
- Low intensity cycling
- Any easy cardio
- 5-10 minutes

### Heart Rate Rise

**Progressive:**
- Gradual increase
- Light sweat
- Breathing elevated
- Muscles warming
- Ready for movement

## Dynamic Movement

### Joint Circles

**Mobilization:**
- Ankles, hips, shoulders
- Full circles
- Both directions
- Gentle motion
- Prepare joints

### Movement Patterns

**Activity specific:**
- Related to workout
- Progressive intensity
- Full range motion
- Muscle activation
- Movement rehearsal

## Activation

### Muscle Prep

**Wake up muscles:**
- Light resistance
- Target areas
- Glute activation
- Core engagement
- Prepare for load

## Structure

### Effective Order

**Warm-up sequence:**
- Light cardio first
- Dynamic mobility
- Activation exercises
- Specific preparation
- Ready to perform

Good warm-up posture prepares the body for safe, effective exercise.
