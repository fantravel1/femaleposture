# Church Sitting Posture

Church services involve extended sitting. Proper posture supports comfortable, respectful attendance.

## Pew Seating

### Sitting Position

**On pew:**
- Sit upright
- Back against pew
- Feet on floor
- Hands in lap
- Attentive posture

### Pew Challenges

**Hard seating:**
- Cushion if available
- Shift occasionally
- Core engagement
- Comfort strategies
- Long service management

## Standing Portions

### Rising and Standing

**Service standing:**
- Rise smoothly
- Stand tall
- Hymnbook held
- Balanced stance
- Unified with congregation

### Returning to Seat

**Sitting again:**
- Lower controlled
- Smooth transition
- Quiet movement
- Ready to sit
- Respectful motion

## Kneeling

### Prayer Position

**When kneeling:**
- Kneel on kneeler
- Upright torso
- Hands folded
- Comfortable position
- Rise carefully

## Long Services

### Extended Sitting

**Hour or more:**
- Subtle shifts
- Core engagement
- Posture awareness
- Break at standing
- Comfortable duration

## Respectful Presence

### Attentive Posture

**During service:**
- Alert position
- Focused attention
- Engaged presence
- Appropriate posture
- Reverent bearing

Good church sitting posture supports comfortable, respectful worship attendance.
