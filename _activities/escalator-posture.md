# Escalator Posture

Escalators require balance and awareness. Proper posture ensures safe riding.

## Getting On

### Entry Timing

**Stepping on:**
- Watch step edges
- Time your step
- Hold handrail
- Quick transition
- Secure footing

### Foot Placement

**On escalator:**
- Center of step
- Both feet flat
- Stable position
- Secure stance
- Ready to ride

## Riding Position

### Standing Still

**During ride:**
- Hold handrail
- Balanced stance
- Upright posture
- Face forward
- Stay attentive

### Moving on Escalator

**If walking:**
- Keep right (or local custom)
- Hold rail
- Careful stepping
- Steady pace
- Safe movement

## Getting Off

### Exit Timing

**Stepping off:**
- Watch exit point
- Step cleanly off
- Move away quickly
- Clear for others
- Smooth transition

### Balance at Exit

**Transition:**
- Floor speed change
- Maintain balance
- Keep moving
- Clear area
- Safe exit

## Safety Awareness

### Attention Required

**While riding:**
- No distraction
- Hold rail
- Watch children
- Be aware
- Safe practice

Good escalator posture ensures safe, balanced riding on moving stairs.
