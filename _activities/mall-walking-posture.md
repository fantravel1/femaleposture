# Mall Walking Posture

Mall walking offers climate-controlled exercise. Proper posture maximizes fitness benefits.

## Walking Form

### Upright Posture

**Indoor walking:**
- Head up
- Shoulders back
- Core engaged
- Natural stride
- Confident pace

### Arm Swing

**Natural motion:**
- Arms swing naturally
- Relaxed shoulders
- Opposite arm to leg
- Comfortable movement
- No carrying bags

## Mall Environment

### Surface Benefits

**Indoor advantages:**
- Level floors
- No weather issues
- Temperature controlled
- Safe environment
- Consistent conditions

### Crowds Navigation

**Busy times:**
- Adjust pace
- Weave carefully
- Early morning less crowded
- Maintain form
- Stay aware

## Exercise Walking

### Fitness Focus

**Workout walking:**
- Brisk pace
- Elevated heart rate
- Purpose-driven
- Minimal stopping
- Exercise priority

### Distance Tracking

**Measuring progress:**
- Know lap distance
- Track laps
- Time your walks
- Set goals
- Progressive improvement

### Casual Pace

- Some exercise

## Social Walking
