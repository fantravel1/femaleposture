# Shuffleboard Posture

Shuffleboard involves precision sliding. Proper posture enables accurate puck placement.

## Table Shuffleboard

### Standing Position

**At the table:**
- Comfortable stance
- Close to edge
- Bent slightly forward
- Relaxed position
- Focused attention

### Push Technique

**Sliding puck:**
- Smooth motion
- Controlled speed
- Consistent release
- Follow through
- Feel the glide

## Deck/Floor Shuffleboard

### Using Cue

**With tang:**
- Proper stance
- Tang position
- Push motion
- Controlled power
- Aimed slide

### Body Position

**For push:**
- Bent forward
- Cue extended
- Smooth stroke
- Target focus
- Balanced stance
