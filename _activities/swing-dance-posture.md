# Swing Dance Posture

Swing dancing is energetic partner dancing. Proper posture enables playful, connected movement.

## Basic Stance

### Ready Position

**Foundation:**
- Weight forward
- Knees bent
- Core engaged
- Arms ready
- Energy up

### Triple Step

**Basic rhythm:**
- Ball of foot
- Quick-quick-slow
- Rhythm maintained
- Light and bouncy
- Energy flowing

## Connection

### Frame

**Partner contact:**
- Elastic connection
- Responsive frame
- Core engaged
- Arms connected
- Equal partnership

### Leading/Following

**Partnership:**
- Clear communication
- Responsive body
- Maintain connection
- Add personal style
- Have fun together

## East Coast Swing

### Basic Moves

**Foundation:**
- Rock step
- Triple step
- Maintained frame
- Rhythm constant
- Partner awareness

### Turns

**Spinning:**
- Core centered
- Arms guide
- Balanced rotation
- Complete movement
- Back to frame

## Lindy Hop

### Swing Out

**Core move:**
- Stretch and come back
- Hip engagement
- Rhythm changes
- Connection maintained
- Musical expression

### Charleston

**Vintage move:**
- Kick and step
- Partner or solo
- Energy high
- Playful expression
- Musical timing

## Expression

### Personal Style

**Making it yours:**
- Jazz steps
- Styling options
- Character expression
- Musical play
- Joy in movement

Good swing dance posture enables playful, connected swing dancing.
