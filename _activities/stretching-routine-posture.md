# Stretching Routine Posture

Stretching routines require proper form. Correct posture ensures effective, safe flexibility work.

## General Principles

### Alignment Matters

**During stretches:**
- Proper alignment
- Neutral spine base
- Controlled movement
- Breath awareness
- Safe practice

### Gradual Stretching

**Progression:**
- Ease into stretch
- No bouncing
- Hold adequately
- Gradual deepening
- Patient progress

## Standing Stretches

### Upright Form

**Standing stretches:**
- Balanced stance
- Core engaged
- Controlled movement
- Safe range
- Stable base

### Balance Needed

**One-leg stretches:**
- Hold support
- Controlled position
- Stable surface
- Safe practice
- Comfortable stretch

## Floor Stretches

**On floor:**
- Comfortable surface
- Proper positioning
