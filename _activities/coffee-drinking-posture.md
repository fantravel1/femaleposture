# Coffee Drinking Posture

Coffee and tea drinking are rituals. Proper posture supports comfortable, enjoyable beverage time.

## Mug Holding

### Comfortable Grip

**Holding mug:**
- Handle or wrap
- Relaxed grip
- Warm hands
- Comfortable hold
- Enjoyable drinking

### Hot Beverages

**Temperature care:**
- Careful sipping
- Not rushing
- Safe drinking
- Enjoyable temperature
- Patient consumption

## Drinking Position

### Sitting with Coffee

**Relaxed drinking:**
- Upright seated
- Mug to mouth
- Brief sips
- Comfortable position
- Ritual enjoyment

### Standing Coffee

**On the go:**
- Careful with hot
- Lid if walking
- Brief sips
- Safe drinking
- Mobile consumption

## Morning Ritual

### Coffee Time

**Daily practice:**
- Moment of calm
- Comfortable position
- Mindful sipping
- Enjoyable start
- Quality time

## At Desk

### Work Coffee

**While working:**
- Careful with spills
- Brief sips
- Mug accessible
- Work continues
- Hydration benefit

Good coffee drinking posture supports enjoyable, comfortable beverage rituals.
