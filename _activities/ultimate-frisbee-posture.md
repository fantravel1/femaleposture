# Ultimate Frisbee Posture

Ultimate frisbee combines throwing with athletic movement. Proper posture enables effective play.

## Throwing

### Backhand Throw

**Standard throw:**
- Disc across body
- Wrist snap release
- Step into throw
- Follow through
- Most common throw

### Forehand Throw

**Flick:**
- Different grip
- Sidearm motion
- Wrist snap
- Practice needed
- Essential skill

### Overhead Throws

**Advanced:**
- Hammer throw
- Scoober
- Specialty throws
- Situation specific
- Developed skill

## Catching

### Two-Hand Catch

**Secure catch:**
- Eyes on disc
- Two hands together
- Clamp down
- Secure immediately
- Most secure

### One-Hand Catch

**Layout or reach:**
- Extension catches
- Layout for dive
- Highlight play
- Risk assessment
- Athletic skill

## Running

### Cutting

**Getting open:**
- Explosive cuts
- Direction changes
- Timing with throw
- Clear space
- Get open

### Deep Routes

**Long passes:**
- Sprint form
- Eyes on disc
- Tracking ability
- Adjustment running
- Athletic play

## Defense

### Marking

**On handler:**
- Stay with player
- Force direction
- Arms ready
- Quick reactions
- Stall count

### Downfield

**Coverage:**
- Position play
- Zone or person
- Anticipation
- Athletic pursuit
- Team defense

Good ultimate frisbee posture enables dynamic disc sport play.
