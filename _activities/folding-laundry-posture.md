# Folding Laundry Posture

Laundry folding involves repetitive arm work. Proper posture prevents strain during clothes care.

## Folding Surface

### Height Matters

**Work surface:**
- Counter height ideal
- Table works
- Bed less ideal (too low)
- Comfortable reach
- Ergonomic setup

### Standing vs Sitting

**Position options:**
- Standing at counter
- Seated at table
- Choose comfort
- Alternate as needed
- Sustainable folding

## Folding Technique

### Arm Motion

**Folding action:**
- Relaxed arms
- Smooth movements
- Consistent technique
- Efficient motion
- Quality folds

### Repetitive Motion

**Managing repetition:**
- Vary movements
- Take breaks
- Alternate items
- Prevent strain
- Sustainable task

## Basket Handling

### Lifting Basket

**Moving laundry:**
- Lift with legs
- Close to body
- Partial loads
- Multiple trips
- Protect back

### Basket Position

**During folding:**
- At comfortable height
- Within reach
- Easy access
- Organized approach
- Efficient flow

## Putting Away

### After Folding

**Storage:**
- Carry comfortable loads
- Multiple trips
- Organized putting away
- Complete task
- Satisfying finish

Good laundry folding posture enables comfortable, efficient clothes care.
