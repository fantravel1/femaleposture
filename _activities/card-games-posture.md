# Card Games Posture

Card games require hand and seated position. Proper posture supports comfortable card playing.

## Holding Cards

### Hand Position

**Card grip:**
- Relaxed fingers
- Fanned view
- Comfortable hold
- Not too tight
- Easy manipulation

### Card Privacy

**Keeping hidden:**
- Cards to chest
- Natural position
- Relaxed hold
- Secure grip
- Strategic viewing

## Seated Position

### At Card Table

**Sitting setup:**
- Comfortable chair
- Good table height
- Close access
- Clear view
- Relaxed position

### Chips and Cards

**Managing elements:**
- Organized space
- Easy reach
- Comfortable access
- Smooth play
- Efficient handling

## Dealing

### Dealer Position

**When dealing:**
- Smooth motion
- Relaxed arm
- Consistent technique
- Comfortable reach
- Efficient distribution

## Long Sessions

### Extended Games

**Hours of cards:**
- Shift positions
- Take breaks
- Stand occasionally
- Stretch hands
- Sustainable play

### Hand Fatigue

**Holding cards long:**
- Card holder option
- Rest cards on table
- Relax grip between hands
- Prevent cramping
- Comfortable duration

## Social Gaming

### Casual Play

**Friendly games:**
- Relaxed posture
- Conversation friendly
- Open position
- Enjoyable atmosphere
- Comfortable gaming

Good card games posture supports comfortable, enjoyable card playing sessions.
