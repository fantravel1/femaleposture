# Hula Dance Posture

Hula dance tells stories through movement. Proper posture enables authentic Hawaiian expression.

## Basic Stance

### Foundation

**Grounded position:**
- Feet hip-width
- Knees bent
- Weight low
- Core engaged
- Connected to earth

### Hip Position

**Center of movement:**
- Hips free to sway
- Core supports
- Continuous motion
- Natural rhythm
- Flowing quality

## Hip Movement

### Kaholo Step

**Basic travel:**
- Side step
- Hip sways
- Weight shift
- Continuous motion
- Rhythmic pattern

### Ami

**Hip circle:**
- Full rotation
- Controlled motion
- Core engaged
- Beautiful line
- Expressive quality

## Hand Movements

### Storytelling

**Meaningful gestures:**
- Represent words
- Nature elements
- Emotional expression
- Graceful motion
- Cultural significance

### Hand Position

**Beautiful hands:**
- Soft curves
- Expressive fingers
- Natural movement
- Unified motion
- Connected to story

## Upper Body

### Carriage

**Graceful frame:**
- Shoulders relaxed
- Spine tall but soft
- Head follows hands
- Eye engagement
- Connected expression

## Cultural Context

### Authentic Practice

**Honoring tradition:**
- Learn meanings
- Respect culture
- Understand stories
- Practice with aloha
- Share respectfully

Good hula posture enables authentic Hawaiian storytelling through movement.
