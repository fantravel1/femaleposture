# Pet Grooming Posture

Pet grooming requires bending and reaching. Proper posture prevents strain during care.

## Brushing Pets

### Seated Brushing

**Small pets:**
- Pet on lap
- Comfortable seat
- Relaxed shoulders
- Easy reach
- Patient grooming

### Standing Brushing

**Larger pets:**
- Table height if possible
- Or kneel comfortably
- Access all areas
- Take breaks
- Alternate positions

## Bathing Pets

### Tub or Sink

**Washing:**
- Comfortable height
- Support back
- Keep pet secure
- Organized supplies
- Efficient process

### Reaching Into Tub

**Bending concerns:**
- Kneel beside tub
- Use bath kneeler
- Don't hunch over
- Keep close
- Protect back

## Nail Trimming

### Secure Hold

**Nail care:**
- Pet restrained gently
- Comfortable position
- Good lighting
- Steady hands
- Careful work

### Position Options

**Various approaches:**
- Pet on table
- Pet on lap
- Pet standing
- Helper holds
- Best visibility

## Ear and Eye Care

### Detailed Work

**Close attention:**
- Good lighting
- Steady hands
- Comfortable position
- Pet secured
- Gentle care

## Professional Grooming

### Drop-Off Option

**Full service:**
- Professional handling
- Saves your body
- Expert care
- Consider for complex grooming
- Worth investment

Good pet grooming posture enables thorough care without physical strain.
