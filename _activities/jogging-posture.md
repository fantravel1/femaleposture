# Jogging Posture

Jogging is relaxed running for fitness. Proper posture enables comfortable, sustainable effort.

## Body Position

### Upright Posture

**While jogging:**
- Tall spine
- Shoulders relaxed
- Slight forward lean
- Core engaged
- Natural position

### Head Position

**Looking ahead:**
- Eyes forward
- Chin level
- Neck relaxed
- Not looking down
- Natural alignment

## Foot Strike

### Natural Landing

**Ground contact:**
- Under hips
- Midfoot common
- Personal pattern
- Avoid overstriding
- Light landing

### Cadence

**Step rate:**
- Natural rhythm
- Quick turnover
- Short strides
- Sustainable effort
- Comfortable pace

## Arms

### Arm Swing

**Upper body:**
- 90 degree bend roughly
- Relaxed swing
- Don't cross midline
- Natural motion
- Efficient movement

### Hands

**Relaxed hold:**
- Loosely cupped
- Not clenched
- Relaxed fingers
- Natural position
- No tension

## Breathing

### Relaxed Breath

**Easy effort:**
- Conversational pace
- Natural breathing
- Belly breathing
- Relaxed effort
- Sustainable rate

## Enjoyment

### Recreational Running

**Fun focus:**
- Enjoyable pace
- Scenic routes
- Social running
- Fitness benefit
- Lifestyle activity

Good jogging posture enables relaxed, sustainable recreational running.
