# Meeting Posture

Meeting posture communicates engagement, confidence, and professionalism. Proper positioning supports your presence and participation.

## Seated Position

### Basic Alignment

**Professional sitting:**
- Sit fully in chair
- Back supported
- Feet flat on floor
- Shoulders back
- Core engaged

### Table Positioning

**At the table:**
- Arms on table naturally
- Lean forward slightly when engaged
- Hands visible
- Not slumped back
- Attentive posture

## Showing Engagement

### Active Listening

**Attentive posture:**
- Slight forward lean
- Eye contact
- Open body language
- Nodding when appropriate
- Responsive position

### When Speaking

**Contributing posture:**
- Sit up straighter
- Open gestures
- Eye contact around room
- Confident positioning
- Clear voice projection

## Long Meetings

### Sustainability

**Extended sitting:**
- Position shifts acceptable
- Stand if appropriate
- Move during breaks
- Stretch discretely
- Maintain core engagement

### Energy Management

**Staying alert:**
- Deep breaths
- Micro-movements
- Good sitting posture
- Active participation
- Awareness of fatigue signals

## Video Meetings

### Camera Position

**Virtual presence:**
- Camera at eye level
- Good lighting
- Professional background
- Seated upright
- Engaged expression

Good meeting posture supports your professional presence and participation throughout the discussion.
