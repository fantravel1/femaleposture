# Carrying Bags Posture

Carrying bags is daily activity. Proper technique prevents shoulder and back strain.

## Purse Carrying

### Shoulder Bag

**On shoulder:**
- Alternate sides
- Not too heavy
- Strap length matters
- Close to body
- Organized contents

### Crossbody Bag

**Diagonal wear:**
- Even weight distribution
- Proper strap length
- Switch sides periodically
- Comfortable fit
- Preferred style

### Backpack Style

**Two straps:**
- Best weight distribution
- Both shoulders carry
- Close to back
- Adjusted properly
- Healthiest option

## Shopping Bags

### Hand Carrying

**Multiple bags:**
- Balance both sides
- Don't overload
- Keep close to body
- Multiple trips better
- Know your limits

### Reusable Bags

**Ergonomic options:**
- Bags with good handles
- Crossbody shopping bags
- Rolling carts
- Backpack for shopping
- Better options

## Heavy Items

### Lifting Technique

**Picking up bags:**
- Bend at knees
- Keep back straight
- Close to body
- Use both hands
- Don't twist

### Carrying Distance

**How far:**
- Short distances
- Rest if needed
- Set down carefully
- Reposition grip
- Sustainable effort

## Prevention

### Lightening the Load

**Less is more:**
- Clean out purse regularly
- Only essentials
- Lighter wallet options
- Digital when possible
- Mindful packing

Good bag carrying posture prevents strain from daily carrying.
