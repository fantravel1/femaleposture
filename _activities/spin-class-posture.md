# Spin Class Posture

Spin classes combine cycling with music and motivation. Proper posture handles intense varied workouts.

## Setup

### Bike Adjustment

**Before class:**
- Seat height checked
- Handlebar position set
- Resistance accessible
- Shoe clips engaged
- Water accessible

### Warm-up Position

**Starting easy:**
- Light resistance
- Comfortable cadence
- Body warming
- Form established
- Ready for work

## Class Positions

### Seated Flat

**Base position:**
- Core engaged
- Shoulders relaxed
- Steady cadence
- Controlled breath
- Sustainable effort

### Seated Climb

**Added resistance:**
- Heavier resistance
- Lower cadence
- Power push
- Core very engaged
- Climbing simulation

### Standing Flat

**Speed work:**
- Out of saddle
- Quick cadence
- Light resistance
- Core stable
- Controlled motion

### Standing Climb

**Power work:**
- Heavy resistance
- Slow powerful
- Core engaged
- Full body effort
- Maximum exertion

## Instructor Cues

### Following Along

**In class:**
- Modify as needed
- Honor your body
- Stay safe
- Match effort level
- Enjoy the music

## Recovery

### Rest Periods

**Between intervals:**
- Active recovery
- Catch breath
- Light pedaling
- Prepare for next
- Quick recovery

Good spin class posture enables safe, effective group cycling workouts.
