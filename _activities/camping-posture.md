# Camping Posture

Camping presents unique postural challenges. Proper technique enables comfortable outdoor experiences.

## Sleeping

### Ground Sleeping

**On tent floor:**
- Quality sleeping pad
- Pillow essential
- Side sleeping often
- Maintain alignment
- Layers for comfort

### Pad Selection

**Sleep surface:**
- Thickness matters
- Insulation value
- Comfort level
- Weight consideration
- Personal preference

## Sitting

### Camp Chairs

**Outdoor seating:**
- Use available support
- Sit upright
- Don't slouch deep
- Change positions
- Use what you have

### Ground Sitting

**No furniture:**
- Pad underneath
- Cross-legged or extended
- Back support if available
- Change position often
- Core engagement

## Camp Tasks

### Cooking

**At camp stove:**
- Comfortable height
- Squat or kneel
- Safe distance
- Efficient setup
- Careful of fire

### Lifting Gear

**Handling equipment:**
- Proper lifting technique
- Cooler management
- Tent setup
- Share heavy items
- Pace yourself

## Backpacking

### Load Carrying

**Heavy pack:**
- Hip belt essential
- Weight on hips
- Proper fit
- Chest strap
- Adjusted for comfort

### Trail Posture

**While hiking:**
- Upright with pack
- Use trekking poles
- Core engaged
- Steady pace
- Rest when needed

## Recovery

### End of Day

**After activities:**
- Stretch
- Position for sleep
- Address discomfort
- Next day prep
- Rest well

Good camping posture enables comfortable outdoor living.
