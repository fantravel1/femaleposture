# Leaf Peeping Posture

Fall foliage viewing combines driving and walking. Proper posture supports comfortable autumn outings.

### Car Posture

**Extended driving:**
- Good seat position
- Back supported
- Arms comfortable
- Regular stops
- Comfortable journey

### Viewing Stops

**Pull-off areas:**
- Exit car carefully
- Stretch when stopped
- Enjoy views
- Brief walks
- Break from sitting

## Walking Trails

### Hiking Form

**Nature walks:**
- Upright posture
- Steady pace

**Looking at trees:**

- Don't strain
- Pause to look
- Comfortable viewing
