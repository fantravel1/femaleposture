# FaceTiming Posture

Video calls on phones require positioning. Proper posture supports comfortable, flattering calls.

## Phone Position

### Camera Angle

**Optimal view:**
- Eye level or above
- Propped if possible
- Flattering angle
- Good lighting
- Stable position

### Holding Phone

**Extended calls:**
- Support arm
- Prop phone
- Not constantly holding
- Comfortable position
- Sustainable calls

## Sitting for Calls

### Comfortable Position

**Video chat sitting:**
- Upright posture
- Face in frame
- Good background
- Comfortable seat
- Extended call ready

### Phone Stand

**Propped option:**
- Hands free
- Stable image
- Comfortable viewing
- Natural conversation
- Better experience

## Moving Calls

### Walking and Talking

**On the move:**
- Careful walking
- Stable phone
- Watch surroundings
- Brief movement
- Safe practice

## Call Duration

### Long Calls

**Extended chatting:**
- Change positions
- Take breaks
- Prop phone
- Sustainable talking
- Comfortable calls

Good FaceTiming posture supports comfortable, natural video conversations.
