# Karate Posture

Karate emphasizes powerful striking with traditional form. Proper posture enables effective technique.

## Stances

### Zenkutsu-dachi

**Front stance:**
- Forward weight
- Deep bend front knee
- Back leg straight
- Hip square or angled
- Strong foundation

### Kokutsu-dachi

**Back stance:**
- Weight back
- Defensive ready
- Mobile position
- Balance maintained
- Quick to counter

### Kiba-dachi

**Horse stance:**
- Wide base
- Even weight
- Knees bent outward
- Core engaged
- Training stance

## Strikes

### Punches

**Tsuki:**
- From hip
- Rotate on extension
- Pull opposite hand
- Hip drive
- Focus at target

### Blocks

**Uke:**
- Defensive technique
- Various angles
- Set up counter
- Core stability
- Prepared position

### Kicks

**Keri:**
- Chamber position
- Hip drive
- Various techniques
- Rechamber
- Balance recovery

## Kata

### Forms Practice

**Pattern training:**
- Precise technique
- Correct stances
- Breath coordination
- Fighting application
- Mental focus

## Kumite

### Sparring

**Application:**
- Distance management
- Timing development
- Reaction training
- Control required
- Respect for partner

Good karate posture creates the foundation for powerful traditional technique.
