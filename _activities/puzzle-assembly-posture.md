# Puzzle Assembly Posture

Puzzle assembly involves seated concentration. Proper posture supports comfortable, extended sessions.

## Table Setup

### Work Surface

**Puzzle table:**
- Adequate size
- Comfortable height
- Good lighting
- Undisturbed space
- Organized area

### Seating Position

**Chair setup:**
- Comfortable chair
- Proper height
- Good posture
- Feet on floor
- Sustainable sitting

## Reaching Pieces

### Piece Access

**During assembly:**
- Pieces organized
- Within easy reach
- Sorted by type
- Comfortable access
- Efficient work

### Leaning Concerns

**Large puzzles:**
- Don't overreach
- Stand for far pieces
- Move around table
- Change positions
- Comfortable access

## Fine Motor Work

### Placing Pieces

**Precision work:**
- Relaxed hands
- Gentle placement
- Not forcing
- Patient fitting
- Satisfying clicks

### Sorting

**Organizing pieces:**
- Trays or cups
- Color grouping
- Edge separating
- Efficient sorting
- Comfortable process

## Extended Sessions

### Long Puzzling

**Hours of work:**
- Take breaks
- Stretch periodically
- Rest eyes
- Move around
- Sustainable sessions

### Posture Awareness

**During work:**
- Check position
- Correct hunching
- Shoulders down
- Back supported
- Comfortable duration

Good puzzle assembly posture enables enjoyable, comfortable puzzle completion.
