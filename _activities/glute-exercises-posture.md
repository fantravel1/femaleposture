# Glute Exercises Posture

Glute exercises build posterior strength. Proper posture ensures effective, safe glute training.

## Hip Thrusts

### Thrust Position

**Setup:**
- Back against bench
- Feet flat
- Hips to floor
- Drive through heels
- Squeeze at top

### Form Focus

**Technique:**
- Chin tucked
- Core engaged
- Full hip extension
- Controlled movement
- Quality contractions

## Glute Bridges

### Floor Bridge

**Bridge form:**
- Back on floor
- Feet flat
- Drive hips up
- Squeeze glutes
- Controlled return

### Single Leg

**Advanced:**
- One leg extended
- Drive through planted foot
- Hip stability
- Core engaged
- Balanced work

## Kickbacks

### Donkey Kicks

**Kickback form:**
- Quadruped position
- Kick leg back
- Squeeze at top
- Controlled movement
- Core stable

## Romanian Deadlift

### Hip Hinge Pattern

**RDL form:**
- Hips back
- Slight knee bend
- Feel hamstring stretch
- Hip drive return
- Glute and hamstring

## Mind-Muscle Connection

### Activation

**Feeling glutes:**
- Focus on target
- Quality over quantity
- Full contraction
- Proper engagement
- Effective training

Good glute exercise posture ensures effective posterior chain strengthening.
