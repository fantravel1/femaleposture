# Bathing Posture

Bathing involves reaching, bending, and slippery surfaces. Proper posture and technique keep you safe in the bathroom.

## Showering

### Standing Position

**In shower:**
- Stable stance
- Non-slip mat
- Core engaged
- Careful movements
- Balance awareness

### Reaching and Bending

**Shower movements:**
- Squat to reach low
- Wall for support
- Careful turning
- Slow movements
- Balance maintained

## Bath Tub

### Getting In

**Safe entry:**
- Hand hold if available
- One leg at a time
- Core engaged
- Watch footing
- Controlled descent

### Soaking Position

**In the tub:**
- Neck supported
- Comfortable position
- Not too long
- Safe water level
- Easy exit planned

### Getting Out

**Safe exit:**
- Use grab bar
- Rise carefully
- Non-slip mat outside
- Dry hands first
- Steady yourself

## Washing Routine

### Hair Washing

**Head position:**
- Don't arch back excessively
- Support neck when tilted
- Shower head adjustment
- Alternate methods
- Comfortable positioning

### Body Washing

**Reaching everywhere:**
- Tools for hard to reach
- Stable stance
- Balance maintained
- Careful bending
- Foot on ledge for legs

## Bathroom Safety

### Prevention

**Fall prevention:**
- Non-slip surfaces
- Grab bars
- Good lighting
- Careful entry/exit
- Temperature awareness

Good bathing posture keeps you safe during this daily routine in a potentially hazardous environment.
