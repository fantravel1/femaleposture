# Flag Football Posture

Flag football offers football without tackling. Proper posture enables safe, effective play.

## Throwing

### Quarterback Form

**Passing:**
- Ball at ear
- Step into throw
- Hip rotation
- Arm whips
- Follow through

### Grip

**Holding ball:**
- Fingers on laces
- Secure grip
- Release for spiral
- Practice needed
- Consistent position

## Receiving

### Catching Position

**Ready to catch:**
- Eyes on ball
- Hands out
- Secure catch
- Turn upfield
- Protect flags

### Route Running

**Getting open:**
- Explosive cuts
- Sharp breaks
- Head turns
- Catch and run
- Create separation

## Running

### Ball Carrier

**With the ball:**
- Protect flags
- Change direction
- Speed variation
- Vision downfield
- Avoid pullers

### Flag Protection

**While running:**
- Arm over flags
- Legal protection
- Evasive moves
- Direction changes
- Strategic running

## Defense

### Flag Pulling

**Making stops:**
- Pursue runner
- Aim for flags
- Quick grab
- Pull cleanly
- Legal technique

### Coverage

**Pass defense:**
- Stay with receiver
- Eyes on ball
- Position for play
- Intercept opportunity
- Team defense

Good flag football posture enables safe, enjoyable recreational football.
