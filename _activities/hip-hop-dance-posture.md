# Hip-Hop Dance Posture

Hip-hop dance requires grounded, expressive movement. Proper posture enables authentic style.

## Foundation

### Base Stance

**Grounded position:**
- Feet wide
- Knees bent
- Weight low
- Core engaged
- Attitude present

### Groove

**Finding rhythm:**
- Body bounce
- Natural pulse
- Core engagement
- Relaxed upper
- Feel the beat

## Basic Movements

### Bounce

**Foundation move:**
- Knees pulse
- Core engaged
- Shoulders respond
- Head grooves
- Natural rhythm

### Rock

**Weight shift:**
- Side to side
- Front to back
- Controlled motion
- Hip engagement
- Musical timing

## Upper Body

### Chest Movement

**Isolation:**
- Pop and roll
- Controlled motion
- Core stability
- Shoulder support
- Sharp or smooth

### Arm Styling

**Hip-hop arms:**
- Sharp angles
- Smooth flows
- Character expression
- Full range
- Personal style

## Lower Body

### Footwork

**Foundation:**
- Quick steps
- Slides and pivots
- Grounded movement
- Weight control
- Rhythm matched

### Legs

**Power source:**
- Strong stance
- Quick action
- Deep bends
- Full extensions
- Controlled power

## Performance

### Character

**Expression:**
- Confidence
- Personal style
- Own the movement
- Attitude
- Enjoy the music

Good hip-hop posture creates grounded, expressive urban dance.
