# Laundry Basket Carrying Posture

Laundry basket carrying involves lifting and balance. Proper posture prevents strain during transport.

## Lifting Basket

### Safe Pickup

**Lifting technique:**
- Squat down
- Grip handles
- Back straight
- Lift with legs
- Controlled rise

### Weight Assessment

**Before lifting:**
- Partial loads lighter
- Full baskets heavy
- Multiple trips option
- Manage weight
- Safe lifting

## Carrying Position

### Hip Carry

**One-sided:**
- Common but asymmetric
- Brief duration only
- Switch sides
- Not ideal
- Convenient option

### Front Carry

**Better option:**
- Basket in front
- Both arms hold
- More balanced
- Visible path
- Preferred method

## Stair Navigation

### With Basket

**On stairs:**
- Careful footing
- See steps
- One hand on rail option
- Partial loads
- Safe navigation

### Visibility

**Seeing path:**
- Basket doesn't block view
- Careful stepping
- Take time
- Safety first
- Slow down

## Multiple Loads

### Efficient Approach

**Several trips:**
- Lighter loads
- Faster trips
- Protect body
- Total time similar
- Safer method

Good laundry basket carrying posture prevents strain during household transport.
