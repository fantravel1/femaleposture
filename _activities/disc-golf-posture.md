# Disc Golf Posture

Disc golf combines throwing with walking. Proper posture enables accurate throws and comfortable play.

## Backhand Throw

### Stance

**Ready position:**
- Staggered stance
- Weight on back foot
- Core loaded
- Arm ready
- Target focus

### Throwing Motion

**Full throw:**
- Hip rotation
- Core engagement
- Arm follows body
- Snap at release
- Follow through

### Follow Through

**Completing throw:**
- Full rotation
- Balanced finish
- Controlled motion
- Recovery position
- Ready for next

## Forehand Throw

### Sidearm Grip

**Different technique:**
- Grip disc firmly
- Wrist position
- Sidearm motion
- Snap release
- Alternative throw

### When to Use

**Strategic choice:**
- Curve opposite direction
- Certain obstacles
- Personal preference
- Shot selection
- Variety advantage

## Putting

### Approach

**Near basket:**
- Square to target
- Balanced stance
- Smooth motion
- Consistent release
- Focused aim

### Push vs Spin

**Putting styles:**
- Push putt
- Spin putt
- Personal preference
- Consistent practice
- Accuracy focus

## Course Walking

### Between Holes

**Traversing course:**
- Comfortable walking
- Disc bag carried
- Cart if preferred
- Terrain navigation
- Enjoyable pace

## Benefits

### Full Sport

**Why disc golf:**
- Walking exercise
- Throwing activity
- Strategic thinking
- Outdoor enjoyment
- Social or solo

Good disc golf posture enables accurate throws and enjoyable course play.
