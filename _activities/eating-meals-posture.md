# Eating Meals Posture

Eating meals is daily activity. Proper posture supports comfortable, mindful dining.

## At Table

### Seated Position

**Dining posture:**
- Sit upright
- Close to table
- Feet on floor
- Relaxed shoulders
- Comfortable eating

### Table Height

**Comfortable eating:**
- Arms comfortable
- Plate accessible
- No reaching
- Natural position
- Enjoyable meals

## Eating Form

### Bringing Food Up

**To mouth:**
- Sit upright
- Bring food to mouth
- Don't hunch to plate
- Utensils at comfortable angle
- Comfortable eating

### Between Bites

**Resting:**
- Utensils down
- Upright posture
- Chewing thoroughly
- Mindful eating
- Relaxed pace

## Casual Eating

### Couch Eating

**Less formal:**
- Plate secure
- Comfortable position
- Still mindful
- Occasional okay
- Balance with table

### Desk Eating

**Working lunch:**
- Take breaks from screen
- Focus on food
- Upright posture
- Mindful eating
- Quality break

## Mindful Dining

### Presence

**During meals:**
- Focus on food
- Slow eating
- Good posture
- Enjoyable experience
- Healthy habit

Good eating posture supports mindful, comfortable meal experiences.
