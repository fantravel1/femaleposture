# Machine Training Posture

Weight machines guide movement patterns. Proper setup and posture maximize benefits.

## Machine Setup

### Seat Adjustment

**Proper positioning:**
- Joint alignment critical
- Check axis points
- Individual sizing
- Comfortable position
- Full range enabled

### Weight Selection

**Appropriate load:**
- Start lighter
- Form first
- Progressive increase
- Controlled reps
- Safe challenge

## Upper Body Machines

### Chest Press

**Pushing:**
- Back against pad
- Handles at chest height
- Full extension
- Controlled return
- Shoulder alignment

### Lat Pulldown

**Pulling:**
- Thigh pad secure
- Full reach
- Pull to chest
- Squeeze back
- Controlled return

### Shoulder Press

**Overhead:**
- Seated stable
- Core engaged
- Full lockout
- Controlled descent
- Shoulder safety

## Lower Body Machines

### Leg Press

**Pushing:**
- Back flat against pad
- Foot position varied
- Full range
- No knee lockout
- Controlled motion

### Leg Extensions

**Quad isolation:**
- Pad position correct
- Controlled lift
- Full contraction
- Slow lower
- Knee safety

### Leg Curls

**Hamstring work:**
- Lying or seated
- Pad position correct
- Full curl
- Controlled extension
- Even resistance

## Benefits

### Machine Advantages

**Why use machines:**
- Guided movement
- Isolation possible
- Beginner friendly
- Rehabilitation value
- Supplement training

Good machine training posture ensures safe, effective exercise on gym equipment.
