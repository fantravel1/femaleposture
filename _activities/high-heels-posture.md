# High Heels Posture

High heels change body mechanics. Proper posture enables comfortable, confident wearing.

## Standing Posture

### Weight Distribution

**In heels:**
- Weight balanced
- Not all on toes
- Use whole foot
- Core engaged
- Upright stance

### Alignment

**Body position:**
- Hips over feet
- No leaning back
- Shoulders over hips
- Core support
- Tall posture

## Walking Technique

### Step Pattern

**Heel walking:**
- Heel to toe (as able)
- Shorter steps
- Confident stride
- Core engaged
- Eyes forward

### Pace

**Speed:**
- Slower than flats
- Controlled steps
- Don't rush
- Confident pace
- Safe walking

## Common Issues

### Pain Prevention

**Comfort strategies:**
- Limit wear time
- Cushioned insoles
- Proper fit
- Break in gradually
- Know your limits

### Muscle Fatigue

**Managing strain:**
- Take sitting breaks
- Stretch calves after
- Alternate heel heights
- Bring backup shoes
- Listen to body

## Event Wearing

### Special Occasions

**Long wear:**
- Sit when possible
- Dance breaks
- Flat shoe backup
- Plan for comfort
- Enjoy the event

## Driving

### Car Considerations

**Driving in heels:**
- Consider flats for driving
- Safety first
- Heel can catch
- Keep alternate shoes
- Safe practice

## Health Impact

### Long-Term Considerations

**Moderation:**
- Limit frequency
- Vary heel heights
- Foot exercises
- Calf stretches
- Balance with flats

Good high heel posture enables occasional confident, comfortable wearing.
