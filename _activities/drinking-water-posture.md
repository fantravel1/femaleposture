# Drinking Water Posture

Drinking water happens frequently. Proper posture supports comfortable, efficient hydration.

## Glass or Bottle

### Holding Position

**Drinking:**
- Bring container to mouth
- Head level or slight tilt
- Not extreme neck extension
- Comfortable drinking
- Efficient hydration

### Large Bottles

**Water bottles:**
- Two hands if heavy
- Controlled tipping
- Comfortable drinking
- Regular hydration
- Easy access

## Standing vs Sitting

### Either Position

**Hydrating:**
- Works seated
- Works standing
- Brief activity
- Frequent occurrence
- Natural motion

## Straw Use

### Drinking Straws

**Alternative:**
- Less head tilt
- Comfortable sipping
- Reusable straws
- Easy drinking
- Personal preference

## Frequency

### Regular Hydration

**Throughout day:**
- Frequent drinking
- Easy access
- Water available
- Healthy habit
- Good hydration

## At Desk

### Work Hydration

**While working:**
- Water within reach
- Brief breaks
- Regular sips
- Stay hydrated
- Healthy habit

Good drinking posture supports comfortable, frequent hydration throughout the day.
