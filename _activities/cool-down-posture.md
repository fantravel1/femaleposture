# Cool Down Posture

Cool down returns the body to rest. Proper posture enables effective recovery transition.

## Purpose

### Transition

**Returning to normal:**
- Gradual heart rate reduction
- Blood redistribution
- Breathing normalization
- Temperature regulation
- Mental transition

### Recovery Start

**Beginning recovery:**
- Muscle relaxation
- Waste product clearance
- Flexibility work
- Body scan
- Well-being check

## Light Movement

### Walking

**Gentle activity:**
- Slow walk
- Gradual slowing
- Arms swinging
- Breathing normalizing
- Heart rate dropping

### Easy Movement

**Gentle motion:**
- Arm swings
- Gentle twists
- Easy steps
- Flowing motion
- Relaxed effort

## Stretching

### Static Holds

**Flexibility work:**
- Major muscle groups
- 15-30 seconds each
- Relaxed breathing
- Progressive depth
- Full body coverage

### Breathing Focus

**Relaxation:**
- Deep breaths
- Exhale to relax
- Body awareness
- Mental calm
- Recovery mindset

## Body Awareness

### Post-Workout Scan

**Checking in:**
- Any discomfort?
- Energy level
- Hydration status
- Nutrition need
- Overall feeling

## Importance

### Why Cool Down

**Benefits:**
- Reduces DOMS
- Prevents dizziness
- Improves flexibility
- Mental closure
- Recovery start

Good cool down posture facilitates effective post-workout recovery.
