# Ultra Running Posture

Ultra running extends beyond marathon distance. Proper posture sustains movement for hours.

## Sustainable Form

### Economy First

**Energy conservation:**
- Relaxed movement
- Minimal excess motion
- Efficient stride
- Patient pace
- Long-term thinking

### Variable Posture

**Changing terrain:**
- Adaptable form
- Hike when needed
- Run when able
- Read the course
- Smart decisions

## Terrain Adaptation

### Climbing

**Uphill sections:**
- Power hiking often faster
- Hands on knees if needed
- Steady effort
- Conserve energy
- Patient climbing

### Descending

**Downhill form:**
- Controlled speed
- Quick feet
- Protect muscles
- Use terrain
- Save quads

## Fatigue Management

### Form Degradation

**When exhausted:**
- Regular form checks
- Mental cues
- Basic mechanics
- Simplify motion
- Keep moving forward

### Rest and Refuel

**Aid stations:**
- Sit briefly if needed
- Stretch if helpful
- Eat and drink
- Reset mentally
- Continue journey

## Mental Posture

### Mind-Body Connection

**Long days:**
- Stay present
- Manage thoughts
- Body scan regularly
- Gratitude practice
- Enjoy the journey

### Problem Solving

**When struggling:**
- Identify issue
- Address specifically
- Adjust strategy
- Keep perspective
- Finish the journey

Good ultra running posture sustains movement across extreme distances.
