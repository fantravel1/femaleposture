# Wedding Guest Posture

Wedding attendance involves various postures. Proper form supports comfortable, respectful presence.

## Ceremony Seating

### Pew or Chair

**During ceremony:**
- Sit upright
- Attentive posture
- Respectful presence
- Quiet sitting
- Focused attention

### Standing Moments

**When standing:**
- Rise smoothly
- Stand tall
- Balanced stance
- Quiet movement
- Unified rising

## Photos

### Group Shots

**Posing:**
- Natural smile
- Good posture
- Flattering angle
- Follow photographer
- Confident presence

### Candid Moments

**Casual photos:**
- Natural position
- Relaxed posture
- Genuine expression
- Aware of cameras
- Comfortable being seen

## Reception

### Cocktail Hour

**Standing period:**
- Mingle comfortably
- Shift positions
- Find resting spots
- Comfortable shoes matter
- Pace yourself

### Seated Dinner

**At reception table:**
- Table manners posture
- Conversation position
- Long sitting comfort
- Good dining form
- Social engagement

## Dancing

### Dance Floor

**Reception dancing:**
- Move naturally
- Comfortable shoes
- Posture varies
- Enjoy movement
- Have fun

## Formal Wear

### Dress Considerations

**In formal attire:**
- Move in dress
