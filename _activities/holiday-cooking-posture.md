# Holiday Cooking Posture

Holiday cooking involves extended kitchen time. Proper posture prevents fatigue during feast prep.

## Long Standing

### Kitchen Marathon

**Hours of cooking:**
- Anti-fatigue mat
- Supportive shoes
- Shift positions
- Take sitting breaks
- Pace yourself

### Fatigue Management

**Preventing strain:**
- Alternate tasks
- Sit while prepping
- Strategic breaks
- Delegate tasks
- Sustainable pace

## Heavy Lifting

### Large Items

**Turkey, roasts:**
- Lift with legs
- Get help if needed
- Close to body
- Controlled placement
- Safe handling

### Oven Work

**Hot, heavy pans:**
- Square to oven
- Both hands
- Careful transfer
- Hot pads secure
- Safe movement

## Prep Work

### Extended Chopping

**Vegetable prep:**
- Counter height
- Cutting board stable
- Sharp knives
- Relaxed shoulders
- Efficient technique

### Mixing and Stirring

**Large batches:**
- Bowl stable
- Core engaged
- Alternate arms
- Take breaks
- Comfortable mixing

## Multiple Dishes

### Coordination

**Many dishes:**
- Organize workflow
- Strategic timing
- Efficient movement
- Kitchen flow
- Calm approach

## Cleanup

### End of Day

**After feast:**
- Pace cleanup
- Sit when possible
- Get help
- Don't overdo
- Tomorrow is fine too

Good holiday cooking posture supports enjoyable, manageable feast preparation.
