# Party Standing Posture

Party standing involves extended socializing. Proper posture projects confidence and prevents fatigue.

## Standing Form

### Confident Stance

**Party posture:**
- Upright position
- Shoulders back
- Open body language
- Balanced weight
- Approachable presence

### Weight Distribution

**Long standing:**
- Shift weight periodically
- Both feet grounded
- Alternate legs
- Prevent fatigue
- Comfortable stance

## Holding Drinks

### Glass Position

**With beverage:**
- One hand holds drink
- Not too high or low
- Relaxed arm
- Easy conversation
- Comfortable hold

### Plate Management

**With food:**
- Cocktail plate technique
- Balance manageable
- Accessible eating
- Graceful handling
- Practice helps

## Conversation Position

### Group Circles

**In groups:**
- Face speakers
- Open stance
- Not crossing arms
- Engaged posture
- Welcoming presence

### One-on-One

**Direct conversation:**
- Face person
- Comfortable distance
- Open body
- Attentive posture
- Connected presence

## Fatigue Management

### Long Events

**Extended standing:**
- Find resting spots
- Lean appropriately
- Sit when available
- Comfortable shoes help
- Pace yourself

### Break Taking

**Strategic rest:**
- Find seating
- Bathroom breaks
- Outside moments
- Brief sits
- Resume refreshed

Good party standing posture enables confident, comfortable social presence.
