# Taekwondo Posture

Taekwondo emphasizes spectacular kicking technique. Proper posture enables powerful, flexible movement.

## Stances

### Fighting Stance

**Sparring position:**
- Narrow base
- Weight on balls of feet
- Hands guard
- Light and mobile
- Ready to kick

### Traditional Stances

**Form positions:**
- Front stance
- Back stance
- Horse stance
- Precise angles
- Strong foundation

## Kicks

### Front Kick

**Basic kick:**
- Chamber high
- Extend with snap
- Ball of foot strikes
- Rechamber quickly
- Balance recovery

### Roundhouse Kick

**Signature kick:**
- Pivot support
- Hip rotation
- Instep or ball strike
- Full follow through
- Quick recovery

### Side Kick

**Powerful thrust:**
- Chamber position
- Hip drive
- Heel connection
- Blade of foot option
- Controlled power

### Spinning Kicks

**Advanced technique:**
- Spot the target
- Full rotation
- Power generation
- Balance crucial
- Practice required
