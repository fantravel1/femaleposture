# Chiropractic Visit Posture

Chiropractic visits involve specific positioning. Proper posture assists effective care.

## Exam Positions

### Standing Assessment

**Posture analysis:**
- Natural stance
- Arms at sides
- Normal posture
- Don't correct artificially
- Honest assessment

### Range of Motion

**Movement tests:**
- Follow directions
- Controlled movements
- Report limitations
- Honest feedback
- Note restrictions

## Adjustment Positions

### Table Positioning

**During adjustment:**
- Follow instructions
- Relaxed muscles
- Trust the process
- Breathe normally
- Don't tense up

### Face-Down

**Prone adjustments:**
- Comfortable position
- Relaxed breathing
- Arms positioned
- Head in cradle
- Full relaxation

### Side-Lying

**Lateral work:**
- Stable position
- Supported properly
- Relaxed muscles
- Follow guidance
- Cooperative positioning

## During Adjustments

### Relaxation Key

**Mental approach:**
- Stay relaxed
- Don't anticipate
- Trust provider
- Breathe normally
- Don't resist

### Communication

**With chiropractor:**
- Report sensations
- Express concerns
- Ask questions
- Provide feedback
- Honest dialogue

## After Adjustment

### Post-Care

**Following visit:**
- Move normally
- Follow advice
- Hydrate well
- Note changes
- Schedule follow-up

Good chiropractic visit posture supports effective spinal care and adjustment.
