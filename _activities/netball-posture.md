# Netball Posture

Netball demands precise positioning. Proper posture enables effective team play.

## Basic Stance

### Ready Position

**On court:**
- Balanced stance
- Knees bent
- Arms ready
- Eyes on play
- Quick reaction ready

### Landing Rule

**After receiving:**
- Land balanced
- Pivot foot established
- Don't step with it
- Rule awareness
- Controlled landing

## Passing

### Chest Pass

**Basic pass:**
- Ball at chest
- Step into pass
- Arms extend
- Snap at release
- Follow through

### Shoulder Pass

**One-handed:**
- Ball by ear
- Step forward
- Throwing motion
- Power and distance
- Accurate delivery

### Bounce Pass

**Through defense:**
- Similar to chest
- Aim at ground
- Bounce to receiver
- Strategic use
- Deceptive pass

## Shooting

### Goal Position

**At circle:**
- Balanced stance
- Ball high
- Eyes on target
- Smooth motion
- Follow through

### Technique

**Shot mechanics:**
- Knees slightly bent
- Push from legs
- Arms extend
- Release at peak
- Consistent motion

## Defense

### Marking

**Defending player:**
- Three feet distance
- Arms ready
- Quick reactions
- Intercept opportunity
- Constant pressure

### Interceptions

**Stealing ball:**
- Read the play
- Anticipate pass
- Quick movement
- Clean take
- Transition to offense

Good netball posture enables effective passing, shooting, and defending.
