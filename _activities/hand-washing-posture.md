# Hand Washing Posture

Hand washing is frequent daily activity. Proper posture supports comfortable hygiene practice.

## Sink Position

### Standing Height

**At sink:**
- Comfortable standing
- Close to sink
- Not hunching
- Upright posture
- Brief activity

### Reaching Faucet

**Controls access:**
- Easy reach
- Comfortable use
- Temperature control
- Efficient operation
- Quick access

## Washing Technique

### Hand Position

**Under water:**
- Hands comfortable height
- Wrists neutral
- Thorough washing
- 20 seconds minimum
- Complete clean

### Soap Application

**Using soap:**
- One-hand pump
- Even distribution
- Lathering motion
- Complete coverage
- Effective washing

## Drying

### After Washing

**Drying hands:**
- Towel access easy
- Thorough drying
- Quick motion
- Complete task
- Ready hands

## Frequent Washing

### Multiple Times Daily

**Regular hygiene:**
- Quick practice
- Efficient technique
- Good habit
- Health benefit
- Sustainable practice

## Accessibility

### Comfortable Access

**Sink setup:**
- Appropriate height
- Easy controls
- Soap accessible
- Towels ready
- Efficient station

Good hand washing posture supports comfortable frequent hygiene practice.
