# Aerobics Posture

Aerobics classes offer fun, energetic workouts, but the fast pace can lead to poor form. Proper aerobics posture protects your body while you enjoy the music and movement.

## Basic Position

### Standing Posture

**Base alignment:**
- Feet hip-width
- Knees soft
- Core engaged
- Shoulders relaxed
- Head balanced

### Movement Posture

**While exercising:**
- Core stability
- Controlled movement
- Proper landing
- Breathing pattern
- Rhythm awareness

## Common Movements

### Step-Touch

**Side movement:**
- Weight transfer
- Controlled step
- Soft landing
- Core engaged
- Arm coordination

### Grapevine

**Lateral movement:**
- Smooth steps
- Controlled pace
- Core stability
- Arm swing
- Rhythm match

### Jumping Movements

**Impact work:**
- Soft landings
- Knee alignment
- Core bracing
- Arm involvement
- Proper breathing

## High vs Low Impact

### High Impact

**Jumping movements:**
- Both feet leave floor
- Higher intensity
- More joint stress
- Proper landing crucial
- Know your limits

### Low Impact

**Grounded option:**
- One foot always down
- Joint protection
- Still effective
- Modification option
- Appropriate choice

## Women-Specific Considerations

### Physical Factors

**Female exercisers:**
- Sports bra importance
- Joint protection
- Pelvic floor awareness
- Impact modifications
- Fun factor matters

Good aerobics posture lets you enjoy high-energy classes while protecting your joints and building fitness.
