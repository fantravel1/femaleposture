# Teeth Brushing Posture

Teeth brushing happens daily. Proper posture ensures comfortable, effective oral care.

## Standing Position

### At Sink

**Basic position:**
- Face mirror
- Comfortable stance
- Relaxed shoulders
- Arms at natural height
- Brief standing

### Don't Lean

**Common habit:**
- Many lean on sink
- Avoid if possible
- Stand upright
- Core engaged
- Better posture

## Brushing Technique

### Arm Position

**While brushing:**
- Elbow at side
- Wrist does work
- Relaxed motion
- Thorough cleaning
- Not tense

### Mirror Use

**Watching technique:**
- Check coverage
- Brief looking
- Not constant staring
- Feel the clean
- Efficient brushing

## Duration

### Two Minutes

**Recommended time:**
- Use timer
- Thorough coverage
- All surfaces
- Gumline care
- Complete clean

### Efficient Routine

**Quick but thorough:**
- Systematic approach
- Same pattern daily
- Complete coverage
- Twice daily
- Good habit

## Flossing

### Additional Care

**Complete oral care:**
- Stand comfortably
- Both hands
- Thorough coverage
- Mirror optional
- Essential habit

## Morning Rush

### Quick Routine

**When hurried:**
- Still do it
- Efficient technique
- Same quality
- Prioritize oral health
- Good habit maintained

Good teeth brushing posture supports daily oral hygiene routines.
