# Briefcase Carrying Posture

Briefcase carrying requires hand strength. Proper posture prevents strain from weighted carrying.

## Hand Carrying

### Grip Technique

**Holding briefcase:**
- Firm but relaxed
- Comfortable handle
- Arm at side
- Not too heavy
- Controlled carry

### Alternating Hands

**Switching sides:**
- Alternate regularly
- Balance strain
- Prevent asymmetry
- Both sides work
- Sustainable carrying

## Weight Considerations

### Contents Management

**Briefcase weight:**
- Essential items only
- Digital alternatives
- Regular cleanout
- Manageable load
- Protect joints

### Heavy Loads

**When weighted:**
- Switch hands often
- Take breaks
- Consider wheeled option
- Rolling briefcase
- Protect body

## Posture While Carrying

### Walking Form

**With briefcase:**
- Upright posture
- Arm swings limited
- Core engaged
- Balanced walking
- Professional bearing

### Shoulder Position

**Avoid imbalance:**
- Shoulders level
- Not leaning
- Balanced posture
- Core support
- Neutral position

## Alternative Options

### Wheeled Bags

**Rolling option:**
- No carrying strain
- Easy transport
- Professional styles
- Worth consideration
- Protect body

Good briefcase carrying posture prevents strain from professional bag transport.
