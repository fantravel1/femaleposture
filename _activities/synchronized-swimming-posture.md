# Synchronized Swimming Posture

Synchronized swimming combines swimming with artistic movement. Proper posture creates beautiful lines both above and below water.

## Basic Positions

### Vertical

**Upright in water:**
- Core extremely engaged
- Legs together
- Pointed toes
- Head aligned
- Sculling support

### Horizontal

**Surface positions:**
- Flat body line
- Core engaged
- Breath control
- Artistic expression
- Synchronized movement

## Leg Positions

### Straight Lines

**Extended legs:**
- Pointed toes
- Tight lines
- Core support
- Controlled movement
- Height above water

### Figures

**Artistic positions:**
- Flexibility demanded
- Core control
- Breath management
- Precise angles
- Team synchronization

## Underwater

### Propulsion

**Moving in water:**
- Sculling technique
- Eggbeater kick
- Core engagement
- Efficiency
- Breath management

### Inverted

**Upside down:**
- Core stability
- Breath control
- Orientation
- Artistic positions
- Safe surface

## Performance

### Artistic Expression

**Entertainment value:**
- Facial expression
- Movement quality
- Synchronization
- Character
- Stamina

## Training

### Physical Demands

**Building skills:**
- Swimming foundation
- Flexibility work
- Core strength
- Breath training
- Artistic development

Good synchronized swimming posture creates the beautiful lines that define this artistic sport.
