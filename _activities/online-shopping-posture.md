# Online Shopping Posture

Online shopping involves extended browsing. Proper posture supports comfortable digital shopping.

## Computer Shopping

### Desk Position

**At computer:**
- Standard desk posture
- Monitor at eye level
- Comfortable keyboard
- Relaxed shopping
- Ergonomic setup

### Mouse Use

**Clicking and scrolling:**
- Relaxed hand
- Supported arm
- Natural movement
- Efficient browsing
- Comfortable shopping

## Mobile Shopping

### Phone Position

**On smartphone:**
- Raise phone up
- Reduce neck drop
- Comfortable viewing
- Brief sessions
- Good positioning

### Tablet Shopping

**Larger screen:**
- Propped position
- Comfortable viewing
- Relaxed browsing
- Extended sessions okay
- Better than phone

## Duration Awareness

### Extended Browsing

**Long sessions:**
- Take breaks
- Stretch periodically
- Eye rest
- Move around
- Mindful shopping

### Impulse Prevention

**Take a break:**
- Step away
- Consider purchase
- Review cart
- Thoughtful shopping
- Intentional buying

Good online shopping posture supports comfortable digital commerce browsing.
