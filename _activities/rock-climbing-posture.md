# Rock Climbing Posture

Rock climbing demands precise body positioning on vertical terrain. Understanding proper climbing posture maximizes efficiency while preventing injury.

## Climbing Body Position

### Wall Position

**Basic principles:**
- Hips close to wall
- Arms straight when resting
- Feet driving movement
- Core engaged
- Eyes on route

### Balance Points

**Weight distribution:**
- Three points of contact
- Center of gravity awareness
- Foot placement priority
- Hip positioning
- Dynamic balance
