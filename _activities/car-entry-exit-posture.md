# Car Entry Exit Posture

Getting in and out of cars requires technique. Proper posture ensures graceful, safe vehicle entry.

## Entering Vehicle

### Sitting First

**Getting in:**
- Face away from seat
- Sit on seat edge
- Swing legs in together
- Smooth motion
- Controlled entry

### Tall Vehicles

**Higher entry:**
- Use step if available
- Hold door frame
- Controlled climbing
- Careful footing
- Safe entry

## Exiting Vehicle

### Legs First

**Getting out:**
- Swing legs out together
- Plant feet
- Push up to stand
- Smooth exit
- Controlled motion

### Low Cars

**Lower vehicles:**
- More effort rising
- Use door frame
- Core engaged
- Controlled rising
- Safe exit

## Considerations

### Dress and Clothing

**In skirts or dresses:**
- Legs together technique
- Modest exit
- Graceful motion
- Practiced technique
- Confident movement

### Safety Elements

**Door awareness:**
- Check surroundings
- Hold door securely
- Mind traffic
- Complete exit
- Safe practice

## Frequent Entry

### Commuting

**Daily practice:**
- Smooth technique
- Efficient motion
- Quick entry/exit
- Comfortable practice
- Routine perfected

Good car entry exit posture enables graceful, safe vehicle access.
