# Samba Posture

Samba is energetic Brazilian dance. Proper posture enables the characteristic bounce and hip action.

## Basic Stance

### Foundation

**Ready position:**
- Feet together
- Knees soft
- Hips free
- Core engaged
- Arms ready

### The Bounce

**Characteristic quality:**
- Continuous pulse
- Knee driven
- Rhythmic motion
- Natural bounce
- Energy foundation

## Hip Movement

### Samba Roll

**Hip action:**
- Alternating hips
- Continuous roll
- Core stable
- Natural motion
- Signature style

### Weight Transfer

**Moving weight:**
- Quick shifts
- Light feet
- Hip emphasis
- Continuous motion
- Rhythmic flow

## Basic Steps

### Samba Basic

**Foundation step:**
- Step together step
- Quick quick slow
- Hip roll continuous
- Arms natural
- Carnival rhythm

### Voltas

**Traveling step:**
- Crossing pattern
- Quick feet
- Hip emphasis
- Arm coordination
- Carnival movement

## Upper Body

### Arms

**Brazilian style:**
- Natural movement
- Character expression
- Carnival flair
- Coordinate with hips
- Add energy

### Expression

**Carnival spirit:**
- Joy and celebration
- Big energy
- Smile
- Community spirit
- Pure happiness

### Parade Dancing

**Big expression:**

Good samba posture creates the characteristic bounce and hip action of Brazilian dance.
