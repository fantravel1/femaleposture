# Decorating Posture

Home decorating involves reaching and arranging. Proper posture prevents strain during setup.

## Overhead Reaching

### High Placement

**Above head:**
- Use step stool
- Stable platform
- Don't overreach
- Controlled placement
- Safe reaching

### Ladder Safety

**When needed:**
- Secure placement
- Three points contact
- Don't lean too far
- Move ladder
- Safety first

## Tree Decorating

### Holiday Tree

**Ornament hanging:**
- Work around tree
- Bend at knees for low
- Step stool for high
- Move frequently
- Balanced decorating

### Light Stringing

**Tree lights:**
- Start at top
- Work down
- Alternate positions
- Take breaks
- Careful handling

## Mantle and Shelves

### Moderate Heights

**Eye level work:**
- Stand close
- Comfortable reach
- Organized approach
- Aesthetic adjustments
- Enjoyable process

## Hanging Decor

### Wall Placement

**Hanging items:**
- Proper tools
- Level use
- Appropriate height
- Secure hanging
- Safe process

## Table Settings

### Tablescapes

**Centerpieces:**
- Stand or sit
- Reach comfortably
- Arrange carefully
- Creative process
- Enjoyable decorating

## Duration Management

### Long Sessions

**Decorating day:**
- Take breaks
- Hydrate
- Vary activities
- Rest periodically
- Pace yourself

Good decorating posture enables safe, comfortable home beautification.
