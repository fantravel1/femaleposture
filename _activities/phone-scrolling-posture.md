# Phone Scrolling Posture

Phone scrolling is pervasive activity. Proper posture prevents "tech neck" and strain.

## Holding Phone

### Eye Level Goal

**Optimal position:**
- Raise phone up
- Eye level viewing
- Reduce head drop
- Arms supported
- Neck neutral

### Common Mistakes

**Avoid:**
- Head dropped forward
- Hunched posture
- Unsupported arms
- Extended duration
- Poor positioning

## Seated Use

### Chair Position

**Sitting with phone:**
- Back supported
- Arms rested
- Phone raised
- Comfortable viewing
- Sustainable position

### Couch Relaxation

**Casual use:**
- Props help
- Pillow support
- Raised viewing
- Relaxed position
- Neck aware

## Standing Use

### Brief Checks

**Quick viewing:**
- Raise phone
- Head stays level
- Brief duration
- Efficient use
- Good position

## Duration Limits

### Break Taking

**Extended use:**
- Regular breaks
- Look away
- Stretch neck
- Reset position
- Manage usage

### Screen Time

**Total use:**
- Aware of time
- Meaningful use
- Break habits
- Neck health
- Eye health

Good phone scrolling posture prevents tech neck and maintains neck health.
