# Dumbbell Training Posture

Dumbbell training offers versatile strength work. Proper posture enables effective, safe exercise.

## Upper Body Pressing

### Chest Press

**Bench work:**
- Shoulder blades retracted
- Slight arch
- Controlled descent
- Full range
- Core engaged

### Shoulder Press

**Overhead movement:**
- Seated or standing
- Core braced
- Press to lockout
- Controlled return
- Stable base

## Upper Body Pulling

### Rows

**Back development:**
- Bent over position
- Back flat
- Pull to hip
- Squeeze back
- Controlled return

### Curls

**Arm work:**
- Stable torso
- Controlled movement
- Full range
- No swinging
- Isolated effort

## Lower Body

### Lunges

**Leg work:**
- Dumbbells at sides
- Controlled step
- Knee tracking
- Core engaged
- Balanced movement

### Romanian Deadlift

**Hip hinge:**
- Dumbbells in front
- Hip hinge pattern
- Back flat
- Hamstring stretch
- Full extension

## Compound Movements

### Thrusters

**Full body:**
- Front rack position
- Squat depth
- Drive up
- Press overhead
- Continuous motion

## Form Principles

### General Guidelines

**Training quality:**
- Control the weight
- Full range motion
- Breathe properly
- Core engaged
- Mind-muscle connection

Good dumbbell training posture enables effective strength development.
