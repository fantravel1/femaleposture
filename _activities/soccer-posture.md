# Soccer Posture

Soccer demands running, kicking, and dynamic movement for extended periods. Understanding proper soccer posture improves performance and prevents injury.

## Running Form

### Soccer Running

**Field movement:**
- Upright torso
- Short, quick steps when needed
- Change of direction
- Balance maintenance
- Economy of motion

### Sprint Form

**Speed running:**
- Forward lean
- Arm drive
- High knees
- Quick turnover
- Efficient movement

## Kicking Mechanics

### Proper Form

**Kick posture:**
- Plant foot position
- Hip rotation
- Core engagement
- Follow through
- Balance recovery

### Different Kicks

**Technique variations:**
- Instep drive
- Inside foot pass
- Outside foot
- Volley
- Headers

## Defensive Posture

### Positioning

**Defensive stance:**
- Low center of gravity
- Ready to react
- Quick feet
- Body positioning
- Anticipation

## Women-Specific Considerations

### ACL Prevention

**Knee protection:**
- Landing mechanics
- Cutting technique
- Strength training
- Neuromuscular training
- Awareness

### Heading Considerations

**Safe heading:**
- Proper technique
- Neck strengthening
- Age-appropriate
- Limitations awareness
- Alternative options

Good soccer posture supports the endurance, power, and agility the sport demands while protecting vulnerable areas.
