# Pool Relaxation Posture

Poolside time involves lounging and water. Proper positioning enables comfortable relaxation.

## Pool Loungers

### Lying Position

**On lounge chair:**
- Reclined angle
- Head supported
- Comfortable level
- Shade if hot
- Adjustable position

### Sitting Up

**More upright:**
- Back support
- Feet comfortable
- Transition position
- Conversation friendly
- Reading possible

## In the Water

### Floating

**Relaxed floating:**
- Back float if able
- Arms out
- Head back
- Totally relaxed
- Trust the water

### Pool Edge

**Resting position:**
- Arms on edge
- Body vertical
- Relaxed floating
- Chat with others
- Low effort rest

## Pool Activities

### Light Swimming

**Relaxed laps:**
- Easy stroke
- Comfortable breathing
- Not for workout
- Enjoying water
- Gentle movement

### Water Walking

**Gentle exercise:**
- Upright posture
- Natural walking
- Water resistance
- Joint friendly
- Comfortable pace

## Deck Furniture

### Umbrellas and Shade

**Sun protection:**
- Seek shade periodically
- Umbrella positioning
- Move with sun
- Protect skin
- Healthy practice

### Changing Positions

**Throughout day:**
- Alternate positions
- Avoid pressure points
- Move regularly
- Stay comfortable
- Enjoy the day

## Getting In and Out

### Pool Entry

**Safe entry:**
- Use stairs or ladder
- Hold rail
- Careful steps
- Gradual entry
- Watch for wet surfaces

Good pool relaxation posture enables comfortable poolside days.
