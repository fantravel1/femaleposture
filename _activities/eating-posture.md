# Eating Posture

Eating posture affects both digestion and social presence. Proper positioning supports healthy eating and comfortable dining.

## Table Position

### Chair Setup

**Seated for dining:**
- Chair at proper height
- Feet flat on floor
- Comfortable distance from table
- Back supported or upright
- Arms at table level

### Body Alignment

**While eating:**
- Upright torso
- Not hunched over plate
- Shoulders relaxed
- Food comes to mouth
- Core engaged

## Eating Mechanics

### Bringing Food to Mouth

**Proper technique:**
- Sit upright
- Lift utensil to mouth
- Don't lean to plate
- Small bites
- Comfortable pace

### Chewing and Swallowing

**Digestive support:**
- Upright position helps digestion
- Relaxed eating
- Not rushed
- Proper breathing
- Present eating

## Social Dining

### Restaurant Posture

**In public:**
- Proper table etiquette
- Comfortable but alert
- Engaged with companions
- Not slouched
- Natural positioning

### At Home

**Casual dining:**
- Table still preferred
- Not on couch (digestion)
- Mindful eating encouraged
- Comfortable position
- Family connection

## Screen-Free Eating

### Why It Matters

**Posture impact:**
- Looking at phone = neck strain
- Screen eating = poor posture
- Mindless eating
- Missed signals
- Digestion affected

Good eating posture supports digestion and makes meals more enjoyable social experiences.
