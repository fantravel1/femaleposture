# Rugby Posture

Rugby combines running, tackling, and contact. Proper posture protects your body in this demanding sport.

## Running Form

### Ball Carrying

**With ball:**
- Upright running
- Ball protection
- Balance maintained
- Prepared for contact
- Core engaged

### Support Running

**Off ball:**
- Natural run form
- Aware of play
- Ready to receive
- Defensive awareness
- Athletic positioning

## Contact Positions

### Tackling

**Safe tackling:**
- Head to side
- Shoulder contact
- Arms wrap
- Leg drive
- Core braced

### Taking Contact

**Being tackled:**
- Protect ball
- Brace for impact
- Core tight
- Controlled fall
- Ball presentation

## Scrum Position

### Forward Positions

**Scrum binding:**
- Flat back
- Core extremely engaged
- Proper binding
- Leg drive
- Neck alignment

## Lineout

### Lifting

**If involved:**
- Proper technique
- Core stability
- Lifting with legs
- Controlled movement
- Safety priority

## Injury Prevention

### Physical Preparation

**Safe play:**
- Strength training
- Neck conditioning
- Proper technique
- Rest and recovery
- Equipment proper

Good rugby posture protects your body during the high-contact demands of this sport.
