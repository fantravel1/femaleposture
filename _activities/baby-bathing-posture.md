# Baby Bathing Posture

Baby bathing requires secure holding in water. Proper posture ensures safety and comfort.

## Bath Setup

### Tub Height

**Optimal position:**
- Counter height ideal
- Sink bathing option
- Floor is low
- Kneeling for floor
- Comfortable access

### Baby Tub Types

**Equipment:**
- Infant tub
- Sink insert
- Bath support
- Various options
- Choose comfortable

## Holding Baby

### Secure Grip

**In water:**
- Arm under head/shoulders
- Secure hold always
- Never let go
- Confident grip
- Baby security

### One-Handed Washing

**While holding:**
- Free hand washes
- Organized supplies
- Quick access
- Efficient bathing
- Secure throughout

## Your Body Position

### At Counter

**Standing:**
- Close to tub
- Upright posture
- Arms at comfortable height
- Relaxed shoulders
- Core engaged

### At Floor Level

**Kneeling:**
- Padded knees
- Stable position
- Comfortable reach
- Take breaks
- Rise carefully

## Safety First

### Never Leave

**Critical rule:**
- Always present
- Secure hold
- Quick bath
- Complete attention
- No distractions

### Water Safety

**Temperature and depth:**
- Test temperature
- Shallow water
- Quick bathing
- Warm baby quickly
- Safe process

Good baby bathing posture ensures safe, comfortable bath time experiences.
