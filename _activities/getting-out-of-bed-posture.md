# Getting Out of Bed Posture

Getting out of bed starts the day. Proper posture supports safe, gentle morning rising.

## Waking Transition

### Before Rising

**Still in bed:**
- Wake fully
- Gentle stretching
- Awareness returning
- Body warming
- Prepare to move

### Roll to Side

**First movement:**
- Roll to side
- Don't sit straight up
- Gentle transition
- Back friendly
- Safe movement

## Rising Technique

### Side to Seated

**Sitting up:**
- Push with arms
- Swing legs over
- Come to seated
- Feet on floor
- Controlled motion

### Seated Pause

**Before standing:**
- Sit momentarily
- Orient yourself
- Avoid dizziness
- Gentle transition
- Ready to stand

## Standing Up

### From Seated

**Final rise:**
- Feet grounded
- Lean forward slightly
- Push to stand
- Steady yourself
- Begin day

## Morning Stiffness

### Managing Stiffness

**After sleep:**
- Gentle movement
- Easy stretches
- Gradual warming
- Listen to body
- Gentle start

## Daily Practice

### Consistent Technique

**Every morning:**
- Same approach
- Safe routine
- Protected back
- Gentle start
- Good habit

Good getting out of bed posture supports safe, gentle morning transitions.
