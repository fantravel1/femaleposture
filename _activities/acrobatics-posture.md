# Acrobatics Posture

Acrobatics combines strength, flexibility, and balance. Proper posture ensures safety in both solo and partner work.

## Solo Acrobatics

### Balance Positions

**Static holds:**
- Core engagement
- Aligned stacking
- Controlled breathing
- Strong lines
- Stable base

### Dynamic Movement

**Flipping and moving:**
- Core control
- Spatial awareness
- Proper technique
- Safe landings
- Progressive training

## Partner Acrobatics

### Basing

**Supporting partner:**
- Strong foundation
- Core braced
- Communication
- Safe catching
- Trust building

### Flying

**Being supported:**
- Core extremely tight
- Trust in base
- Clear communication
- Body control
- Safe dismounts

### Spotting

**Safety support:**
- Positioned properly
- Ready to assist
- Eyes on flyer
- Quick reactions
- Clear communication

## Acroyoga

### Basing

**Yoga base:**
- Feet on partner
- Core stable
- Communication
- Controlled movement
- Safe support

### Flying

**Partner trust:**
- Core engagement
- Balance centered
- Clear communication
- Relaxed but controlled
- Safe exits

## Training

### Building Safely

**Progressive approach:**
- Strength foundation
- Technique first
- Proper spotting
- Gradual progression
- Professional guidance

Good acrobatics posture builds the foundation for safe, impressive partner and solo work.
