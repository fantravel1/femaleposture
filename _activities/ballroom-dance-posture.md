# Ballroom Dance Posture

Ballroom dancing requires elegant posture and partnership. Proper form enables beautiful, connected dancing.

## Frame

### Upper Body Structure

**Dance frame:**
- Arms lifted
- Elbows supported
- Shoulders down
- Core engaged
- Head positioned

### Partner Connection

**Dance hold:**
- Shared frame
- Equal partnership
- Responsive connection
- Maintained structure
- Connected movement

## Standard Dances

### Waltz Posture

**Elegant frame:**
- Maximum stretch
- Head left
- Strong frame
- Rise and fall
- Graceful carriage

### Tango Posture

**Compact frame:**
- Closer hold
- Sharp edges
- Strong core
- Staccato quality
- Dramatic presence

### Foxtrot

**Smooth frame:**
- Extended lines
- Flowing quality
- Continuous motion
- Elegant carriage
- Sustained movement

## Latin Dances

### Cha-Cha Posture

**Cuban motion:**
- Hip action
- Compact frame
- Quick feet
- Rhythm centered
- Playful character

### Rumba

**Sensual movement:**
- Deep hip action
- Strong connection
- Slow sustained
- Emotional expression
- Controlled power

## Following

### Responsive Movement

**Partnership:**
- Read the lead
- Maintain frame
- Stay connected
- Add styling
- Equal partnership

Good ballroom dance posture creates elegant, connected partner dancing.
