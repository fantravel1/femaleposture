# Concert Standing Posture

Concert standing can last hours. Proper posture supports enjoyable, comfortable music experiences.

## Standing Form

### Basic Stance

**At concert:**
- Balanced stance
- Weight distributed
- Core engaged
- Shoulders relaxed
- Comfortable position

### Weight Shifting

**Extended standing:**
- Shift periodically
- Alternate legs
- Movement helps
- Prevent fatigue
- Sustainable standing

## Crowd Dynamics

### Packed Spaces

**Dense crowds:**
- Stay stable
- Core engaged
- Protect space
- Comfortable boundaries
- Safe positioning

### Personal Space

**When possible:**
- Comfortable distance
- Room to move
- Better experience
- More enjoyment
- Ideal positioning

## Movement

### Dancing

**Moving to music:**
- Natural movement
- Enjoy motion
- Balance maintained
- Have fun
- Good exercise

### Crowd Surfing Caution

**Safety note:**
- Consider carefully
- Know risks
- Protect yourself
- Make informed choice
- Safety first

## Venue Seating

### When Available

**Seated options:**
- Take advantage
- Rest legs
- Still enjoy music
- Alternate standing
- Comfort choice

## Footwear

### Shoe Choice

**Concert shoes:**
- Comfortable essential
- Standing support
- Flat preferred
- Break in beforehand
- Protect feet

Good concert standing posture enables enjoyable live music experiences.
