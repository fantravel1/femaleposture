# Qigong Posture

Qigong uses specific body positions to cultivate and circulate energy. Understanding proper Qigong posture supports the practice's health and energetic benefits.

## Standing Posture

### Basic Stance

**Foundation position:**
- Feet shoulder-width
- Knees slightly bent
- Pelvis slightly tucked
- Spine elongated
- Crown lifted

### Relaxed Alignment

**Soft structure:**
- Shoulders relaxed
- Arms at sides or lifted
- Chest open
- Breathing easy
- Stable but soft

## Key Principles

### Wu Chi Stance

**Empty stance:**
- Standing meditation
- Minimal muscular effort
- Internal awareness
- Energy cultivation
- Peaceful presence

### Energy Points

**Attention areas:**
- Crown of head
- Dan tian (belly center)
- Ming men (lower back)
- Palms
- Feet centers

## Moving Qigong
