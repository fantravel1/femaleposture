# Trying On Clothes Posture

Trying on clothes requires dressing and assessment. Proper posture helps evaluate true fit.

## In Fitting Room

### Space Considerations

**Small room:**
- Limited space
- Careful movement
- Use mirrors
- Organized approach
- Efficient process

### Mirror Assessment

**Evaluating fit:**
- Stand naturally
- Turn all angles
- Check movement
- Sit if possible
- Honest assessment

## Trying On Tops

### Overhead Dressing

**Shirts and blouses:**
- Arms through sleeves
- Head through neck
- Natural motion
- Check fit relaxed
- Normal posture

### Buttoning and Zipping

**Closures:**
- Stand naturally
- Check range of motion
- Breathe normally
- Move naturally
- Comfortable fit

## Trying On Bottoms

### Balance Needed

**Pants and skirts:**
- Use wall or hook
- Balance carefully
- Step in safely
- Check movement
- Sit and stand

### Sitting Test

**Essential:**
- Sit in pants
- Check comfort
- Range of motion
- Gap check
- Practical test

## Shoe Trying

### Stand and Walk

**Testing shoes:**
- Stand in shoes
- Walk around
- Check comfort
- Both feet equally
- Proper assessment

## Natural Posture

### True Fit Assessment

**Evaluation stance:**
- Stand naturally
- Don't suck in
- Relaxed shoulders
- Normal posture
- Honest fit check

Good trying on posture enables accurate assessment of clothing fit.
