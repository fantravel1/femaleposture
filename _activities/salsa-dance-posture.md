# Salsa Dance Posture

Salsa dancing requires hip action and rhythmic movement. Proper posture enables authentic Latin style.

## Basic Stance

### Foundation

**Ready position:**
- Weight centered
- Knees soft
- Core engaged
- Hips free
- Arms ready

### Cuban Motion

**Hip movement:**
- Weight shift
- Knee straighten
- Hip settles
- Natural drop
- Rhythmic motion

## Basic Step

### Timing

**On1 or On2:**
- Quick-quick-slow
- Weight transfer
- Hip emphasis
- Arms natural
- Rhythm constant

### Foot Work

**Foundation steps:**
- Ball of foot
- Light and quick
- Full transfer
- Clean steps
- Musical timing

## Turns

### Spot Turns

**Turning technique:**
- Core engaged
- Spot with eyes
- Arms controlled
- Balanced rotation
- Clean finish

### Partner Turns

**Led turns:**
- Frame maintained
- Core stable
- Smooth rotation
- Trust the lead
- Complete the turn

## Partner Work

### Connection

**Dancing together:**
- Responsive frame
- Feel the lead
- Add styling
- Maintain rhythm
- Have fun

### Styling

**Personal expression:**
- Arm styling
- Hip accents
- Head movements
- Character
- Musicality

## Solo Work

### Shines

**Individual expression:**
- Footwork patterns
- Arm styling
- Hip action
- Personal style
- Show off

Good salsa dance posture enables authentic, joyful Latin dancing.
