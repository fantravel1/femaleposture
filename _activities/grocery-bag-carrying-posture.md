# Grocery Bag Carrying Posture

Grocery bag carrying can be heavy. Proper posture prevents strain during shopping transport.

## Lifting Bags

### Safe Lifting

**Picking up bags:**
- Bend at knees
- Back straight
- Close to body
- Lift with legs
- Controlled motion

### Weight Assessment

**Before lifting:**
- Test weight
- Manage loads
- Multiple trips better
- Don't overdo
- Safe lifting

## Carrying Technique

### Balanced Loads

**Both hands:**
- Equal weight each
- Balanced carrying
- Arms at sides
- Close to body
- Symmetric strain

### Walking Form

**With bags:**
- Upright posture
- Shorter trips
- Core engaged
- Controlled pace
- Safe transport

## Multiple Trips

### Preference

**Better approach:**
- Several lighter trips
- Manage weight
- Protect body
- Sustainable approach
- Worth time

### Cart to Car

**Store to vehicle:**
- Use cart fully
- Load into car
- Manageable transfer
- Don't overload
- Efficient process

## Reusable Bags

### Bag Choice

**Better options:**
- Boxes for heavy items
- Structured bags
- Comfortable handles
- Manageable sizes
- Quality bags

Good grocery bag carrying posture prevents strain from shopping transport.
