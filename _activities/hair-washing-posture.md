# Hair Washing Posture

Hair washing involves arm reaching and head positioning. Proper posture prevents neck and shoulder strain.

## Shower Washing

### Standing Position

**In shower:**
- Upright stance
- Head back to rinse
- Brief neck extension
- Alternate positions
- Comfortable washing

### Arm Position

**Shampooing:**
- Arms overhead
- Relaxed shoulders
- Efficient scrubbing
- Take breaks
- Comfortable motion

## Sink Washing

### Head Forward

**Over sink:**
- Bend at waist
- Support with arms
- Brief position
- Rinse thoroughly
- Complete wash

### Salon Style

**Head back:**
- Neck supported
- Comfortable position
- Another washes
- Relaxing experience
- Professional option

## Long Hair

### More Work

**Lengthy hair:**
- More time needed
- Thorough washing
- Conditioning
- Arm fatigue possible
- Take your time

### Conditioning

**After shampoo:**
- Work through hair
- Arm work
- Thorough coverage
- Rinse well
- Complete care

## Frequency

### Washing Schedule

**Personal choice:**
- Varies by hair type
- Regular routine
- Comfortable practice
- Sustainable schedule
- Hair health

Good hair washing posture supports comfortable, thorough hair care.
