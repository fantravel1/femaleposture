# Dishwashing Posture

Dishwashing involves prolonged standing at a fixed height. Proper posture protects your back during this common task.

## Sink Position

### Height Issues

**Counter challenges:**
- Often too low
- Causes stooping
- Back strain
- Shoulder tension
- Arm fatigue

### Adaptations

**Making it work:**
- Step stool if too high
- Basin raised if too low
- Anti-fatigue mat
- Foot rest
- Core engagement

## Body Position

### Standing Form

**At the sink:**
- Stand close to counter
- Weight balanced
- Knees soft
- Core engaged
- Shoulders relaxed

### Avoiding Strain

**Prevention:**
- Don't lean forward
- Keep items close
- Arms at comfortable angle
- Regular position shifts
- Take breaks

## During Washing

### Hand Position

**Comfortable work:**
- Elbows at comfortable angle
- Wrists neutral
- Varied grip
- Not gripping tightly
- Relaxed hands

### Movement

**While washing:**
- Shift weight
- Take breaks
- Sit for drying
- Divide task
- Break up time

## Environment

### Floor Surface

**Standing comfort:**
- Anti-fatigue mat
- Comfortable shoes
- Avoid bare feet on hard floor
- Floor temperature
- Standing time limits

Good dishwashing posture protects your back during this necessary daily task.
