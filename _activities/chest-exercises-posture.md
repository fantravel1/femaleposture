# Chest Exercises Posture

Chest exercises build upper body strength. Proper posture ensures effective, safe pectoral training.

## Bench Press

### Flat Bench

**Press form:**
- Back arched naturally
- Shoulder blades squeezed
- Feet grounded
- Bar path controlled
- Safe technique

### Incline Press

**Upper chest:**
- Bench at angle
- Same principles
- Adjust path slightly
- Upper pec focus
- Controlled movement

## Push-Ups

### Standard Push-Up

**Body position:**
- Plank alignment
- Hands under shoulders
- Core engaged
- Full range
- Controlled movement

### Modifications

**Easier options:**
- Knee push-ups
- Incline push-ups
- Wall push-ups
- Build strength
- Progress toward standard

## Fly Movements

### Dumbbell Fly

**Fly form:**
- Elbows slightly bent
- Wide arc
- Chest stretch
- Controlled motion
- Safe range

## Cable Work

### Cable Crossover

**Cable fly:**
- Controlled motion
- Squeeze at center
- Chest contraction
- Proper resistance
- Quality reps

## Posture Points

### Shoulder Safety

**Chest training:**
- Shoulder blades set
- Proper depth
- Controlled weight
- Joint protection
- Safe training

Good chest exercise posture ensures effective, safe pectoral strengthening.
