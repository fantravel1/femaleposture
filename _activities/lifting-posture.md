# Lifting Posture

Lifting is a daily activityâ€”from groceries to children to exercise weights. Proper lifting technique protects your back and allows you to handle loads safely.

## Basic Lifting Principles

### The Fundamentals

**Safe lifting:**
- Get close to load
- Squat down
- Lift with legs
- Keep load close
- Avoid twisting

### Body Position

**Proper form:**
- Feet shoulder-width
- Knees bend
- Back neutral
- Core braced
- Head up

## Different Lifting Scenarios

### Floor to Waist

**Ground lifting:**
- Full squat or lunge
- Grip securely
- Rise with legs
- Keep spine neutral
- Controlled movement

### Waist to Overhead

**High lifting:**
- Stable base
- Core engaged
- Press from shoulder
- Avoid arching back
- Controlled placement

### Awkward Objects

**Challenging lifts:**
- Get help if needed
- Find best grip
- Redistribute if possible
- Use proper tools
- Know limits

## Everyday Lifting

### Groceries

**Shopping bags:**
- Balance weight
- Multiple trips okay
- Squat for low items
- Cart use when available
- Car loading technique

### Children

**Child lifting:**
- Squat down to level
- Keep child close
- Rise with legs
- Support child properly
- Transition positions safely

### Household Items

**Home lifting:**
- Laundry baskets
- Storage boxes
- Furniture moving
- Pet handling
- General household

## When Not to Lift

### Knowing Limits

**Smart decisions:**
- Too heavy alone
- Awkward shape
- Poor position
- Pain signal
- Recovery period

Good lifting posture protects your back through countless daily lifting tasks throughout your life.
