# Aerial Arts Posture

Aerial arts demand incredible strength and body control. Proper posture creates safe, beautiful movement in the air.

### Core Engagement

**Foundation:**
- Hollow body
- Engaged core
- Controlled movement
- Strong lines
- Safe positioning

### Grip Strength

**Holding on:**
- Secure grip
- Arm engagement
- Shoulder stability
- Core connection
- Safe release

- Core engagement

- Safe descent

**Securing position:**
- Proper wrapping
- Tension awareness

- Safe transitions
- Multiple contacts

- Core timing

- Safe catches

**In the hoop:**

- Balance in circle

### Training Properly
