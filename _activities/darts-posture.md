# Darts Posture

Darts requires precision throwing. Proper posture enables accurate, consistent throws.

## Stance

### At the Oche

**Throwing line:**
- Toe at or behind line
- Dominant foot forward
- Weight on front foot
- Stable base
- Comfortable position

### Body Angle

**To board:**
- Sideways to board
- Shoulder points to target
- Balanced position
- Consistent stance
- Personal preference

## Throwing Arm

### Starting Position

**Ready to throw:**
- Elbow pointed at board
- Forearm vertical
- Dart at eye level
- Relaxed shoulder
- Consistent setup

### Throwing Motion

**The throw:**
- Elbow stays fixed
- Forearm moves
- Wrist snaps
- Follow through
- Consistent motion

## Grip

### Holding Dart

**Grip style:**
- Various grips
- Stable hold
- Relaxed fingers
- Consistent placement
- Personal preference

### Pressure

**How tight:**
- Not too tight
- Secure but relaxed
- Consistent grip
- Smooth release
- Practice refines

## Aim

### Sight Line

**Alignment:**
- Eye to dart to target
- Consistent aim point
- Focus on target
- Visualize path
- Confidence

### Follow Through

**After release:**
- Arm continues to target
- Full extension
- Consistent finish
- Don't pull back
- Complete motion

Good darts posture enables accurate, consistent precision throwing.
