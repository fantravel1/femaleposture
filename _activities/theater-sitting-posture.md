# Theater Sitting Posture

Theater attendance involves extended sitting. Proper posture supports comfortable show enjoyment.

## Theater Seating

### Standard Seats

**Venue chairs:**
- Often narrow
- Limited legroom
- Make best of space
- Sit upright
- Use armrests

### Premium Seating

**Better options:**
- More space
- Better views
- Worth upgrade sometimes
- More comfort
- Enhanced experience

## Seated Position

### Upright Viewing

**For show:**
- Sit up straight
- Clear sightlines
- Respectful of others
- Engaged posture
- Quality viewing

### Armrest Use

**Shared spaces:**
- Negotiate politely
- Take turns
- Be considerate
- Support arms
- Comfortable sharing

## Long Performances

### Extended Shows

**Multi-hour events:**
- Shift positions subtly
- Intermission stretch
- Maintain decorum
- Quiet movement
- Comfortable duration

### Intermission

**Break time:**
- Stand up
- Move around
- Bathroom break
- Refresh position
- Valuable break

## Viewing Angle

### Sight Lines

**Optimal viewing:**
- Direct view
- No obstructions
- Comfortable angle
- Neck neutral
- Enjoyable watching

Good theater sitting posture enables comfortable, respectful performance enjoyment.
