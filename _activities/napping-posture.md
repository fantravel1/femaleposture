# Napping Posture

Napping offers brief rejuvenation. Proper posture supports restful, restorative short sleep.

## Lying Down

### Bed Napping

**Reclined rest:**
- Comfortable position
- Pillow for head
- Relaxed body
- Quality rest
- Brief duration

### Side or Back

**Position choice:**
- Personal preference
- Supported well
- Comfortable
- Restful position
- Brief sleep

## Seated Napping

### Chair Rest

**Not lying down:**
- Supported head
- Upright or reclined chair
- Brief rest
- Workplace option
- Acceptable nap

### Desk Nap

**At work:**
- Head on arms
- Brief rest
- Less ideal
- Better than nothing
- Short duration

## Duration

### Brief Naps

**Optimal length:**
- 10-20 minutes
- Set alarm
- Brief restoration
- Not too long
- Refreshed feeling

## Environment

### Comfortable Setting

**Nap setup:**
- Quiet space
- Dim light
- Comfortable temperature
- Brief setup
- Quality rest

Good napping posture supports brief, restorative rest periods.
