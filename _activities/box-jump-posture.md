# Box Jump Posture

Box jumps develop explosive power. Proper posture enables safe, effective jumping.

## Starting Position

### Ready Stance

**Before jump:**
- Athletic stance
- Feet hip-width
- Arms back
- Knees bent
- Core engaged

### Loading

**Power generation:**
- Countermovement
- Arms swing back
- Hips load
- Prepare for explosion
- Full tension

## Takeoff

### Explosive Jump

**Leaving ground:**
- Arms drive up
- Legs extend
- Full body effort
- Upward trajectory
- Maximum height

### Flight

**In the air:**
- Knees tuck
- Prepare for landing
- Eyes on box
- Core tight
- Controlled position

## Landing

### Soft Landing

**On the box:**
- Both feet land
- Quiet landing
- Absorb with legs
- Hips back
- Stand tall

### Step Down

**Safe descent:**
- Step down
- Don't jump down
- Reduce impact
- Reset position
- Prepare for next

## Progressions

### Starting Safe

**Building up:**
- Lower box first
- Perfect landings
- Progressive height
- Confidence building
- Skill development

### Variations

**Advanced options:**
- Seated box jump
- Single leg hops
- Depth jumps
- Rebounding
- Sport-specific

## Safety

### Injury Prevention

**Safe practice:**
- Appropriate height
- Fatigue awareness
- Warm up first
- Soft landings
- Know your limits

Good box jump posture enables explosive power development safely.
