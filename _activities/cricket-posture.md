# Cricket Posture

Cricket combines batting, bowling, and fielding with specific technique requirements. Proper posture protects your body while enhancing performance.

## Batting

### Stance

**At the crease:**
- Balanced weight
- Knees bent
- Bat ready
- Eyes level
- Comfortable position

### Stroke Play

**Shot technique:**
- Weight transfer
- Head still
- Full swing
- Follow through
- Balanced finish

## Bowling

### Run-Up

**Approaching crease:**
- Rhythmic run
- Building momentum
- Core engaged
- Ready for action
- Consistent approach

### Delivery

**Bowling action:**
- Arm high
- Body rotation
- Follow through
- Balanced finish
- Repeated safely

## Fielding

### Ready Position

**Ground fielding:**
- Low stance
- Core engaged
- Quick reactions
- Balanced position
- Eyes on ball

### Throwing

**Returns to keeper:**
- Good catch
- Quick transfer
- Power from legs
- Follow through
- Accurate throw

## Injury Prevention

### Common Issues

**Cricket injuries:**
- Back strain from bowling
- Shoulder from throwing
- Knee from quick movements
- Prevention strategies

Good cricket posture enables long days on the field while protecting your body from repetitive strain.
