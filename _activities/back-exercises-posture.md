# Back Exercises Posture

Back exercises build posterior strength. Proper posture ensures effective, safe back training.

## Rowing Movements

### Bent-Over Row

**Row technique:**
- Hinged position
- Back flat
- Core engaged
- Pull to ribcage
- Squeeze shoulder blades

### Seated Row

**Cable or machine:**
- Upright torso
- Chest proud
- Controlled pull
- Full squeeze
- Controlled return

## Pulling Movements

### Lat Pulldown

**Pulldown form:**
- Seated upright
- Chest up
- Pull to upper chest
- Controlled movement
- Full range

### Assisted Pull-Ups

**Building strength:**
- Proper grip
- Core engaged
- Full range effort
- Progressive assistance
- Building toward unassisted

## Back Extension

### Hyperextension

**Extension exercise:**
- Controlled range
- Core engaged
- Glute activation
- Not overextending
- Safe technique

## Posture Muscles

### Rhomboids and Traps

**Postural strength:**
- Squeeze shoulder blades
- Controlled movements
- Balanced training
- Posture improvement
- Functional strength

## Form Priority

### Back Safety

**Training carefully:**
- Neutral spine
- Core engagement
- Appropriate weight
- Controlled movement
- Safe training

Good back exercise posture ensures effective, safe posterior strengthening.
