# Sauna Posture

Sauna sessions offer heat therapy. Proper posture supports comfortable, safe heat exposure.

## Seating Options

### Bench Levels

**Heat positioning:**
- Higher is hotter
- Lower more moderate
- Choose comfort level
- Adjust as needed
- Personal preference

### Seated Position

**On bench:**
- Seated comfortably
- Feet up or down
- Relaxed posture
- Towel underneath
- Comfortable sitting

## Lying Down

### Reclined Option

**Stretched out:**
- Full bench use
- Complete relaxation
- Considerate of others
- When space allows
- Deep relaxation

## Duration

### Time Awareness

**Session length:**
- Start with short
- Build tolerance
- Exit when needed
- Know limits
- Safe practice

### Heat Acclimatization

**Getting used to:**
- Gradual increase
- Listen to body
- Cool breaks
- Progressive adaptation
- Comfortable exposure

## Breathing

### In Heat

**Respiratory:**
- Breathe normally
- Through nose
- Relaxed breathing
- Not too deep
- Comfortable respiration

## Exit Protocol

### Leaving Sauna

**Transition:**
- Exit slowly
- Cool gradually
- Sit briefly
- Don't rush
- Safe transition

### Cooling Down

**After sauna:**
- Cool shower
- Rest period
- Hydrate well
- Gentle transition
- Complete cycle

Good sauna posture supports safe, beneficial heat therapy sessions.
