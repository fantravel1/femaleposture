# Tai Chi Posture

Tai Chi emphasizes precise body positioning for energy flow and balance. Understanding proper Tai Chi posture deepens your practice while providing physical benefits.

## Basic Stance

### Root and Grounding

**Foundation:**
- Feet shoulder-width
- Weight in whole foot
- Rooted connection
- Stable base
- Relaxed power

### Body Alignment

**Stacked posture:**
- Slightly bent knees
- Pelvis tucked slightly
- Spine elongated
- Head suspended
- Relaxed shoulders

## Key Principles

### Relaxed Alignment

**Soft power:**
- Muscles relaxed
- Joints open
- No tension holding
- Energy flowing
- Natural alignment

### Slow Movement

**Moving meditation:**
- Continuous motion
- No rushing
- Breath coordination
- Present awareness
- Flowing transitions

## Common Positions

### Ward Off

**Basic form:**
- Arms rounded
- Shoulders relaxed
- Core engaged
- Weight shifting
- Rooted stance

### Grasp Sparrow's Tail

**Flowing sequence:**
- Weight transfers
- Arm movements
- Turning waist
- Coordinated flow
- Balanced transitions
