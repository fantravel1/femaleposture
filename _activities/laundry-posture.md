# Laundry Posture

Laundry involves repetitive bending, lifting, and standing tasks. Proper posture protects your back through this regular household chore.

## Loading Machines

### Washer Loading

**Front-load:**
- Squat or kneel
- Sort at raised level
- Don't round back
- Core engagement
- Rise safely

### Dryer Access

**Reaching in:**
- Squat for low dryers
- Stacked units easier
- Core engaged
- Pull items close
- Safe rising

## Carrying Laundry

### Basket Carrying

**Proper technique:**
- Hold close to body
- See over basket
- Balanced weight
- Multiple trips okay
- Safe stairs

### Alternative Options

**Easier transport:**
- Rolling baskets
- Smaller loads
- Multiple trips
- Cart options
- Elevated hampers

## Folding

### Table Height

**Proper surface:**
- Waist height ideal
- Standing or sitting
- Good lighting
- Arms comfortable
- Posture awareness

### Technique

**Folding form:**
- Keep items close
- Vary positions
- Take breaks
- Batch similar items
- Efficient movements

## Ironing

### Board Height

**Proper setup:**
- Elbow height
- Standing or sitting
- Core engaged
- Arm at comfortable angle
- Position changes

Good laundry posture makes this regular chore easier on your body and more efficient.
