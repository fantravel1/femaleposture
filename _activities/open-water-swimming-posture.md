# Open Water Swimming Posture

Open water swimming presents unique challenges. Proper posture addresses navigation and conditions.

## Stroke Adaptation

### Modified Freestyle

**In open water:**
- Higher arm recovery
- Stronger catch
- Eyes on sighting
- Adaptable rhythm
- Core stability

### Bilateral Breathing

**Both sides:**
- Navigation aware
- Wave management
- Balanced stroke
- Condition response
- Flexible timing

## Sighting

### Crocodile Eyes

**Seeing ahead:**
- Lift eyes only
- Quick look
- Minimal disruption
- Regular intervals
- Target identified

### Navigation

**Staying on course:**
- Identify landmarks
- Sight regularly
- Adjust as needed
- Trust the process
- Efficient lines

## Conditions

### Waves and Chop

**Rough water:**
- Higher recovery
- Breathe away from waves
- Core stability
- Adaptable rhythm
- Calm approach

### Current

**Moving water:**
- Angle adjustment
- Increased effort upsteam
- Strategic approach
- Energy management
- Safety awareness

## Race Starts

### Mass Start

**Group swimming:**
- Find space
- Protect yourself
- Stay calm
- Find rhythm
- Navigate pack

## Safety

### Open Water Awareness

**Safe swimming:**
- Know conditions
- Swim with others
- Visible gear
- Know exits
- Weather awareness

Good open water swimming posture adapts pool technique to natural conditions safely.
