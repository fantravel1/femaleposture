# Rowing Posture

## The Rowing Stroke

### Catch Position

**Start of stroke:**
- Shins vertical
- Arms extended
- Back upright
- Core engaged
- Shoulders relaxed

### Drive Phase

**Power application:**
- Legs drive first
- Back opens second
- Arms pull last
- Core stable
- Sequential power

### Finish Position

**End of stroke:**
- Legs extended
- Back slightly reclined
- Handle to chest
- Elbows back
- Controlled position

### Recovery

**Return to catch:**
- Arms extend first
- Body swings forward
- Knees come up
- Controlled slide
- Smooth transition

## Common Errors

### Technique Problems

**What to avoid:**
- Back first instead of legs
- Over-reaching at catch
- Excessive lay-back
- Pulling with arms early
- Rushing the slide

### Corrections

**Better form:**
- Legs-back-arms sequence
- Appropriate reach
- Controlled finish
- Sequential power
- Patient recovery
