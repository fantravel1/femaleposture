# Water Polo Posture

Water polo demands constant treading and dynamic movement. Proper posture enables effective play.

## Treading Water

### Eggbeater Kick

**Staying up:**
- Alternating circular kicks
- Hips stable
- Core engaged
- Arms free
- Sustainable motion

### Height Control

**Raising up:**
- Faster kick
- Arms assist
- Core engaged
- Explosive when needed
- Return to base

## Swimming Position

### Head-Up Freestyle

**Moving with awareness:**
- Head elevated
- Eyes forward
- Modified stroke
- Speed and vision
- Quick transitions

### Sprint Position

**Fast movement:**
- Streamlined body
- Powerful stroke
- Quick kicks
- Head up as needed
- Explosive speed

## Ball Handling

### Receiving

**Catching passes:**
- One hand up
- Tread higher
- Secure ball
- Quick transition
- Protect ball

### Throwing

**Passing and shooting:**
- Rise up high
- Arm cocked back
- Core rotation
- Explosive throw
- Follow through

## Defense

### Pressing

**Guarding:**
- Arms wide
- Tread actively
- Block passing lanes
- Quick reactions
- Stay with opponent

### Blocking

**Shot defense:**
- Rise up high
- Arms extended
- Time the shot
- Core engaged
- Quick recovery

Good water polo posture enables the dynamic movement required for competitive play.
