# Video Making Posture

Video making requires on-camera presence. Proper posture supports professional, comfortable filming.

## Camera Position

### Eye Level

**Camera setup:**
- Camera at eye level
- Direct engagement
- Natural gaze
- Professional appearance
- Flattering angle

### Distance Framing

**Shot composition:**
- Appropriate distance
- Good framing
- Visible gestures
- Room to move
- Professional shot

## Standing Videos

### Upright Presence

**Standing to camera:**
- Confident stance
- Shoulders back
- Core engaged
- Natural posture
- Professional bearing

### Movement

**On camera motion:**
- Purposeful movement
- Controlled gestures
- Stay in frame
- Natural motion
- Engaging presence

## Seated Videos

### Chair Position

**Sitting to camera:**
- Upright posture
- Engaged lean
- Camera height adjusted
- Professional appearance
- Comfortable filming

## Extended Filming

### Long Sessions

**Multiple takes:**
- Maintain posture
- Take breaks
- Reset position
- Consistent appearance
- Sustainable filming

### Energy Management

**Screen presence:**
- Slightly elevated energy
- Engaged appearance
- Fresh takes
- Rested performer
- Quality content

Good video making posture supports professional, comfortable content creation.
