# Kickball Posture

Kickball combines kicking with baseball rules. Proper posture enables effective, fun play.

## Kicking

### At the Plate

**Ready position:**
- Behind home plate
- Balanced stance
- Eyes on ball
- Timing ready
- Kick foot chosen

### Kicking Motion

**Contact:**
- Step into kick
- Plant foot stable
- Kick through ball
- Follow through
- Run immediately

### Kick Placement

**Strategy:**
- Power or placement
- Direction choice
- Ground or air
- Strategic kicking
- Get on base

## Running

### Base Running

**After kick:**
- Sprint immediately
- Watch ball
- Base decisions
- Tag up rules
- Score runs

## Fielding

### Catching

**Fly balls:**
- Position under ball
- Secure catch
- Throw to base
- Communication
- Outfield play

### Ground Balls

**Infield play:**
- Get in front
- Scoop up
- Quick throw
- Accurate delivery
- Make the out

## Pitching

### Underhand Roll

**Delivering ball:**
- Smooth roll
- Consistent bounce
- Strike zone
- Speed variation
- Strategic pitching

### Fielding Position

**After pitch:**
- Ready for kick
- Field your area
- Quick reactions
- Play the ball
- Make the play

Good kickball posture enables effective, enjoyable playground sport play.
