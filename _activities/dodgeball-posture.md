# Dodgeball Posture

Dodgeball combines throwing with athletic evasion. Proper posture enables effective play.

## Ready Position

### Athletic Stance

**Before action:**
- Feet shoulder-width
- Knees bent
- Weight on balls of feet
- Ready to move
- Eyes scanning

### Ball in Hand

**Holding ball:**
- Secure grip
- Ready to throw
- Quick release
- Aim ready
- Attack mode

## Throwing

### Overhand Throw

**Power throw:**
- Ball behind head
- Step into throw
- Core rotation
- Arm whips
- Follow through

### Sidearm

**Speed throw:**
- Horizontal motion
- Quick release
- Harder to see
- Surprise factor
- Alternative throw

## Dodging

### Evading Throws

**Movement:**
- Quick reflexes
- Explosive moves
- Any direction
- Duck and jump
- Survival instincts

### Reading Throwers

**Anticipation:**
- Watch their setup
- Predict target
- Early movement
- Constant motion
- Awareness crucial

## Catching

### Eliminating Thrower

**Secure catch:**
- Eyes on ball
- Soft hands
- Absorb impact
- Hold on
- Game changer

### Two-Hand Catch

**Most secure:**
- Both hands together
- Bring to body
- Secure grip
- Celebrate safely
- Return teammate

## Strategy

### Team Play

**Coordinated effort:**
- Protect teammates
- Collective throws
- Communication
- Strategic positioning
- Team victory

Good dodgeball posture enables agile, effective playground sport play.
