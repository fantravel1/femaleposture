# Handball Posture

Handball combines running, throwing, and defending. Proper posture enables effective play.

## Athletic Stance

### Ready Position

**On court:**
- Feet shoulder-width
- Knees bent
- Weight on balls of feet
- Arms ready
- Quick reaction

### Movement

**Court movement:**
- Light and quick
- Direction change
- Explosive starts
- Defensive slides
- Athletic motion

## Throwing

### Overhand Throw

**Standard throw:**
- Ball in one hand
- Arm cocked back
- Hip rotation
- Shoulder follows
- Powerful release

### Jump Shot

**Elevated throw:**
- Jump and throw
- Core rotation
- Release at peak
- Follow through
- Power generation

## Catching

### Receiving Ball

**Secure catch:**
- Eyes on ball
- Hands ready
- Soft reception
- Secure immediately
- Ready for next move

## Defense

### Defensive Stance

**Guarding:**
- Low stance
- Arms wide
- Quick feet
- Stay with player
- Pressure constant

### Blocking

**Shot defense:**
- Arms up
- Time the shot
- Body positioning
- Controlled block
- Quick recovery

## Goalkeeping

### Special Position

**In goal:**
- Unique stance
- Arms ready
- Quick reactions
- Full body saves
- Courage required

Good handball posture enables dynamic, effective court sport play.
