## Yoga and Posture Benefits

### What Yoga Offers

**Posture improvements:**
- Body awareness development
- Flexibility increase
- Strength building
- Alignment learning
- Muscle balance

### Women-Specific Benefits

**For female bodies:**
- Core and pelvic floor strengthening
- Hormonal balance support
- Menstrual cycle adaptation
- Pregnancy modifications
- Menopause support

## Foundation Poses for Posture

### Standing Poses

**Alignment focus:**
- Mountain Pose (Tadasana)
- Warrior poses
- Triangle pose
- Standing forward fold
- Tree pose

### Core Strengthening

**Building support:**
- Plank pose
- Boat pose
- Cat-cow
- Bird-dog
- Locust pose

## Common Alignment Errors

### What to Watch

**Frequent issues:**
- Hyperextending knees
- Overarching low back
- Collapsing chest
- Sinking shoulders
- Neck strain

### Corrections

**Proper alignment:**
- Micro-bend in joints
- Engage core for spine support
- Open chest without arching
- Draw shoulders back and down
- Long neck, chin level

## Practice Considerations

### Modifications for Women

**Adaptations:**
- Cycle-appropriate practice
- Breast support in inversions
- Hip opening variations
- Pregnancy modifications
- Menopause considerations

### Building a Practice

**Sustainable approach:**
- Start with basics
- Focus on alignment first
- Progress gradually
- Listen to body
- Regular practice

Regular yoga practice builds the body awareness and strength necessary for good posture both on and off the mat.
