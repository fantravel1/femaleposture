# Playground Supervision Posture

Playground supervision combines watching and waiting. Proper posture supports comfortable oversight.

## Standing Supervision

### Active Watching

**Standing position:**
- Upright stance
- Mobile as needed
- Following child
- Quick response ready
- Alert positioning

### Extended Standing

**Long playground visits:**
- Shift weight
- Move around
- Find leaning spots
- Comfortable shoes
- Sustainable standing

## Seated Options

### Bench Sitting

**Park benches:**
- Upright posture
- Eye on children
- Comfortable sitting
- Back supported
- Alert but relaxed

### Ground Sitting

**With younger kids:**
- Various positions
- Cross-legged
- Kneeling
- Comfortable options
- Ready to assist

## Active Participation

### Playing Together

**Joining in:**
- Climbing carefully
- Safe play
- Model good form
- Enjoy movement
- Active parenting

### Pushing Swings

**Common activity:**
- Stable stance
- Arms at comfortable level
- Core engaged
- Repetitive motion
- Alternate arms

## Weather Considerations

### Outdoor Comfort

**All conditions:**
- Dressed appropriately
- Shade when possible
- Hydrated
- Comfortable duration
- Prepared parent

Good playground supervision posture supports comfortable, alert child watching.
