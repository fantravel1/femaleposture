# Slam Ball Posture

Slam balls are designed for impact exercises. Proper posture enables safe, explosive training.

## Basic Slam

### Overhead Slam

**Power movement:**
- Ball overhead
- Arms extended
- Explosive slam
- Hip hinge
- Full body power

### Setup

**Starting position:**
- Athletic stance
- Core engaged
- Ball lifted overhead
- Prepared for slam
- Safe surroundings

## Slam Variations

### Side Slams

**Rotational:**
- Ball to side
- Rotate and slam
- Core power
- Alternating sides
- Rotational strength

### Reverse Slam

**Behind:**
- Ball at chest
- Rotate back
- Throw behind
- Full body
- Retrieve and repeat

## Movement Quality

### Explosive Effort

**Maximum power:**
- Full commitment

### Safe Execution

**Injury prevention:**

- Safe surface
