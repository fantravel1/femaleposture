# Mirror Checking Posture

Mirror checking is frequent activity. Proper posture during appearance checks supports body awareness.

## Standing at Mirror

### Full-Length Viewing

**Checking outfit:**
- Stand naturally
- View full body
- Turn for angles
- Posture awareness
- Honest assessment

### Bathroom Mirror

**Face level:**
- Close viewing
- Comfortable stance
- Relaxed face
- Natural lighting
- Brief checks

## Posture Awareness

### What You See

**Mirror feedback:**
- Check posture
- Shoulders level
- Head position
- Full alignment
- Visual cues

### Daily Checks

**Using mirror:**
- Posture check opportunity
- Alignment awareness
- Correction if needed
- Visual feedback
- Self-awareness tool

## Compact Mirrors

### Small Mirrors

**Quick checks:**
- Brief use
- Specific focus
- Quick touch-ups
- Minimal time
- Efficient checking
