# Beach Lounging Posture

Beach time involves extended relaxation. Proper positioning prevents discomfort during beach days.

## Beach Chair

### Lounge Position

**In beach chair:**
- Reclined comfortable
- Support under knees optional
- Shade face if possible
- Comfortable angle
- Adjustable positions

### Sitting Up

**More upright:**
- Use back support
- Feet comfortable
- Not slumped
- Reading position
- Change positions

## On Sand

### Lying Down

**Direct on beach:**
- Towel or mat
- Sand contours
- Pillow or rolled towel
- Comfortable position
- Even surface

### Prone Position

**Face down:**
- Arms comfortable
- Head turned
- Alternate sides
- Limited duration
- Sun exposure awareness

## Activities

### Reading

**At the beach:**
- Raise book up
- Don't hunch
- Shade screen
- Take breaks
- Eye protection

### Watching Others

**Observing:**
- Sitting up
- Good visibility
- Comfortable position
- Shade if possible
- Relaxed posture

## Sun Exposure

### Position Changes

**During sun:**
- Rotate for even exposure
- Change position regularly
- Seek shade breaks
- Protective awareness
- Safe sun practice

### Shade Seeking

**When needed:**
- Umbrella use
- Shade structure
- Move to shade
- Cover up
- Healthy habits

## Getting Up

### From Sand

**Rising safely:**
- Roll to side
- Push up
- Shake off sand
- Careful standing
- Brush off

Good beach lounging posture enables comfortable, relaxing beach time.
