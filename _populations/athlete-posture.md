# Athlete Posture Guide

> **Performance and Protection:** Athletic training demands specific positions, but daily posture still matters. Balance sport-specific needs with overall body health.

---

## The Athlete's Posture Challenge

### Unique Considerations

- **Sport-specific positions** - Training creates patterns
- **Muscle imbalances** - From repetitive movements
- **High volume** - Hours of practice
- **Off-field demands** - School, work, recovery
- **Body as tool** - Optimizing for performance

### Common Issues

- Sport-induced postural patterns
- Imbalances from one-sided sports
- Overuse from training volume
- Recovery posture neglected
- "Off" time not truly recovery

---

## Sport-Specific Patterns

### One-Sided Sports

**Tennis, golf, baseball, etc.:**
- Rotational asymmetry
- One side stronger/tighter
- Postural compensation
- Balance work essential

### Forward-Dominant Sports

**Cycling, swimming, running:**
- Anterior muscle emphasis
- Upper back rounding tendency
- Hip flexor tightness
- Counter-positioning needed

### Overhead Sports

**Volleyball, swimming, tennis:**
- Shoulder demands
- Thoracic mobility requirements
- Specific postural needs
- Injury prevention focus

### Contact Sports

**Basketball, soccer, hockey:**
- Bracing patterns
- Core emphasis
- Impact considerations
- Flexibility balance
