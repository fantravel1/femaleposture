# EMT and Paramedic Posture

Emergency medical technicians and paramedics perform physically demanding work in unpredictable conditions. Patient lifting, treatment in confined spaces, and working in the back of moving ambulances create significant physical challenges. Female EMS professionals face these demands while often working with equipment sized for larger bodies.
