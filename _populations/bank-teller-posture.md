# Bank Teller Posture

Bank tellers perform extended standing work at fixed stations, processing transactions while maintaining customer service presence. The combination of standing, repetitive reaching, and computer work creates specific physical challenges. Female bank tellers need strategies for their workstation-bound role.

## Station Work Demands

### Extended Standing
Hours at the teller station.

### Transaction Processing
Repetitive handling of cash and documents.

### Computer Work
Data entry and account access.

### Customer Interaction
Service-oriented positioning.

## Physical Challenges

### Standing Duration
Long shifts on feet.

### Reaching
Cash drawers and equipment placement.

### Repetitive Motion
Same transactions repeated.

### Counter Height
Fixed counter heights.

## Common Physical Issues

### Foot Pain
Extended standing on hard floors.

### Lower Back
Standing without movement.

### Shoulder Strain
Repetitive reaching.

### Neck Tension
Screen positioning.
