# Dental Assistant Posture

Dental assistants work in challenging positions while supporting dental procedures. The work involves sustained awkward postures, repetitive reaching, and extended time in positions that strain the body. Female dental assistants need attention to ergonomics in this demanding healthcare role.

## Chairside Work

### Patient Support
Positioning for procedure assistance.

### Instrument Transfer
Handing instruments to dentists.

### Suction Operation
Managing suction during procedures.

### Retraction
Holding retractors and mirrors.

## Physical Positioning

### Stool Positioning
Proper height and placement.

### Patient Relation
Positioning relative to patient.

### Reach Distance
Managing arm extension.

### Twisted Positions
Avoiding spinal rotation.

## Common Physical Issues

### Neck Pain
Forward and twisted positions.

### Shoulder Strain
Sustained arm positions.

### Back Problems
Seating and positioning challenges.

### Hand Fatigue
Instrument handling.

Reaching equipment efficiently.
