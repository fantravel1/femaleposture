# Flight Dispatcher Posture

Aircraft dispatchers share responsibility with pilots for flight safety, working in operations centers monitoring weather, planning routes, and managing flight activities. The work involves extended seated time at multi-screen workstations, often during long shifts with irregular hours. Female dispatchers need attention to ergonomics in this critical aviation role.
