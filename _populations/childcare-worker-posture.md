# Childcare Worker Posture

Childcare involves constant physical engagement with young children. Female childcare workers spend days bending, lifting, sitting on floors, and moving quickly to supervise active children. The physical demands accumulate throughout long shifts.

## Daily Physical Demands

### Child Lifting
Picking up and carrying children.

### Floor Activities
Extended floor-level work.

### Diapering
Changing table work.

### Feeding
Mealtime assistance.

## Activity Engagement

### Active Play
Movement activities with children.

### Circle Time
Floor sitting for groups.

### Outdoor Play
Playground supervision.

### Art and Crafts
Table activity facilitation.

## Common Physical Strains

### Lower Back
Lifting and bending.

### Knees
Floor work.

### Shoulders
Carrying children.

### Voice
Constant communication.

### Proper Mechanics
Using legs not back.

### Weight Limits
Knowing personal limits.

Knee protection.
