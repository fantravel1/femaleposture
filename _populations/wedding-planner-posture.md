# Wedding Planner Posture

Wedding planning combines desk-based organization with active event execution. The work varies from quiet computer time designing timelines to 12+ hour wedding days on your feet. Female wedding planners must prepare their bodies for both extremes and everything in between.

## Dual Nature of the Work

### Office Planning
Computer work, client communication, vendor coordination.

### Site Visits
Venue tours, vendor meetings, walkthroughs.

### Event Execution
Long days on feet executing events.

### Client Meetings
Consultations and planning sessions.

## Office Work

### Computer Time
Proposals, timelines, budgets, and communication.

### Phone Calls
Extended phone conversations with vendors and clients.

### Design Work
Creating visual presentations and mood boards.

### Administrative Tasks
Contracts, invoicing, and record-keeping.

### Extended Standing

### Walking Distance
