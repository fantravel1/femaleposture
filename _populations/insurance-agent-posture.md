# Insurance Agent Posture

> **Phone, Computer, Client, Repeat:** Insurance work means hours of calls, screen time, and client meetings. Protect your body across all these demands.

---

## The Insurance Work Challenge

### Physical Demands

- **Phone calls** - Hours of talking, headset or handset
- **Computer work** - Quotes, policies, documentation
- **Client meetings** - In office or field visits
- **Driving** - For field agents
- **Extended sitting** - Most of the day

### Common Problems

- Neck pain (phone posture)
- Lower back issues (sitting)
- Shoulder tension
- Eye strain from screens
- Stress-related muscle tension

---

## Phone Posture

### The Neck Cramp

**Common problem:**
- Cradling phone between ear and shoulder
- Sustained neck tilt
- Hours of this = injury

**Solutions:**

**Headset (essential):**
- Hands-free calling
- Neutral neck position
- Can type while talking
- Worth the investment

**Speakerphone (when private):**
- No neck strain
- Hands free
- Position flexibility

**If handset:**
- Alternate ears
- Hold to ear, don't cradle
- Brief calls only

## Computer Work

### Standard Desk Setup

**Ergonomic basics:**
- Monitor at eye level
- Keyboard at elbow height
- Chair properly adjusted
- See [Desk Work Posture](/activities/desk-work/)

### Multi-Tasking Station

**Phone + computer:**
- Headset for phone
- Type while talking
- Screen visible during calls
- Neutral positions for both

## Client Meetings

### In-Office Meetings

**Your setup:**
- Comfortable meeting seating
- Appropriate table height
- Documents accessible
- Good position for conversation

**Client seating:**
- Appropriate distance
- Not straining to converse
- Natural positioning

### Field Visits

**At client locations:**
- Variable seating quality
- Make the best of it
- Keep visits appropriate length
- Movement between meetings

- Shoulder rolls
