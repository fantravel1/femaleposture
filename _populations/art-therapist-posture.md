# Art Therapist Posture

Art therapy combines psychological healing with creative expression. Female art therapists facilitate sessions involving various media, participate alongside clients, and manage studio spaces. The work involves standing, sitting on the floor, reaching, and demonstrating techniques throughout therapeutic sessions.

## Session Demands

### Client Engagement
Working alongside clients.

### Media Preparation
Setting up art supplies and materials.

### Demonstration
Showing techniques to clients.

### Movement Between Clients
Group session circulation.

## Various Art Media

### Painting
Easel and table work.

### Sculpting
Clay and 3D media.

### Drawing
Table-based work.

### Mixed Media
Varied material handling.

### Standing Duration

### Floor Work

### Reaching
Accessing supplies and displays.

### Hand Strain
Demonstrating techniques.
