# Jewelry Maker Posture

Jewelry making is meticulous work requiring sustained focus, fine motor control, and extended time in precise positions. Whether fabricating, setting stones, or finishing pieces, jewelers work at close range with small objects. Female jewelry makers need attention to the cumulative effects of this detailed work.
