# Technology Overwhelm and the Body

> **Always Connected, Always Tense:** Constant notifications, endless scrolling, and digital demands create physical stress patterns. Your body feels your inbox.

---

## The Digital Stress Response

### How Tech Creates Physical Stress

**Constant connection means:**
- Always-on stress response
- Notification reactivity
- Information overload
- Decision fatigue
- Attention fragmentation

### Body Manifestations

**You may notice:**
- Tension from constant vigilance
- Posture collapse from scrolling
- Eye strain and headaches
- Neck pain from devices
- Shallow breathing
