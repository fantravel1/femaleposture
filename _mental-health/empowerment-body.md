### What Empowerment Feels Like

- Expanded chest
- Strong core

**Physical expression:**
- Upright, tall spine
- Open shoulders
- Grounded feet
- Centered balance
- Appropriate space-taking

### Posture Practice

- Power pose practice

- Stretch into power

## Empowerment in Situations

### Speaking Up

**Physical voice:**
- Stand or sit tall
- Ground through feet
- Open chest
- Project from core
- Take appropriate space

### Setting Boundaries

**Physical no:**
- Solid stance
- Clear body language
- Grounded weight
- Steady presence
- Unwavering posture

### Receiving Recognition

**Accepting power:**
- Stand fully
- Receive with grace

- Embody capability
- Own your strength

- Minimal space

- Diminished presence

### Rebuilding Power

**Recovery:**
- Gradual expansion
- Strength building
- Posture correction
- Space reclaiming
- Power embodiment

Physical empowerment and psychological empowerment reinforce each other. Building your body's strength and presence supports your overall sense of capability and personal power.
