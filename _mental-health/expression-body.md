## Physical Nature of Expression

### How Body Expresses

**Expression channels:**
- Voice and speech
- Facial expressions
- Body posture
- Gestures and movement

### Free vs Blocked Expression

**The difference:**
- Free: unimpeded, flowing
- Blocked: restricted, held
- Free: authentic, full
- Blocked: partial, controlled
- Free: natural, spontaneous

## Expression Blocks

### Where Expression Gets Stuck

**Common blocks:**
- Throat tightness
- Jaw clenching
- Shoulder tension
- Chest constriction
- Breath holding

### Why Blocks Happen

**Origins:**
- Fear of judgment
- Past silencing
- Trauma history
- Social conditioning
- Self-protection
