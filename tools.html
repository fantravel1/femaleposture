How to Use These Tools

1. Start with the Complete Assessment

Take 10-15 minutes to do the full posture self-assessment. This gives you a baseline and helps identify priority areas.

2. Use the Wall Test for Tracking

Once a week or every few weeks, do the quick wall test to monitor your progress. It only takes 30 seconds.

3. Reassess After 4-6 Weeks

After consistent work on your posture, redo the complete assessment. Compare your results to see improvement.

Important Notes

  • These are screening tools, not diagnoses
  • Seek professional evaluation for pain or concerning findings
  • Progress takes time—be patient with yourself
  • Consistency matters more than perfection
