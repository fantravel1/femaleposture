# Bicycle Crunches: Oblique Exercise

> **At a Glance:** A dynamic ab exercise that targets the obliques through rotation.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Obliques, rectus abdominis |
| **Best For** | Core strength, rotational work |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Equipment** | Mat |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 3 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Lie on back, hands behind head
2. Lift shoulders off ground
3. Bring right elbow toward left knee
4. While extending right leg
5. Switch sides in pedaling motion
6. Keep lower back pressed down

### Form Tips

- Don't pull on neck
- Rotate from ribcage
- Control the movement
- Quality over speed

---

## Common Mistakes

- Pulling neck forward
- Moving too fast
- Not rotating fully
- Arching lower back
