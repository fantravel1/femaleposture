# Butterfly Stretch: Hip Opener

> **At a Glance:** A gentle, seated stretch that opens the hips and inner thighs.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Adductors, hip flexors |
| **Best For** | Hip mobility, inner thigh flexibility |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Mat |
| **Time** | 3-5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Sit on floor
2. Bring soles of feet together
3. Let knees fall out to sides
4. Hold feet or ankles
5. Sit tall
6. Gently press knees down
7. Hold 30-60 seconds

### Variations

- Forward fold for deeper stretch
- Reclined butterfly (lying down)
- Against wall for back support

---

## Benefits

- Opens inner thighs
- Improves hip mobility
- Gentle and accessible
- Good for beginners
