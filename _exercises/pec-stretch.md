# Pec Stretch: Chest Opener

> **At a Glance:** Stretching the chest muscles helps reverse rounded shoulder posture.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Pectoralis major and minor |
| **Best For** | Rounded shoulders, chest tightness |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Doorway or wall |
| **Time** | 2-3 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Multiple times daily |

---

## How to Perform (Doorway)

1. Stand in doorway
2. Place forearms on doorframe
3. Elbows at shoulder height
4. Step through doorway
5. Feel stretch across chest
6. Hold 30-60 seconds

### Arm Positions

- Low: Targets upper pec
- Level: Targets mid pec
- High: Targets lower pec

---

## Benefits

- Opens chest
- Improves posture
- Counteracts sitting
- Better breathing
