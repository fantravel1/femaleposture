# Dumbbell Row: Back Strength

> **At a Glance:** A fundamental back exercise that counters rounded shoulders.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Lats, rhomboids, rear delts |
| **Best For** | Upper back strength, posture |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Equipment** | Dumbbell, bench |
| **Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 2-3 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Support one hand and knee on bench
2. Other foot on floor
3. Hold dumbbell in free hand
4. Row dumbbell to hip
5. Squeeze shoulder blade
6. Lower with control
7. Switch sides

---

## Tips

- Keep back flat
- Don't rotate torso
- Pull elbow back, not up
- Squeeze at top
