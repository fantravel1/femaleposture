# Jumping Jacks: Full Body Warm-Up

> **At a Glance:** A classic exercise that warms up the entire body quickly.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Full body, cardiovascular |
| **Best For** | Warm-up, cardio |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | None |
| **Time** | 2-5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | As warm-up |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand with feet together, arms at sides
2. Jump feet apart while raising arms overhead
3. Jump back to start
4. Maintain rhythm

---

## Low-Impact Version

- Step out instead of jumping
- Good for joint issues
- Same arm movement

---

## Benefits

- Quick warm-up
- Raises heart rate
- Full body movement
- No equipment needed
