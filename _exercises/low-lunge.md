# Low Lunge: Hip Flexor Opener

> **At a Glance:** A deep hip flexor stretch that counteracts sitting.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Hip flexors, psoas, quads |
| **Best For** | Hip flexor tightness, pelvic posture |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Mat, padding for knee |
| **Time** | 2-3 minutes per side |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Start in lunge position
2. Lower back knee to floor
3. Front knee over ankle
4. Tuck pelvis slightly
5. Sink hips forward and down
6. Hands on front thigh or overhead
7. Hold 30-60 seconds

---

## Variations

- Crescent lunge: back knee lifted
- Twisted low lunge: add rotation
- Low lunge with quad stretch
