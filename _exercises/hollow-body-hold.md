# Hollow Body Hold: Core Bracing

> **At a Glance:** A gymnastics staple that teaches total core engagement and body tension.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Entire core, hip flexors |
| **Best For** | Core strength, body control |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Equipment** | Mat |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 3-4 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Lie on back
2. Press lower back into floor
3. Lift shoulders and legs off ground
4. Arms by ears or at sides
5. Body forms gentle curve (like a banana)
6. Hold while breathing
7. Start with 10-20 seconds

### Form Tips

- Lower back must stay pressed down
- If back arches, raise legs higher
- Legs together, pointed toes
- Chin tucked slightly

---

## Progressions

1. **Tuck hold** - Knees bent
2. **One leg extended** - Other tucked
3. **Full hollow** - Both legs straight
4. **Arms overhead** - Most challenging
