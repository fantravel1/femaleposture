# Supine Twist: Spinal Rotation Stretch

> **At a Glance:** A gentle lying twist that releases the spine and stretches the back and hips.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Spine, lower back, hip rotators |
| **Best For** | Spinal mobility, back relief |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Mat |
| **Time** | 3-5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Lie on your back
2. Extend arms out to sides
3. Draw knees toward chest
4. Drop knees to one side
5. Turn head opposite direction
6. Keep shoulders on floor
7. Hold 30-60 seconds
8. Switch sides

---

## Variations

- Straight leg version (deeper)
- Eagle legs twist
- Scorpion variation

---

## Benefits

- Releases spine
- Stretches lower back
- Opens chest
- Relaxing before sleep
