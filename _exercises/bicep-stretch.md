# Bicep Stretch: Arm Opener

> **At a Glance:** Stretching the biceps helps with overhead mobility and arm flexibility.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Biceps brachii |
| **Best For** | Arm flexibility, shoulder mobility |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Wall or doorway |
| **Time** | 1-2 minutes |
| **Frequency** | As needed |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand next to wall
2. Place palm flat on wall, fingers down
3. Turn body away from wall
4. Feel stretch in front of arm
5. Hold 20-30 seconds
6. Switch arms
