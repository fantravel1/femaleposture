# High Knees: Cardio & Hip Flexor

> **At a Glance:** A simple cardio move that warms up the body and engages hip flexors.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Hip flexors, core, cardiovascular |
| **Best For** | Warm-up, cardio |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | None |
| **Time** | 2-5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | As warm-up |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand tall
2. Run in place
3. Drive knees up to hip height
4. Pump arms opposite to legs
5. Stay on balls of feet
6. Maintain upright posture

---

## Variations

- Slow march (beginner)
- Quick drive (intermediate)
- Sprint pace (advanced)

---

## Uses

- Dynamic warm-up
- Cardio intervals
- Active recovery
- Movement prep
