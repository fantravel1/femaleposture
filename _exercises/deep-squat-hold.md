# Deep Squat Hold: Hip & Ankle Mobility

> **At a Glance:** The resting squat position restores natural mobility in hips and ankles.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Hips, ankles, thoracic spine |
| **Best For** | Lower body mobility |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Equipment** | None |
| **Time** | 2-5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Feet shoulder-width or wider
2. Toes can angle out slightly
3. Lower into deep squat
4. Heels stay on floor (if possible)
5. Chest up, arms inside knees
6. Relax and breathe
7. Accumulate 3-5 minutes daily

---

## Modifications

- Hold onto doorframe
- Put rolled towel under heels
- Use wall for back support

---

## Benefits

- Restores natural position
- Improves hip mobility
- Stretches ankles
- Counteracts sitting
