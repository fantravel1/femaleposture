# Warrior III: Balance & Core Strength

> **At a Glance:** A challenging single-leg balance that strengthens the entire body.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Standing leg, glutes, core |
| **Best For** | Balance, core strength |
| **Difficulty** | Advanced |
| **Equipment** | None (wall optional) |
| **Time** | 1-2 minutes per side |
| **Frequency** | 3-4 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand on one leg
2. Hinge forward at hips
3. Extend back leg behind
4. Body forms T-shape
5. Arms forward, at sides, or back
6. Hold 15-30 seconds

---

## Modifications

- Hands on blocks
- Hands on wall
- Fingertips touch chair
