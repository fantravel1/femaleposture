# Cobra Stretch: Back Extension

> **At a Glance:** A gentle back extension that opens the chest and strengthens the spine.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Back extensors, chest, abs |
| **Best For** | Back extension, chest opening |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Mat |
| **Time** | 3-5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Lie face down
2. Place hands under shoulders
3. Press through hands, lift chest
4. Keep hips on floor
5. Keep shoulders down
6. Hold 15-30 seconds

### Levels

- Baby cobra: Slight lift, forearms stay down
- Low cobra: Arms partially extended
- Full cobra: Arms fully extended

---

## Caution

- Avoid if sharp back pain
- Don't overextend
- Listen to your body
