# Standing Forward Fold: Hamstring & Back Release

> **At a Glance:** A gravity-assisted stretch that releases the spine and hamstrings.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Hamstrings, lower back, spine |
| **Best For** | Spinal decompression, hamstrings |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | None |
| **Time** | 1-3 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand with feet hip-width apart
2. Hinge at hips, fold forward
3. Let head and arms hang
4. Knees can be slightly bent
5. Let gravity do the work
6. Hold 30-60 seconds

---

## Variations

- Grab opposite elbows
- Clasp hands behind back
- Gentle side-to-side sway
