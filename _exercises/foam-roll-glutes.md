# Glute Foam Rolling: Hip Muscle Release

> **At a Glance:** Rolling the glutes and hip rotators releases tension that can cause low back and hip pain.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Glutes, piriformis, hip rotators |
| **Best For** | Hip tightness, low back relief |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Foam roller or ball |
| **Time** | 3-5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Sit on roller
2. Cross one ankle over opposite knee
3. Lean toward crossed leg side
4. Roll around the glute
5. Pause on tender spots
6. Switch sides

### For Piriformis

- Use a tennis or lacrosse ball
- Target the deep hip area
- Smaller area, more precise
