# Donkey Kicks: Glute Activation

> **At a Glance:** A simple but effective exercise that targets the glutes through isolated hip extension.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Gluteus maximus |
| **Best For** | Glute activation, hip extension |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Mat |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 3-4 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Start on hands and knees
2. Keep core engaged
3. Lift one leg, knee bent at 90 degrees
4. Push foot toward ceiling
5. Squeeze glute at top
6. Lower with control
7. Complete reps, then switch legs

### Form Tips

- Don't arch lower back
- Keep hips level
- Focus on squeezing glute
- Movement comes from hip

---

## Variations

### With Band
- Add resistance band above knees
- Increased glute activation

### Straight Leg
- Extend leg straight back
- Different muscle emphasis
