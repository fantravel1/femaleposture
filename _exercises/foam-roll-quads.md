# Quad Foam Rolling: Front Thigh Release

> **At a Glance:** Rolling the front of the thighs releases quad tension that affects pelvic position.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Quadriceps |
| **Best For** | Hip flexor relief, pelvic alignment |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Foam roller |
| **Time** | 3 minutes per leg |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Lie face down, roller under front of thighs
2. Support yourself on forearms
3. Roll from hip to just above knee
4. Pause on tender spots
5. Can do one leg at a time for more pressure
6. Rotate leg in/out to target different areas

---

## Benefits

- Releases quad tension
- Reduces hip flexor tightness
- Improves pelvic position
- Better squat and lunge mechanics
