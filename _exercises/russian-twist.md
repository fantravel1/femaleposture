# Russian Twist: Rotational Core

> **At a Glance:** A seated twisting exercise that targets the obliques through rotation.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Obliques, hip flexors |
| **Best For** | Rotational strength, core |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Equipment** | None (optional weight) |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 3 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Sit with knees bent, feet flat or elevated
2. Lean back slightly (V-sit position)
3. Clasp hands in front of chest
4. Rotate torso side to side
5. Touch hands to floor on each side
6. Keep core engaged throughout

### With Weight

- Hold dumbbell, medicine ball, or kettlebell
- Increases challenge
- Start light

---

## Caution

- May not be suitable for low back issues
- Keep movement controlled
- Start without weight
