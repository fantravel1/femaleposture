# 90-90 Hip Stretch: Hip Rotation Mobility

> **At a Glance:** This position stretches both hips simultaneously in different directions of rotation.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Hip rotators, glutes, piriformis |
| **Best For** | Hip mobility, rotation flexibility |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Equipment** | Mat |
| **Time** | 5-10 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Sit on floor
2. Front leg: 90 degrees in front
3. Back leg: 90 degrees behind
4. Both knees bent at 90 degrees
5. Sit up tall
6. Hold 30-60 seconds
7. Switch sides

### Progressions

- Lean forward over front leg
- Lean back over back leg
- Rotate torso toward front knee

---

## Benefits

- Stretches both hip rotations
- Improves sitting posture
- Reduces hip tightness
- Common yoga position
