# Happy Baby Pose: Hip & Low Back Release

> **At a Glance:** A gentle, relaxing pose that stretches inner thighs and releases the lower back.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Inner thighs, lower back, hips |
| **Best For** | Hip opening, relaxation |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Mat |
| **Time** | 2-5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Lie on your back
2. Bring knees toward chest
3. Grab outside edges of feet
4. Open knees wider than torso
5. Pull feet toward armpits
6. Keep lower back on floor
7. Rock gently side to side if desired

---

## Benefits

- Gentle hip opener
- Releases lower back
- Calming and relaxing
- Great before sleep
