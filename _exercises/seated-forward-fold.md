# Seated Forward Fold: Hamstring & Back Stretch

> **At a Glance:** A calming stretch that lengthens the entire posterior chain.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Hamstrings, lower back, calves |
| **Best For** | Flexibility, relaxation |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Mat |
| **Time** | 2-5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Sit with legs extended in front
2. Feet flexed, toes pointing up
3. Inhale, sit tall
4. Exhale, hinge at hips forward
5. Reach toward feet (use strap if needed)
6. Hold 30-60 seconds

### Tips

- Bend knees if hamstrings are tight
- Lengthen spine, don't round
- Breathe into the stretch
