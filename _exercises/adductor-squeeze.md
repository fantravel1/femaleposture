# Adductor Squeeze: Inner Thigh Strength

> **At a Glance:** Squeezing against resistance strengthens inner thighs and connects to pelvic floor function.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Adductors (inner thighs) |
| **Best For** | Inner thigh strength, pelvic stability |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Pillow, ball, or yoga block |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Lie on back, knees bent
2. Place pillow/ball between knees
3. Squeeze knees together
4. Hold 5-10 seconds
5. Release slowly
6. Repeat 10-15 times

### Variations

- Seated version (at desk)
- Standing version
- With glute bridge

---

## Benefits

- Strengthens inner thighs
- Supports pelvic floor
- Easy and accessible
- No special equipment needed
