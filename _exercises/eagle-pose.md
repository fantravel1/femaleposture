# Eagle Pose: Shoulder & Hip Stretch

> **At a Glance:** A balance pose that wraps arms and legs for deep stretching.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Upper back, shoulders, hips |
| **Best For** | Upper back stretch, balance |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Equipment** | None |
| **Time** | 1-2 minutes per side |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

### Arms Only (Seated or Standing)
1. Wrap right arm under left
2. Bring palms together or backs of hands touch
3. Lift elbows, feel upper back stretch

### Full Pose
1. Stand on left leg
2. Wrap right leg over left
3. Wrap arms (right under left)
4. Sit into slight squat
5. Hold 30-60 seconds

---

## Benefits

- Stretches upper back
- Opens between shoulder blades
- Challenges balance
- Releases shoulder tension
