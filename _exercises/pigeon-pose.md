# Pigeon Pose: Deep Hip Stretch

> **At a Glance:** A yoga staple that provides deep stretching for tight hips and the piriformis muscle.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Hip rotators, glutes, piriformis |
| **Best For** | Hip flexibility, sciatica relief |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Equipment** | Mat, props optional |
| **Time** | 5-10 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Start in tabletop or downward dog
2. Bring right knee toward right wrist
3. Angle right shin (more parallel = deeper)
4. Extend left leg straight back
5. Square hips toward front
6. Fold forward or stay upright
7. Hold 1-2 minutes per side

### Modifications

- Place block or pillow under hip
- Keep torso upright if folding is difficult
- Reclined figure-4 as alternative

---

## Benefits

- Deep hip opener
- Releases piriformis
- May help sciatica
- Releases stored tension
