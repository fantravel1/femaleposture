# Standing Quad Stretch: Thigh Flexibility

> **At a Glance:** The classic standing stretch for the front of the thigh.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Quadriceps |
| **Best For** | Thigh flexibility, hip mobility |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Wall for balance optional |
| **Time** | 1-2 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand tall (hold wall if needed)
2. Bend one knee, bring heel to glute
3. Grab ankle or foot
4. Keep knees together
5. Tuck pelvis slightly
6. Hold 20-30 seconds
7. Switch legs

---

## Tips

- Don't arch back
- Knees stay close together
- Pelvis tucked increases stretch
