# Seated Good Morning: Hip Hinge Practice

> **At a Glance:** Learn the hip hinge pattern safely from a seated position.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Hamstrings, back extensors |
| **Best For** | Hip hinge learning, hamstring stretch |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Chair |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Sit on edge of chair
2. Feet flat, hands on thighs
3. Keep spine straight
4. Hinge forward at hips
5. Feel hamstring stretch
6. Return to upright
7. Repeat 10-15 times

---

## Purpose

- Teaches hip hinge
- Safe for beginners
- Prepares for deadlifts
- Stretches hamstrings
