# External Rotation: Rotator Cuff

> **At a Glance:** Essential for rotator cuff health and shoulder stability.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Infraspinatus, teres minor |
| **Best For** | Shoulder health, posture |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Light band or dumbbell |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 3-4 times per week |

---

## How to Perform (Side-Lying)

1. Lie on side, arm resting on side
2. Hold light weight in top hand
3. Elbow at 90 degrees, against body
4. Rotate forearm up toward ceiling
5. Lower with control
6. Switch sides

## Standing with Band

1. Band anchored at elbow height
2. Elbow at side, bent 90 degrees
3. Rotate forearm outward
4. Control return
