# Neck Tilts: Gentle Neck Stretch

> **At a Glance:** Side bending the neck stretches the upper trapezius and relieves common tension patterns.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Upper trapezius, scalenes |
| **Best For** | Neck tension, headache relief |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | None |
| **Time** | 2-3 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Multiple times daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Sit or stand tall
2. Drop right ear toward right shoulder
3. Keep left shoulder down
4. Hold 20-30 seconds
5. Optional: gentle hand pressure on head
6. Repeat on left side

---

## Variations

- Add rotation to look up or down
- Hold opposite shoulder down
- Combine with chin tuck

---

## Benefits

- Relieves neck tension
- Stretches upper traps
- Quick office stretch
- Helps with headaches
