# Half Moon Pose: Balance & Strength

> **At a Glance:** A challenging balance pose that opens hips and strengthens legs.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Standing leg, hips, core |
| **Best For** | Balance, hip opening |
| **Difficulty** | Advanced |
| **Equipment** | Block recommended |
| **Time** | 1-2 minutes per side |
| **Frequency** | 3-4 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. From Triangle pose
2. Shift weight to front leg
3. Lift back leg parallel to floor
4. Bottom hand on block or floor
5. Top arm reaches up
6. Stack hips, open chest
7. Hold 15-30 seconds

---

## Tips

- Use block for support
- Keep standing leg strong
- Gaze down first, then up
