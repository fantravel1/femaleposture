# Rear Delt Fly: Posterior Shoulder

> **At a Glance:** An essential exercise for the back of the shoulders and posture.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Rear deltoids, upper back |
| **Best For** | Posture, rounded shoulders |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Equipment** | Light dumbbells |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 2-3 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Hinge forward at hips (bent over)
2. Dumbbells hanging below
3. Lift arms out to sides
4. Squeeze shoulder blades
5. Lower with control
6. Repeat

---

## Tips

- Use light weight
- Lead with elbows
- Don't swing body
- Focus on squeeze
