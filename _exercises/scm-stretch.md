# SCM Stretch: Front Neck Release

> **At a Glance:** The SCM muscle on the front of the neck often tightens with forward head posture.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Sternocleidomastoid (SCM) |
| **Best For** | Forward head posture, neck tension |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | None |
| **Time** | 2 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Sit or stand tall
2. Tilt head back slightly
3. Rotate head to one side
4. Feel stretch on front/side of neck
5. Hold 20-30 seconds
6. Switch sides
