# Goblet Squat: Perfect Squat Form

> **At a Glance:** Holding weight at your chest naturally encourages proper squat form and an upright torso.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Quads, glutes, core |
| **Best For** | Squat form, leg strength |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Dumbbell or kettlebell |
| **Time** | 5-10 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 2-3 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Hold weight at chest level (goblet position)
2. Feet shoulder-width apart
3. Squat down, keeping chest up
4. Elbows can touch inside of knees
5. Push through heels to stand

### Form Tips

- Weight stays close to body
- Elbows pointing down
- Knees track over toes
- Natural upright posture

---

## Benefits

- Teaches proper squat mechanics
- Front load keeps you upright
- Self-correcting form
- Great progression tool
- Works core simultaneously
