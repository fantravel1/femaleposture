# Warrior II: Hip Opening Strength Pose

> **At a Glance:** A hip-opening standing pose that builds leg endurance.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Legs, hips, core |
| **Best For** | Hip opening, leg strength |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Equipment** | None |
| **Time** | 2-3 minutes per side |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Step feet wide (4-5 feet)
2. Turn front foot out 90 degrees
3. Back foot slightly in
4. Bend front knee over ankle
5. Arms extend parallel to floor
6. Gaze over front hand
7. Hold 30-60 seconds

---

## Benefits

- Opens hips
- Strengthens legs
- Builds stamina
- Improves focus
