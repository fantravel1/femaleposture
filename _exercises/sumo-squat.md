# Sumo Squat: Inner Thigh & Glute Focus

> **At a Glance:** The wide stance of sumo squats emphasizes inner thighs and glutes while being easier on the knees.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Inner thighs (adductors), glutes, quads |
| **Best For** | Inner thigh strength, hip mobility |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Optional: dumbbell or kettlebell |
| **Time** | 5-10 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 2-3 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand with feet wider than shoulders
2. Turn toes out 45 degrees
3. Keep chest up, core engaged
4. Lower down by bending knees
5. Knees track over toes
6. Push through heels to stand

### Form Tips

- Keep knees pushing outward
- Don't let knees cave in
- Maintain upright torso
- Go as deep as comfortable

---

## Benefits

- Targets inner thighs
- Easier on knees than regular squat
- Improves hip mobility
- Works glutes effectively
- Good squat alternative
