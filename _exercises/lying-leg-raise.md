# Lying Leg Raises: Lower Ab Exercise

> **At a Glance:** A challenging exercise for the lower abdominals.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Lower abs, hip flexors |
| **Best For** | Core strength |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Equipment** | Mat |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 3 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Lie on back, legs extended
2. Hands under lower back or at sides
3. Keep lower back pressed down
4. Raise legs toward ceiling
5. Lower with control
6. Don't let back arch
7. Repeat 10-15 times

---

## Modifications

- Bend knees (easier)
- One leg at a time
- Don't lower all the way

---

## Caution

If lower back arches, modify or choose easier exercise.
