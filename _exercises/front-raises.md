# Front Raises: Anterior Deltoid

> **At a Glance:** An isolation exercise for the front of the shoulders.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Anterior deltoids |
| **Best For** | Shoulder strength |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Light dumbbells |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 2 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand with dumbbells in front of thighs
2. Arms straight or slight bend
3. Raise one or both arms forward
4. Stop at shoulder height
5. Lower with control
6. Repeat

---

## Note

Front delts often get enough work from pressing exercises. Prioritize rear delts and lateral raises for posture.
