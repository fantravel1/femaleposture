# Calf Foam Rolling: Lower Leg Release

> **At a Glance:** Rolling the calves improves ankle mobility and releases tension from walking and standing.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Gastrocnemius, soleus |
| **Best For** | Ankle mobility, calf tightness |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Foam roller |
| **Time** | 3 minutes per leg |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Sit with roller under one calf
2. Cross other leg on top for pressure
3. Roll from ankle to below knee
4. Rotate leg to hit inside and outside
5. Pause on tender spots
6. Switch legs

---

## Benefits

- Releases calf tension
- Improves ankle mobility
- Better squat depth
- Reduced plantar fascia stress
