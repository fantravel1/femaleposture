# Marble Pickups: Foot Dexterity Exercise

> **At a Glance:** Marble pickups challenge toe strength and coordination while making foot exercises engaging and measurable.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Toe flexors, intrinsic foot muscles |
| **Best For** | Foot strength, toe dexterity |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Marbles or small objects, bowl |
| **Time** | 3-5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

### Setup

1. Place 10-20 marbles on floor
2. Put empty bowl nearby
3. Sit in chair with bare feet

### The Exercise

1. Pick up one marble with toes
2. Lift and drop in bowl
3. Repeat with each marble
4. Try both feet

---

## Variations

### Towel Scrunches

**Alternative version:**
1. Place towel flat on floor
2. Scrunch toward you using only toes
3. Repeat 3-5 times

### Pencil Grip

**Precision work:**
1. Pick up pencil with toes
2. Hold for 10 seconds
3. Place down with control

---

## Benefits

- Strengthens toe flexors
- Improves foot dexterity
- Fun and engaging
- Measurable progress
- Good for all ages
