# Internal Rotation: Rotator Cuff

> **At a Glance:** Strengthens the subscapularis for balanced rotator cuff.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Subscapularis |
| **Best For** | Rotator cuff balance |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Light band |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 2 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand with band anchored at elbow height
2. Hold band, elbow at side, bent 90 degrees
3. Rotate forearm across body
4. Control the return
5. Switch sides

---

## Note

Most people get enough internal rotation from daily activities. Prioritize external rotation for posture.
