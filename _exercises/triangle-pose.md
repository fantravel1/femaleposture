# Triangle Pose: Side Body Stretch

> **At a Glance:** A standing pose that stretches the sides and opens the chest.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Side body, hamstrings, hips |
| **Best For** | Side flexibility, chest opening |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Equipment** | Block optional |
| **Time** | 2-3 minutes per side |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Wide stance, front foot out
2. Legs straight
3. Hinge at front hip, reach down
4. Lower hand to shin, block, or floor
5. Top arm reaches to ceiling
6. Gaze up or forward
7. Hold 30-60 seconds
