# Prone T Raises: Mid Back Activation

> **At a Glance:** Lying face-down arm raises that target mid-back muscles.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Mid trapezius, rhomboids |
| **Best For** | Scapular stability |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Mat |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 3 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Lie face down, arms out to sides (T shape)
2. Thumbs pointing up
3. Lift arms slightly off floor
4. Squeeze shoulder blades together
5. Hold 3-5 seconds
6. Lower with control
7. Repeat 10-15 times
