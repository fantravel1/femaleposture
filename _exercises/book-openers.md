# Book Openers: Thoracic Rotation

> **At a Glance:** A side-lying rotation exercise that opens the chest and improves upper back mobility.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Thoracic spine, chest, shoulders |
| **Best For** | Upper back mobility, chest opening |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Mat |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Lie on side, knees bent to 90 degrees
2. Stack knees and arms in front
3. Keep bottom arm on floor
4. Rotate top arm open (like opening a book)
5. Follow hand with your eyes
6. Try to touch floor behind you
7. Return slowly
8. Repeat 8-10 times per side

---

## Benefits

- Improves thoracic rotation
- Opens chest
- Counteracts slouching
- Feels great
