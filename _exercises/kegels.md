# Kegel Exercises: Pelvic Floor Strength

> **At a Glance:** Strengthening exercises for the pelvic floor muscles.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Pelvic floor muscles |
| **Best For** | Pelvic support, continence |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | None |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Identify pelvic floor muscles (as if stopping urine flow)
2. Contract and lift pelvic floor
3. Hold 5-10 seconds
4. Release fully
5. Rest 5-10 seconds
6. Repeat 10 times

---

## Tips

- Don't hold breath
- Don't squeeze glutes or thighs
- Focus on lift, not just squeeze
- Relaxation equally important

---

## When Needed

- Postpartum
- Pre/post menopause
- Incontinence issues
- Core weakness

---

## Note

If experiencing pelvic floor dysfunction, consult a pelvic floor physical therapist.
