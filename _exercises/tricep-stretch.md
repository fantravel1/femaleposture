# Tricep Stretch: Arm Flexibility

> **At a Glance:** A classic stretch for the back of the upper arm.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Triceps brachii |
| **Best For** | Overhead mobility, arm flexibility |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | None |
| **Time** | 1-2 minutes |
| **Frequency** | As needed |

---

## How to Perform

1. Raise one arm overhead
2. Bend elbow, reach hand down back
3. Use other hand to gently press elbow
4. Feel stretch in back of arm
5. Hold 20-30 seconds
6. Switch arms

---

## Benefits

- Improves overhead reach
- Releases arm tension
- Better shoulder mobility
- Quick stretch anywhere
