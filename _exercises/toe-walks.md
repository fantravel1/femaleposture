# Toe Walks: Calf Strengthening

> **At a Glance:** Walking on your toes builds calf strength and ankle stability through functional movement.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Gastrocnemius, soleus (calves) |
| **Best For** | Calf strength, ankle stability |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | None |
| **Time** | 2-3 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 3-4 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand tall with good posture
2. Rise up onto balls of feet
3. Walk forward 20-30 steps
4. Rest and repeat 2-3 times

### Form Tips

- Stay as high as possible on toes
- Keep core engaged
- Take small, controlled steps
- Maintain upright posture

---

## Benefits

- Strengthens calf muscles
- Improves ankle stability
- Enhances balance
- Functional strength for daily activities
