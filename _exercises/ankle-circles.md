# Ankle Circles: Simple Joint Mobility

> **At a Glance:** Ankle circles are a simple way to maintain ankle mobility and promote circulation, especially important during prolonged sitting.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Ankle joint mobility |
| **Best For** | Sitting breaks, warm-up, circulation |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | None |
| **Time** | 1-2 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Multiple times daily |

---

## Why Ankle Mobility Matters

### For Posture

- Affects how you stand and walk
- Limited ankle mobility changes gait
- Can affect balance
- Connects to whole kinetic chain

### During Sitting

- Promotes circulation
- Prevents stiffness
- Easy to do at desk
- No one notices

---

## Basic Ankle Circles

### Seated Version

1. Sit with feet off floor (or lift one)
2. Point toes down
3. Circle ankle slowly clockwise
4. 10 circles
5. Circle counter-clockwise
6. 10 circles
7. Switch feet

### Standing Version

1. Balance on one foot (hold support if needed)
2. Lift other foot slightly
3. Circle ankle 10 times each direction
4. Switch feet

---

## Variations

### Alphabet Writing

**Full range of motion:**
1. Lift foot
2. "Write" alphabet with toes
3. Moves ankle through all ranges
4. Takes about 1 minute per foot

### Point and Flex

**Two-direction focus:**
1. Point toes away (plantarflexion)
2. Flex toes toward you (dorsiflexion)
3. 15-20 repetitions

### Inversion/Eversion

**Side-to-side:**
1. Turn sole inward
2. Turn sole outward
3. 10 each direction

---

## When to Practice

### Best Times

- During long sitting sessions
- On airplane or long drives
- Before walking or exercise
- When feet feel stiff
- Part of warm-up routine

---

## Benefits Beyond Posture

- Improves circulation
- Reduces swelling during sitting
- Maintains joint health
- Prevents stiffness
- Prepares for activity

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How often should I do ankle circles?

During prolonged sitting, every 20-30 minutes. Otherwise, include in daily warm-up.

### Can this help with ankle stiffness?

Yes, regular practice maintains and improves ankle mobility.

### Is it normal to hear clicking?

Painless clicking is usually normal. If it's painful, consult a professional.
