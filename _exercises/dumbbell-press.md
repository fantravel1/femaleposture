# Dumbbell Press: Chest Strength

> **At a Glance:** A fundamental chest exercise using dumbbells.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Chest, shoulders, triceps |
| **Best For** | Chest strength |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Equipment** | Dumbbells, bench |
| **Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 2 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Lie on bench, dumbbells at chest
2. Feet flat on floor
3. Press dumbbells up
4. Slight arch in wrists
5. Lower with control
6. Repeat

---

## Benefits

- Builds chest strength
- Better range than barbell
- Addresses imbalances
- Core engagement
