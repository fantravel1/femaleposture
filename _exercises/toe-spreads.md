# Toe Spreads: Foot Strength & Mobility

> **At a Glance:** Toe spreads activate the small muscles in your feet that often weaken from shoe wearing, improving foot function and stability.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Intrinsic foot muscles |
| **Best For** | Foot strength, bunion prevention, balance |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | None |
| **Time** | 2-3 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

### Basic Toe Spread

1. Sit comfortably with bare feet flat on floor
2. Spread all toes apart as wide as possible
3. Hold for 5 seconds
4. Relax toes back together
5. Repeat 10-15 times

### Tips for Success

- Focus on spreading, not scrunching
- Keep feet flat on floor
- Don't lift toes, just spread them

## Variations

### With Toe Spacers

**For increased stretch:**
1. Use silicone toe spacers
2. Practice spreading against resistance
3. Helps retrain toe position

### Big Toe Focus

**Isolate big toe:**
1. Spread big toe away from others
2. Hold 5 seconds
3. Common weakness area

---

## Benefits

- Strengthens foot muscles
- May help prevent bunions
- Improves toe mobility
- Better balance foundation
- Counteracts narrow shoes
