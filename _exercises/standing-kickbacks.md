# Standing Kickbacks: Glute Exercise

> **At a Glance:** An accessible standing exercise that works the glutes while challenging balance.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Glutes, hamstrings |
| **Best For** | Glute strength, balance |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Chair for balance (optional) |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand tall, hold chair if needed
2. Shift weight to one leg
3. Extend other leg straight back
4. Squeeze glute at top
5. Return with control
6. Complete reps, then switch

### Form Tips

- Don't lean forward excessively
- Keep leg straight
- Focus on glute squeeze
- Small, controlled movement

---

## Benefits

- Works glutes
- Improves balance
- Can do anywhere
- No equipment needed
