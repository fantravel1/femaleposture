# Shoulder CARs: Joint Mobility

> **At a Glance:** Controlled circles that maintain full shoulder range of motion.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Shoulder joint |
| **Best For** | Shoulder mobility, joint health |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Equipment** | None |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand with arm at side
2. Make fist with thumb out
3. Slowly raise arm forward and up
4. Continue overhead
5. Rotate palm, continue back
6. Complete full circle to side
7. Reverse direction
8. 3-5 circles each arm

---

## Tips

- Move slowly (30-60 seconds per circle)
- Keep shoulder down from ear
- Explore full range
- Stay tension-free
