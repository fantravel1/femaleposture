# Standing Calf Stretch: Lower Leg Flexibility

> **At a Glance:** The classic wall stretch for calf muscles.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Gastrocnemius |
| **Best For** | Ankle mobility, calf tightness |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Wall |
| **Time** | 2 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Face wall, hands on wall
2. Step one foot back
3. Keep back leg straight
4. Press back heel into floor
5. Lean into wall
6. Hold 30 seconds
7. Switch legs

### For Soleus

- Same position but bend back knee
- Targets deeper calf muscle
