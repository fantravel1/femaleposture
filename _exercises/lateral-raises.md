# Lateral Raises: Shoulder Width

> **At a Glance:** An isolation exercise targeting the middle portion of the shoulders.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Lateral deltoids |
| **Best For** | Shoulder strength |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Light dumbbells |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 2-3 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand with dumbbells at sides
2. Slight bend in elbows
3. Raise arms out to sides
4. Stop at shoulder height
5. Lower with control
6. Repeat

---

## Tips

- Use light weight
- Control the movement
- Don't swing or use momentum
- Slight forward lean is okay
