# Shoulder Press: Overhead Strength

> **At a Glance:** A fundamental exercise for shoulder strength and overhead mobility.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Deltoids, triceps, core |
| **Best For** | Shoulder strength |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Equipment** | Dumbbells |
| **Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 2 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand or sit, dumbbells at shoulders
2. Core engaged
3. Press weights overhead
4. Arms fully extended
5. Lower with control
6. Repeat

---

## Variations

- Seated (more stable)
- Standing (more core demand)
- Arnold press (rotation)
