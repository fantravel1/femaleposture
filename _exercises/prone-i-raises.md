# Prone I Raises: Lower Trap Activation

> **At a Glance:** Lying face-down arm raises that target the lower trapezius.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Lower trapezius |
| **Best For** | Scapular stability, posture |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Mat |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | 3 times per week |

---

## How to Perform

1. Lie face down, arms by ears (I shape)
2. Thumbs pointing up
3. Lift arms slightly off floor
4. Hold 3-5 seconds
5. Lower with control
6. Repeat 10-15 times

---

## Part of Y-T-I Series

- Y Raises: Arms in Y position
- T Raises: Arms out to sides
- I Raises: Arms overhead
