# Standing Hip Abduction: Outer Hip Exercise

> **At a Glance:** Lifting the leg to the side strengthens hip abductors essential for walking and pelvic stability.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Gluteus medius, hip abductors |
| **Best For** | Hip stability, balance |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | Chair for balance |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand tall, hold chair for balance
2. Shift weight to one leg
3. Lift other leg out to side
4. Keep hips level
5. Lower with control
6. Complete reps, then switch

### Form Tips

- Don't lean away from lifting leg
- Keep toes pointing forward
- Small, controlled movement
- Quality over height

---

## With Resistance Band

1. Loop band around ankles
2. Perform same movement
3. Band adds resistance
4. Increased muscle activation
