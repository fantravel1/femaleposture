# Hip CARs: Controlled Articular Rotations

> **At a Glance:** Slow, controlled circles that take the hip through its full range.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Hip joint, hip muscles |
| **Best For** | Hip mobility, joint health |
| **Difficulty** | Intermediate |
| **Equipment** | None |
| **Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand on one leg (hold support)
2. Lift knee toward chest
3. Open knee out to side
4. Rotate leg back behind you
5. Return to start
6. Complete 3-5 circles each direction
7. Switch legs

---

## Tips

- Move slowly with control
- Keep torso stable
- Explore full range
- No pain

---

## Benefits

- Maintains hip mobility
- Joint health
- Identifies restrictions
- Movement quality
