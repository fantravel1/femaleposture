# Tree Pose: Standing Balance

> **At a Glance:** A classic balance pose that opens the hips and focuses the mind.

---

## Quick Facts

| | |
|---|---|
| **Targets** | Balance, hip openers, ankles |
| **Best For** | Balance, focus |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner |
| **Equipment** | None |
| **Time** | 1-2 minutes per side |
| **Frequency** | Daily |

---

## How to Perform

1. Stand tall on one leg
2. Place other foot on inner thigh or calf (avoid knee)
3. Bring hands to heart or overhead
4. Fix gaze on stationary point
5. Hold 30-60 seconds
6. Switch sides

---

## Progressions

- Foot on calf (easier)
- Foot on inner thigh (harder)
- Eyes closed (most challenging)
