About FemalePosture.com

Evidence-based posture guidance for women, by women who understand the unique challenges of female bodies.

Our Mission

Most posture advice ignores that women's bodies are different. Standard ergonomic guidelines are based on average male dimensions. Pregnancy, postpartum, menopause—these major life events that affect half the population are often treated as afterthoughts.

FemalePosture.com exists to fill that gap with practical, evidence-based guidance that addresses women's real experiences.

What Makes Us Different

Women-Focused Approach

Every article is written with women's bodies in mind. We address:

  • Anatomical differences that affect posture
  • Pregnancy and postpartum changes
  • Menopause and bone health
  • Breastfeeding and baby carrying
  • Ergonomics for typically smaller frames
  • Bra fitting and breast support considerations

Life Stage Awareness

Your posture needs change throughout life. We provide guidance for:

  • Teen years (developing bodies, backpacks, phones)
  • Young adults (desk careers, early prevention)
  • Pregnancy (trimester-by-trimester guidance)
  • Postpartum (recovery and new challenges)
  • Perimenopause and menopause (bone and joint changes)
  • Aging well (maintaining independence)

Practical and Actionable

We don't just explain problems—we provide solutions. Every condition page includes exercises, every activity page includes practical tips you can use today.

Evidence-Based

Our content is based on current research and best practices. We cite sources, acknowledge uncertainty, and update content as science evolves.

What We Believe

  • Posture is not about perfection. "Perfect" static posture isn't the goal. Movement, variety, and awareness matter more.
  • Small consistent actions beat occasional big efforts. Daily habits create lasting change.
  • Movement is medicine. The best posture is your next posture. Keep moving.
  • Information should be accessible. Clear, practical guidance—not jargon and fear.
  • Professional help is valuable. We provide information, not diagnosis. See a provider when you need one.

Medical Disclaimer

The content on FemalePosture.com is for informational and educational purposes only. It is not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read on this website.

If you think you may have a medical emergency, call your doctor or emergency services immediately.
